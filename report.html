April 9, 2022

We had a great time on the slopes this morning! Despite scattered showers this afternoon, we had over 100 fantastic participants at the Pond-A-Palooza. Grab your Gore-Tex after lunch today to take soft turns on South Ridge and North Peak trails, since tomorrow is the last day lifts servicing this terrain will be spinning for the season. Then, head to the Foggy Goggle for delicious food and brews while enjoying live music from our Spring Concert Series. The system is moving out of town overnight, to make room for warm temperatures and dry skies. Tonight, groomers are putting a fresh corduroy base across our favorite trails for you to ski in the morning. Be sure to check the Spring Schedule for updates on the best ways to get in your runs. Look forward to calm weather, more fun events tomorrow, and over 300 acres of open terrain at 8AM first chair!

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! All uphill access is closed for terrain from Aurora Peak, Oz, Jordan Bowl, and trails serviced by Little White Cap Quad. Beginner terrain is exclusively located within South Ridge and North Peak. We've had an outstanding season skiing across all 8 peaks, and can't wait to continue next 2022-23 season!

Please note Road Runner access to White Cap Basin will be open until Monday, April 11. Ski-in/ski-out access for the Grand Summit Hotel requires navigation of intermediate and advanced terrain. For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

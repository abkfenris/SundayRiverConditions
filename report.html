January 15, 2022

Good afternoon! Please note, due to weather, we've made the decision to close at 4PM today, canceling twilight skiing and Après Aglow. If you purchased twilight-specific tickets or reserved a time for Après Aglow, you will be issued a refund. No action is required by you. Hope you've all been staying warm! We've opened up the Chondola and White Cap Quad, but at this time, many of our lifts are still on wind hold. There's still a few more hours to get your runs in, though! I salute all you hardcore skiers and riders out there today. Stay warm! Coming up this week, it looks like Mother Nature is going to bless us with a dumping of snow Monday! Keep those snow dances coming. We deserve a powder day after all these cold temps. 

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails.

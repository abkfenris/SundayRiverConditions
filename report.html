Morning Report
Last Updated: April 13, 2024 | 6:30 AM

The weekend has arrived, and we are excited to have terrain available from White Heat to Jordan 8. Six lifts will run today, with plenty of trails across the resort freshly groomed and ready for you. 

The weather will remain in the mid to high 40s throughout the day, allowing snow conditions to soften as the day progresses. As we move into the season's final days, please note that the Jordan Hotel is still ski-in/ski-out, but the Grand Summit is just ski-in. For additional operational details, please visit the Spring Schedule. 

Along with the soft trails and warm weather, we have lots going on this weekend! Pond-A-Palooza is today, starting at 11 AM in South Ridge. Registration is first come, first served, so if you want to make a splash (so to speak), get there early to secure your spot. And remember, tomorrow, 4/14, we've got the Great Tailgate in Barker and our annual Passholder Appreciation Lunch in South Ridge.

We'll see you out there!

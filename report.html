THURSDAY, JANUARY 14

Happy Thursday! The long weekend has almost arrived, but before it does hit the slopes today for some mid-week bliss. Lifts start spinning at 9AM. Snow conditions have been incredible with amazing corduroy blanketing all eight peaks. Yesterday ropes dropped on White Heat for the first time this season with fun snowmaking whales. Today, keep your eyes on Lost Princess as ropes could potentially drop there today. As we head into the weekend terrain expansion has been the name of the game. Snowmaking has continued on trails like Vortex, Wildfire, and Salvation, and it also started up on Top Gun and South Paw last night. To put the cherry on top, it looks like Mother Nature could drop 5-8" of new snow on Saturday, but keep your fingers crossed we avoid any mixed precipitation. 

Join us on Martin Luther King Jr. Weekend for twilight skiing Friday and Saturday, an Indie Demo Day on Saturday, and fireworks with a fire dancer Saturday night.

Lodging is still available at both of our slopeside hotels and the budget-friendly Snow Cap Inn. As an added bonus, all lodging guests are guaranteed lift ticket access, even when we are sold out like we are this weekend. Please note that we are experiencing high call volume in our call center and guest services, so we recommending using our online booking engine for lodging quotes and booking, or our lift ticket store for all ticket and pass purchases. 

All three base areas lodges are open with parking and basic skier services, and South Ridge Lodge also offers season pass pickup, shopping, tuning, rentals, and SnowSports school. There are several dining options available around the resort, but please keep in mind that capacity is limited and check weekly schedules on our website or our app.

Need to pick up your lift tickets? Fast and contactless ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) throughout the resort. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember that we are requiring online lift ticket purchase or reloading this season and setting limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes day, half-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to come into play on peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Lastly, please remember to check our COVID Winter Guide before each visit to make a plan for your day and stay on top of any potential changes. We’re in this together and need your help.

Carve on!

Happy New Year, Sunday River!

What a beautiful and crisp morning to start the year off strong. We're diving into 2024 with 168 acres of terrain off eight lifts, including the Chondola this morning. Like yesterday afternoon, the Chondola will spin with cabins only today. 

In the latest of exciting updates, our grooming team put their expertise to work, preparing fresh corduroy for those eagerly anticipated first runs of the year. Meanwhile, our snowmakers covered various sections of the resort, focusing on expanding our terrain in the coming days.

Today, Eddy and Betty will join in on the New Year's fun and say hi to friends in South Ridge.

This New Year, why not kickstart your journey by embracing something new? Whether you're a beginner or aiming to enhance your skills, consider diving into a snowsports lesson. It's the perfect way to start the year, learning, improving, and falling in love with the sport all over again.

Here's to a year filled with adventures, progress, and the joy of being out on the mountain.

Think cold, and Happy New Year!

- Team Snow

Next Steps

Thanks to the amazing efforts of our snowmakers, we have tentative plans to open the Jordan Bowl on Wednesday, January 3. This will include ski-in, ski-out access to the Jordan Hotel. The Alpeniglu is scheduled to re-open on Friday, January 5, as well as the Outpost, for convenient slopeside snacks and beverages. Snowmaking temps will be crucial, but the forecast looks promising, and our snowmakers are working hard.

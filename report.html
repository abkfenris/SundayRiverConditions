Good morning Sunday River!

Our first Saturday of December brings some exciting new trail openings to the mountain. Over 60 new acres of groomed terrain will be open, with access from the South Ridge base area starting at 8AM this morning. Our grooming team was hard at work last night laying down some fresh corduroy, so be sure to check it out!

Please take note, temperatures are fluctuating around freezing across the slopes, which could lead to firm conditions to start the day. Rest assured, temps are expected to rise throughout the morning, which will lead to delightful soft turns by the time you finish coffee.

Demo Day will also be happening Slopeside at the South Ridge base area today. Registration begins at 8AM, so stop on by to try out new gear from brands like Atomic, Stockli, Never Summer, and more.

In need of new gear or Christmas presents? Stop by the Sunday River Sports flagship store for all your new equipment needs. Check out our Tune Shop to get your gear ready for the slopes, and the South Ridge Food Court and the Foggy Goggle are both open for all those mid-day snack cravings, full hours at the River Guide.

Lastly, mark December 9th on your calendars! The Barker 6 is finally here, and it’s ready to spin for its Grand Opening. Check out the Barker 6 Grand Opening page for more info.

As always, keep an eye here for the latest info on our lift and trail status, as well as any other operational updates.

Think cold!

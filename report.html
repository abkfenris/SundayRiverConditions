January 24, 2022

We are enjoying a full day of sunshine and blue skies on the most open terrain in the East! Carve into 78 groomed trails this afternoon, with lifts spinning until 3:45PM, or catch first turns at 9AM. Tonight groomers are putting down stripes on freshly made snow to have excellent corduroy awaiting you in the morning. 1-3" of flurries are forecasted to greet us for first chair tomorrow, with a larger system inbound Saturday. This week we have exciting activities available for Senior Week including the Après Aglow lighted walk, a comedy show, karaoke and more. Come ski nearly 700 acres of options!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl.

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: January 14, 2024 | 1:26 PM

Today we had partly cloudy skies, temps in the low thirties, and the season debut of the Merrill Hill Triple. The softer snow of yesterday afternoon firmed up overnight, making for some excellent corduroy this morning. The trails are still skiing well, and there might be some snow showers this afternoon. Sunday blues, who?

Our snowmaking team will be resurfacing some open terrain in addition to working on new terrain. We hope to open more terrain on Merrill Hill in the next week or so, and we're anticipating the opening of the T72 terrain park by next weekend. Our grooming team will be heading out there tonight, and we can expect stripes on over half of our open terrain. With the forecasted temperatures and the potential for a dusting of snow, the turns tomorrow should be delightful. Who's up for first chair?

There will not be twilight skiing tonight. Our next evening for twilight skiing will be next Friday, January 19. Shipyard will have live music tonight, with Chase Job playing from 2-5PM. Check out our full schedule for live music on our Après and Nightlife page. Looking for a fun activity for next weekend? The Alpeniglu at Sunday River has a new après experience, as well; on Saturdays, take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some drinks, sweets, and music, then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now.

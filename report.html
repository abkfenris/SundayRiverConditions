Mountain Report
Last Updated: September 27, 2023

Come join us at Artisan Fest for a weekend of food, art, and fun! Watch skilled artists demonstrate pottery and blacksmithing, or take a kaleidoscope-making class that you can only find here at Sunday River. 

Don't miss out on the tasting tent where you can sample wines, spirits, and cheeses from local Maine businesses. And if you love trying different kinds of beer, we've got a Maine Beer Box with 78 taps of Maine brews!

As an added bonus, we will be hosting an antique car cruise-in on Saturday and Sunday. Check out the Artisan Fest page  for a full list of events and activities for the weekend! 

As we transition into fall, scenic lift rides will now run on Saturdays and Sundays, from 10AM - 4PM, with 3:30PM being the last time to purchase a ticket for the day. 

Lastly, don't forget to share your summer joy with us on Happy Place Found – we love seeing your adventures!

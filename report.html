January 2, 2022

We are starting 2022 with a forecast featuring 1-3" of fresh snow! This early afternoon and into tonight, we are looking forward to flurries falling on 8 peaks. Today we have 77 open trails across 423 acres for you to shred all day. Groomers put down stripes last night and into this morning, so you can carve into fresh corduroy. Lifts are spinning from 8AM-3:45PM, so get in as many turns as you want today. An abundance of options awaits you, on the most open terrain in the East!

Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out for your convenience. To ski into the Grand Summit Hotel via green terrain, take Little White Cap Quad and ski down Moonstruck via Green Cheese. To access the Jordan Hotel via green terrain, please take the Jordan Mountain Double from Aurora Basin, to ride the Jordan Bowl Express, and ski Lollapalooza. At this time, all other green trails into the Jordan Hotel are temporarily considered advanced terrain.

Around the Resort

Select services, such as lessons and rental equipment, may be limited or sold out due to the holiday vacation week. Online reservations made in advance are recommended to guarantee availability.

Many restaurants and food trucks are open for the season, including Coffee Hound in South Ridge Lodge Food Court, the Ona-Tripp Food Truck, and more. For shopping and relaxation needs, head over to Aster & Birch located within the Jordan Hotel, as well as Sunday River Sports, Betty's Treats and Treasures, or unwind at the Jordan Spa. Please note: Sliders will be closed this week with a planned reopening on January 7, but Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel is open until 8PM daily. 

For the latest updates whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

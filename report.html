December 8, 2021

Good afternoon Sunday River! The peaks sure do look good in white. Flakes have been falling steadily since this morning, so keep those snow dances going. Temps stayed favorable today, allowing our snowmakers to continue their expansion efforts resort wide. The grooming team will hit the slopes this evening to mix in today's fresh snow, meaning tomorrow is going to be full of carvable cord. Lifts open at 9AM tomorrow,  so pack up the car and join us for some midweek turns.

All lift tickets need to be purchased online in advance. Have your RFID from last year? You can head straight to the lift! Skier services are available in both the Barker and South Ridge Lodges, as well as shuttle service between these lodges and the hotels while the lifts spin. We are now accepting cashless forms of payment only. Please be sure to have a gift card, resort charge, credit card, or debit card available for on-site purchases.

Around the Resort

Join us this weekend at the Feztival of Trees for some holiday cheer! Come on down to the Jordan Hotel to see dozens of decorated trees available as prizes - purchase raffle tickets to earn your chance of bringing one home! More info can be found here. 

The South Ridge Food Court is now open, along with the Barker Food Court, Waffle Cabin in Barker Basin, Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel, Camp at the Grand Summit Hotel, and Shipyard Brew Haus at White Cap Lodge. Foggy Goggle is now open for the season! Plus, get your retail therapy in at Sunday River Sports, and your relaxation on at the Jordan Spa. Please note that the Tune Shop is closed at the moment, but you can pick up skis at the repair counter in Sunday River Sports.

For the latest updates whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

COVID-19 Protocols

Though we are no longer requiring guests to wear masks in general areas, we suggest masking indoors. However, some services may require you to wear a mask, so be sure to have one on hand. Please make sure to read through our COVID Winter Guide before visiting the resort to understand our operating plans and how they may affect you. Remember, we're all in this together.

See you on the slopes!

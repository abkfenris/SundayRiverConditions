December 28, 2021

    The snow has been super fun today, folks. With almost 400 acres of open terrain, there's lots of room to explore! Most lifts will close this afternoon at 3:45, but have no fear: Twilight Skiing is on at the Chondola and South Ridge Express! Both lifts will spin until 6:30PM tonight. Twilight skiing will continue throughout Christmas Vacation Week. Tonight, snowmakers will get to work on White Heat, Tempest, Assumption, and Starlight, and the groomers will lay down perfect corduroy for carving. Looking for something to do tonight? Check out the Fire Dancer shows at South Ridge, starting at 6:15PM and 6:45PM. And stick around for the fireworks show at 7PM! Additionally, a winter wonderland illuminated by thousands of twinkling lights awaits you at the Après Aglow Walk open every night this week, and conveniently located behind the Grand Summit Hotel. Tickets for both activities can be purchased online throughout the week. Come join a ski-filled week of festivities slopeside!

    Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out accessible for your convenience. To access the Grand Summit Hotel via green terrain, take Little White Cap Quad and ski down Moonstruck. To access the Jordan Hotel via green terrain, please ski Sensation in order to take the Jordan Mountain Double and then ride Jordan Bowl Express, to ski Lollapalooza. At this time, all other green trails into the Jordan Hotel are temporarily considered advanced terrain. 

      Around the Resort

      Please note: Sliders will be closed this week with a planned reopening on January 7. Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel is open until 8PM tonight.

      Select services, such as lessons and rental equipment, may be limited or sold out from now through New Year's Day. Online reservations made in advance are recommended to guarantee availability.

      Betty's Treats and Treasures is now open! Based on the Betty the Yeti book, and located next to South Ridge Lodge, this shop brings our beloved mascot's dream to life. Grab a photo in the picture-perfect purple gondola Cabin outside before entering the store filled with candy, sweets, treats, and unique Sunday River gift items!

      Many restaurants and food trucks recently opened for the season, including Coffee Hound in South Ridge Lodge Food Court, and in the Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel. For retail needs, head over to recently opened Aster & Birch located within the Jordan Hotel, as well as Sunday River Sports, Betty's Treats and Treasures, or unwind at the Jordan Spa.

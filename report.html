January 14, 2022

Happy Friday Sunday River skiers and riders! The snowmakers took advantage of the cold temps to refresh trails like Jibe and Sunday Punch, as well as burying Shockwave and Top Gun with eyes set on expansion. Snowmaking will be coming offline tonight and tomorrow but will be back in time to help Mother Nature blanket the mountain on Monday. Vortex received a top-to-bottom makeover from the groomers, who pressed perfect corduroy across all eight peaks. USASA is hosting a slopestyle event on T72 today, so the trail will be closed to the public. Speaking of events, we’ve got a fantastic weekend on the way jam-packed with activities that everyone will enjoy, so keep snow-dancing, and we’ll see you out there.

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails. 

Around the Resort

It's Kids' Week here at Sunday River, with deals on lodging, lift tickets, and lessons. We're gearing up for a super fun Martin Luther King, Jr. Weekend on the mountain, with tap takeovers, fireworks, twilight skiing, and more. Just make sure you bundle up! It's going to be a chilly weekend on the mountain, so please dress appropriately if you will be skiing with us. Need to get away? One-night stays just opened up for this Saturday night at the Snow Cap Inn and Jordan Hotel.

Looking ahead, Wicked Air & Après is returning to Barker on January 22. This must-see event combines big air and 3D project lights into one show. 

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

Summer Savings End Soon
Last Updated: August 29, 2023

Would you believe that we're only two days away from September? We don't know about you, but we're ready for some snow. We're officially 1 week away from the next New England Pass price increase. Prices increase at midnight on September 6, so don't miss out on your chance to lock in access to Sunday River, Sugarloaf, and Loon at our summer rates. While we're at it, Ticket Pack prices also go up on September 6 at midnight. With options from 2-5 days, there's a pack for everyone.

We are gearing up for fall and we are happy to invite you to River Fest - the ultimate weekend series full of fall events, that includes beer, food, music, and activities, to enjoy with family and friends this September and October.  Experience the Maine Brew Fest, Maine Artisan Fest, Mahoosuc Adventure Fest, and Fall Fest—all surrounded by the stunning colors of autumn. 

In the meantime, join us for lunch or dinner at The Clubhouse at the Sunday River Golf Club. Chef Brian Nichols and Chef Chad Davidowicz are serving up some impressive dishes. Whether you're fresh off the 18th hole or just prefer to skip cooking, The Clubhouse welcomes all, serving culinary excellence amid stunning mountain landscapes. 

Lastly, don't forget to share your summer joy with us on Happy Place Found – we love seeing your adventures!

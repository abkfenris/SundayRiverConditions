December 10, 2021

Today we were graced with more fresh powder, resulting in opening even more terrain! Sirius, Aludra, and Lights Out were all opened this afternoon as the flakes fell. Look forward to Jibe, Heat's On, Lower Starlight, and Road Runner opening with the White Cap Quad for ski-in/ski-out access from the Grand Summit Hotel and White Cap Lodge, and Dream Maker will also with advanced terrain only by 8AM. Snowbound and Lower Obsession are scheduled to open shortly after lifts start spinning, and don't miss the hike park on Flow State. Barker Mountain Express, South Ridge Express, Locke Mountain Triple, the Chondola, Spruce Peak Triple, and White Cap Quad will all be spinning from 8AM-3:45PM tomorrow. Looking ahead, we are excited for all this new terrain to shred!

Around the Resort

Join us this weekend at the Feztival of Trees for some holiday cheer! Come on down to the Jordan Hotel to see dozens of decorated trees available as prizes - purchase raffle tickets to earn your chance of bringing one home! 

The South Ridge Food Court is now open, along with the Barker Food Court, Waffle Cabin in Barker Basin, Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel, Camp at the Grand Summit Hotel, Shipyard Brew Haus at White Cap Lodge, and Foggy Goggle at South Ridge Lodge. Plus, get your retail therapy in at Sunday River Sports, and your relaxation on at the Jordan Spa. Please note that White Cap Lodge will not have shopping or guest service offerings this weekend, but will have a ticket pick-up box and ski-in/ski-out access via the White Cap Quad.

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: December 1, 2023 | 1:22 PM

December sure started off on a lovely note. Sunshine and comfortable temperatures dominated the sky today, making for delightful turns across the slopes. Tomorrow is scheduled to bring our next big expansion, so let's get into it.

Our snowmakers are scheduled to take another evening off on the hill and will focus on moving equipment and maintenance as tonight's unsettled weather moves through the area. Out on the hill, the grooming team is slated to put stripes on over 60 acres of new terrain in preparation for the Grand Opening of the South Ridge base area tomorrow at 8AM. The Chondola and the South Ridge Express are scheduled to spin, and the South Ridge Base Lodge will be fully open for all of your on-mountain needs.

Check out the Tune Shop to get your gear in mid-season form, visit the Sunday River Sports flagship store for any equipment or early Christmas presents, and check out the South Ridge Food Court and the Foggy Goggle for your snack needs. For full hours and all the info, check out the River Guide.

In addition to new terrain coming online tomorrow, Demo Day will be Slopeside at South Ridge from 8AM-2PM. Demo the hottest gear from brands like Blizzard, Stockli, Atomic, and more. 

Lastly, be sure to keep December 9th open on your calendar. It's finally here - the Grand Opening of the Barker 6. For more information on the celebration, visit the Barker 6 Grand Opening page.

As always, keep an eye here for the latest info on our lift and trail status, as well as any other operational updates.

Think cold!

Spring Update

Sweet sunshine is in the air, and we are basking in the spring days of early May. As the days grow longer and the sun shines brighter, we have some exciting announcements to share with you.

First and foremost, we are delighted to inform you that the Sunday River Golf Club is now open for the season! Dust off those clubs, polish your swing and get ready to tee off amidst breathtaking mountain views. To celebrate the opening week, we're taking 20% off Monday-Thursday Tee Times through June 8. Hurry! This special opening week offer ends May 31.

In addition to the golf course, we have another fantastic event to mark on your calendars. The highly anticipated Maine Brew Fest is just around the corner, and we're thrilled to announce that tickets are now on sale! Get ready to indulge in a celebration of craft beers from all across Maine. This is the perfect opportunity to savor unique flavors, discover new favorites, and meet fellow beer enthusiasts. The Maine Brew Fest promises to be a memorable experience, filled with live music, delicious food, and of course, an incredible selection of beers. Make sure to secure your tickets early, as they tend to sell out quickly, and don't miss out on the special VIP access.

We look forward to seeing you soon at Sunday River for a summer full of adventure and enjoyment!

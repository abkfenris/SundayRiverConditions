FRIDAY, APRIL 2

We woke up this morning to a beautiful sunrise and snowguns blasting Right Stuff and Sunday Punch. "In April?!", you ask. Why, yes indeed, and our snowmaking team is hitting the slopes tonight to do it all over again. Our commitment to snow runs so deep, we start with a test in September, build a base in the fall, and keep it up season long even into April. Technically, that's four seasons of running snowguns. Some might say we are obsessed, but that's okay because we're pretty sure you are too.

Lifts are scheduled to open at 8AM tomorrow. The groomers will lay stripes on several trails across each peak for corduroy cruising, so hop on first chair and enjoy a full day of carving in the sunshine. With some awesome weather for this weekend, make your plans with one-night stays available at the Grand Summit and Jordan Hotels through the rest of the season. We have limited skier services at the White Cap base lodge, so head to South Ridge or Barker to start your day.

With operations open daily through April 24, there is plenty of ski season ahead. View the entire Spring Schedule to learn more about peak access and resort services this spring.

Don't miss out on our on-mountain savings at our retail shops around the resort, including up to 40% off at our Sunday River Sports Flagship shop at South Ridge and 30% off at the Jordan Spa Boutique.

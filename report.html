Morning Report

Another beautiful day is ahead of us here at Sunday River. Our team is out preparing to open 13 lifts at 9 AM this morning. Cold temps overnight allowed our grooming team to put down some perfect corduroy stripes, and conditions will be smooth, flowy, and firm this morning. By the afternoon, snow will surely soften up amazingly as the sun comes out for the day. Natural ungroomed terrain still has plenty of snow coverage, but are pretty skied off and bumped up at this point. Give those trails some time to get a little sun and warmth. By mid-day, those moguls should be softer and more enjoyable.

Overall the forecast is predicting another gorgeous week in western Maine. For today, we are seeing temperatures reaching the 40s by mid-afternoon, low winds, and partially cloudy skies. This Friday and Saturday mark the final weekend of Twilight Skiing, so if you want some after-hours turns, this is your last chance. Our first Spring Apres Concert of the season is this Saturday as well. 

It's going to be an amazing spring, so why not ski or ride for free? All 2023-24 Season Passes are now valid for use throughout the rest of the season. Buy yours, and start using it immediately. No blackouts, no extra charges, just more skiing.

See you out there!
-Team Snow

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 17, 2024 | 1:29 PM

Over 100 acres of mostly natural terrain were opened throughout the morning, bringing us into the afternoon with 710 acres. All 19 lifts spun today, and the Chondola and the South Ridge Express will spin until 6:30PM for some twilight skiing. The flurries earlier were the icing on the cake. 

Our snowmaking team will be off the hill the next few nights, and they'll resume snowmaking sometime Monday or Tuesday. Our grooming team will be giving us over 80 trails with corduroy. White Heat will be the trail of the morning tomorrow with fresh stripes, so be sure to head that way for first chair. Wildfire also joins the lineup of groomed terrain, and Upper Vortex will get a little love, as well. 

Presidents' Week is here, and we have a whole host of events. Red Bull Winter Vibes will go from 12-7PM in South Ridge today, so swing by to listen to a live DJ, try Red Bull-infused cocktails, and check out the photo booth. Can't make it today? No worries, because the party won't stop until Sunday. 7PM will bring some slopeside fireworks. For some more live music tonight, party with Skosh at the Foggy Goggle from 3-6PM, or listen to the Dapper Gents Duo from 2-5PM at the Barker Bar. Stop by the Jordan Spa for Sips & Sweets today from 4-5:30PM today, and enjoy some prosecco and a sweet treat while you mingle. Still deciding on your vacation plans? We have single-night stays at all resort properties this weekend through Presidents' Week, so be sure to check that out.  

We'll see you out there!

- Team Snow

January 16, 2022

We don't want to wish away the sunshine and blue skies that we've been enjoying today, but when there's a foot of snow in the forecast, you can't blame us for looking ahead to tomorrow. Our grooming team is prepping for the pow day by laying down stripes on over 70 trails, including South Paw, overnight. We're expecting snow to begin falling around sunrise and with steady flakes throughout the day for nonstop refills. Don't forget, we open at 8AM tomorrow for the holiday, so set your alarm now to make sure you're here for first chair.

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl. 

Around the Resort

Don't miss 100,000 lights in the forest tonight at Après Aglow. Tickets are available for this half-mile trail at the Grand Summit Hotel from 4:30-8:30PM. 

We've got some fun events coming up! Wicked Air & Après is returning to Barker on January 22. This must-see event combines big air and 3D project lights into one show. 

Senior Week is January 24-28 - lots of deals for the over-65 club, and plenty of fun events and ski meet-ups to join. 

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

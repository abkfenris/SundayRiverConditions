December 20, 2021

    The sun is shining on over 300 acres of open terrain and almost 50 groomed trails for you to carve today! The recent system brought us 6-10" of fresh powder, and we are looking forward to a beautiful day on the mountain. Temperatures are ideal for blasting guns across the resort, and Mother Nature may even make another appearance next weekend to ring in the holidays. What better way to count down to Christmas, than with a lot of open terrain!

    Since Jordan Bowl is open, both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out access for your convenience. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. With this in mind, skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently accessible via blue terrain. Rental snowboards are now back in stock. Have a great day skiing the snowy slopes!

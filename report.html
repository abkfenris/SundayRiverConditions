April 23, 2022

It's the final countdown! Welcome to closing weekend here at Sunday River. After another incredible season, thanks to our Mountain Ops team, we're celebrating the final send-off today and tomorrow. The Barker Mountain Express opens at 8AM to service Barker peak. Please note terrain is considered advanced and be aware of all signage while out on the slopes.

If you don't get enough sunshine and soft turns today, join us tomorrow, Sunday, April 24, for the last day of the season. Be sure to pick up your $0 ticket for the last day.

All skier services, including ticket purchases, dining options, and ski shop essentials, are at Barker base lodge. Guest Services is open in the Welcome Center at South Ridge, if you need assistance. Please note uphill access is prohibited on all trails marked as closed.

Only one week remains to lock in $0 down at the lowest rates on next year's New England Pass. If you are an existing passholder, this is your last chance to take advantage of the renewal discount before it ends on April 30. 

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23.

Tips up!

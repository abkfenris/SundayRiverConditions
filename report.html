January 31, 2022

The only blues we are feeling this Monday, is a classic bluebird! We have nearly 800 acres of open terrain for you to enjoy this afternoon. Lifts are spinning until 3:45PM for you to carve the day away on 80 groomed trails, or enjoy the 12-14" of new snow stashes across all 8 peaks!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl.

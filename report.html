January 15, 2022

Good morning Sunday River skiers and riders! Happy Saturday. The weather is putting up a fight today. High winds will impact our ability to open all of our lifts; currently, the Jordan Bowl Express, Quantum Leap Triple, White Cap Quad, White Heat Quad, Chondola, Spruce Peak Triple, Aurora Peak Quad, Barker Mountain Express, and Locke Mountain Triple will start the day on wind hold. Check back later on for more updated information. Temps are also low and will stay below zero all day. If you're joining us on the mountain, please wear lots of warm layers and don't be afraid to take breaks in our lodges! Grab a coffee in the morning at Coffee Hound or Northern Lights at the Jordan, or dig in a with a full breakfast at Camp to help fuel you up for the day. The grooming team laid down stripes on all eight peaks last night, so if you're after the corduroy, it's certainly out there. All eyes are on a Nor'easter for Monday, so keep the snow dances going. 

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails. 

Around the Resort

It's Martin Luther King, Jr. Weekend and we've got lots of fun going on around the mountain - tap takeovers, fireworks, twilight skiing, and more. Just make sure you bundle up! It's going to be a chilly weekend, so please dress appropriately if you will be visiting us. Need to crash here tonight? One-night stays just opened up for this Saturday night at the Snow Cap Inn and Jordan Hotel.

Looking ahead, Wicked Air & Après is returning to Barker on January 22. This must-see event combines big air and 3D project lights into one show. 

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

Stay warm!

-Team Snow

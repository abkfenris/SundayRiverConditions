Afternoon Report
Last Updated: January 10, 2024 | 1:26 PM

Today was a wild one. It began with 15" of snow, transitioned into rain, and now we've got blue skies and wind gusts. The natural snow we got allowed us to open ten new trails, including Locke Line, Wizard's Gulch, Airglow, and Lower Caramba. Wind holds limited access to some of our new terrain, but the winds are currently forecasted to ease up by tonight and into tomorrow. Who knows- we might get a few flurries tomorrow, and there's even more snow coming on Friday.  

Our snowmakers will have a maintenance night tonight, and they'll be out and around the mountain, positioning snow guns on new trails. Our grooming team will hit a similar list of terrain tonight, with today's new terrain staying natural. Tempest will likely stay whalebacked through tomorrow. 

Dash Deals are already winding down. They disappear at midnight tomorrow, January 11, so check them out before then for up to 20% off of lodging and up to 50% off of lift tickets. Don't forget to check out our event lineup for Martin Luther King Jr. Weekend, as well as all of our live music for next weekend. We have après options for everyone, including at our Alpeniglu. Take a cabin snowcat down from Sliders, and enjoy some drinks, sweets, and music before taking the cabin cat back up. Additionally, we're currently offering one-night stays in all Sunday River lodging for this weekend, so be sure to snag that deal before it goes.

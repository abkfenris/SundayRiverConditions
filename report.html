December 17, 2021

The countdown to snow has begun! In anticipation of the incoming flurries, we have a bit of a breeze in the air this morning. Please make sure to check the lift and trail status throughout the day as conditions improve. Starting off Winterfest Weekend with snow is exactly what we have in mind, accompanied by great events for family and friends. Jordan Bowl opens this weekend as well, allowing both hotels to be ski-in/ski-out for your convenience. Lifts are scheduled to spin from 9AM-3:45PM today. Come ski in a winter wonderland tomorrow night, at the opening of twilight skiing, with tickets just $10 for all ages!

Please note that our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. With this in mind, skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently accessible via blue terrain. Although the White Cap Lodge and Grand Summit Hotel are now ski-in/ski-out, all skier services including shopping and guest service are temporarily concentrated to Barker and South Ridge Lodges. 

Around the Resort

Join us at Winterfest Weekend for some holiday cheer! Come on down to see fireworks, live music, and more. Twilight skiing will be opening this weekend as well, with $10 tickets for all ages.

The South Ridge Food Court is now open, along with the Barker Food Court, Waffle Cabin in Barker Basin, Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel, Camp at the Grand Summit Hotel, Shipyard Brew Haus at White Cap Lodge, and Foggy Goggle at South Ridge Lodge. Plus, get your retail therapy in at Sunday River Sports, and your relaxation on at the Jordan Spa. Please note that White Cap Lodge will not have shopping or guest service offerings this weekend, but will have a ticket pick-up box and ski-in/ski-out access via the White Cap Quad. 

For the latest updates whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

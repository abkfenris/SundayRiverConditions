No sign of the Monday blues on the slopes! Stunning conditions have us skiing every chance we get on 134 glades and trails. After a calm morning of carving, we are shredding snow stashes. An adventure awaits within almost 2,000 acres, including our boundary-to-boundary skiing options. With all of our open terrain, you can take turns among the trees, ski powdery trails, and carve fresh corduroy all day. Tonight groomers are putting stripes on nearly 90 trails for you to enjoy. Catch first chair flurries at 9AM tomorrow morning!

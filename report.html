December 31, 2021

It has been a beautiful morning with temperatures ideal for a full day of skiing, continuing into tonight. Lifts are spinning until 8:00PM for Twilight Skiing so you can ring in 2022 with as many runs as you desire. We have 76 open trails and 422 acres of terrain for you to enjoy. More corduroy is being put across 8 peaks tonight, for ample carving in the New Year. Celebrate slopeside with festivities including fireworks, live music, fire dancing and more! Our newest activity Après Aglow is open until 8:30PM tonight, featuring thousands of twinkling lights on an enchanting walk through the forest outside of the Grand Summit Hotel. The skiing options are endless, on the most open terrain in the East!

Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out for your convenience. To ski into the Grand Summit Hotel via green terrain, take Little White Cap Quad and ski down Moonstruck via Green Cheese. To access the Jordan Hotel via green terrain, please take the Jordan Bowl Express, and ski Lollapalooza. At this time, all other green trails into the Jordan Hotel are temporarily considered advanced terrain.

Around the Resort

The Foggy Goggle is open from 9:30AM-12:30AM tonight for a New Year's Eve Party for ages 21+ featuring live music by CC & Friends. Passholders get in for free tonight, all other customers please have a $15 cashless payment option ready upon entry.

Select services, such as lessons and rental equipment, may be limited or sold out from now through New Year's Day. Online reservations made in advance are recommended to guarantee availability.

Many restaurants and food trucks are open for the season, including Coffee Hound in South Ridge Lodge Food Court, the Ona-Tripp Food Truck, and more. For shopping and relaxation needs, head over to recently opened Aster & Birch located within the Jordan Hotel, as well as Sunday River Sports, Betty's Treats and Treasures, or unwind at the Jordan Spa. Please note: Sliders will be closed this week with a planned reopening on January 7, but Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel is open until 8PM daily. 

For the latest updates whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

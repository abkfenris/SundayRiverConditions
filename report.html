Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 22, 2024 | 3:15 PM

It's been a beautiful day out on the hill, with warm comfortable temps across all eight peaks. 

For tonight, the snowmaking team will be taking a break as warm temps stay in the area. Grooming will have a busy night, as they tackle all normal groomed terrain, as well as grooming in the fresh snow on White Heat and Excalibur. 

Following a mechanical issue, the Aurora Quad will be on hold until further notice. Trails into the Aurora Basin will remain open and will be serviced by the Quantum Leap Triple, and the Jordan Mountain Double. Please be advised that limited lift access will be available out of Aurora. 

It's never a bad time to review the Skier Responsibility Code. Take a moment, refresh yourself before heading out, and please remember to go slow in the base areas.

Presidents' Week is continuing, and our events schedule remains jam-packed. Tonight, we have laser tag at the Grand Summit Hotel from 5-8PM, for ages 6+. Get your tickets here! We have twilight skiing tonight as well, so stick around and ski under the lights until 6:30PM. After that, head over to the Grand Summit Hotel to check out the Après Aglow Lighted Trail from 4:30-8:30PM. We also have live music today at the Foggy Goggle, so swing by to see James Rice play from 3-6PM.  Still deciding what to do this vacation week? We have single-night stays at all resort properties through the rest of this week, so be sure to check that out.

We'll see you out there!

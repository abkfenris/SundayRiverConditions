April 15, 2022

What a stunning spring day on the slopes! Sunshine and blue skies light up nearly 200 acres of open terrain. Warm temperatures are staying so you can ski until last chair today. Forecasts are calling for mixed weather tomorrow, followed by dry skies on Sunday. Groomers are adding a great base of fresh corduroy to some of our favorite trails so you can get in those runs. Grab your Gore-Tex and catch first chair at 8AM tomorrow for soft turns and fun festivities. Please check out our Spring Schedule for weekly updates on the best ways to enjoy the trails. Look forward to exciting events celebrating a great season, including The Great Tailgate tomorrow!

Please note uphill access is closed for South Ridge, North Peak, Aurora Peak, Oz, Jordan Bowl, and terrain serviced by Little White Cap Quad. All terrain is intermediate and advanced, including ski-in/ski-out access to the Grand Summit Hotel. Access to White Cap Basin via Road Runner is closed. We look forward to another great season skiing across all 8 peaks!

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

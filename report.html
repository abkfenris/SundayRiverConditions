Afternoon Update

Sunday brought blue skies and sun to Sunday River. It was cold enough to keep the snow guns on throughout the day, and trails remained firm enough not to become skied off and filled with moguls. Temps were steady around 30 all day and will dip below 20 overnight. Our team will get out there to turn on the snow guns all over the resort. The snow cats will hit the slopes to groom as many trails as they can get to if not all of them.

It looks like another week of colder temps is ahead, perfect for making more snow to grow our open terrain and bring the best skiing we can to western Maine. Keep an eye on the mountain report for lift and trail status changes as we push to open more trails soon.

Tomorrow is the start of Kid's week here at Sunday River. Make sure to check out all the deals and events we have planned for your little ones to enjoy.

Please note: There is currently no beginner terrain into and out of the Grand Summit Hotel.

Visiting Sunday River anytime soon? We are offering one-night stays at the Snow Cap Inn and Grand Summit hotel this weekend. Also, be sure to take a look at our dash deals sale. Here you will find the best deals on lodging and the return of our Threedom Pack. This sale ends January 9th.

See you on the slopes!
-Team Snow

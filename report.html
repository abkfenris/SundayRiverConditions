November 29, 2021

Blue skies all day made skiing on our newly opened terrain even brighter. A beautiful spread of snowmaking last night on Broadway, Lower Lazy River, Lower Mixing Bowl, and Easy Street made for a great morning on South Ridge. Beginner terrain is available starting tomorrow, with grooming on Broadway tonight. We expect the Barker Mountain Express to remain closed tomorrow, with hiking access from Upper Sunday Punch across Jungle Road. (It’s well worth the walk—trust us!). Locke Mountain Triple and South Ridge Express are scheduled to spin tomorrow from 9AM-3:45PM.  

Weather reports are looking sweet for snowmaking, allowing us to blast the guns on Race Arena, Monday Morning, American Express, Upper Risky Business, Sirius, Aludra, Lights Out, Escapade, Sundance, Lower Grand Rapids, Lower Downdraft, and Southway trails overnight. With so much snow, we’re expecting terrain on Spruce Peak by the weekend.

All lift tickets need to be purchased online in advance. Have your RFID from last year? You can head straight to the lift! All skier services will be based out of Barker Lodge. Season Pass Office is also open at South Ridge Lodge and we will have shuttle service between Barker, South Ridge, and the hotels while the lifts spin. We are now accepting cashless forms of payment only. Please be sure to have a gift card, resort charge, credit card, or debit card available for on-site purchases. 

COVID-19 Protocols

Though we are no longer requiring guests to wear masks in general areas, we suggest masking indoors. However, some services may require you to wear a mask, so be sure to have one on hand. Please make sure to read through our COVID Winter Guide before visiting the resort to understand our operating plans and how they may affect you. Remember, we're all in this together.

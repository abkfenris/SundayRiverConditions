Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 4, 2024 | 1:51 PM

What a day! Bluebird skies, light winds, and conditions perfect for carving. We had a great day, and we hope you did, too. How are those mid-season goggle tans coming?

Our snowmaking team will keep working on Salvation and Shock Wave, and they're also planning on resurfacing Broadway and Lower Lazy River. Our parks will get their standard groom tonight, as well.

We also have a few spots left for our Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River during President's Week. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now.

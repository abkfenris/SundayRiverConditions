SUNDAY, APRIL 4

Rise and shine! It is another gorgeous day on the mountain with sunshine, fresh stripes, and mild temps. Enjoy carving up the groomers this morning, then slash up soft snow once the sun warms things up. After snowmaking on Right Stuff and Lower Sunday Punch, groomers have pushed out the new snow and added stripes for some fun turns today. Quickly find your Easter basket this morning then head to the mountain to soak up this sunny Sunday.

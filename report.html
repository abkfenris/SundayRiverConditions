Morning Report
Last Updated: March 22, 2024 | 7:35 AM

Blue skies and low temps at the mountain today, with single digits up top and low teens down low right now. It should reach up to the high teens and mid-twenties today, and those skies should stay blue. Winds are currently high, and we are expecting wind holds this morning. They should even out by this afternoon, and tomorrow we should have low winds and lots of snow. Keep an eye on the lift and trail status below for the most up-to-date information. Uphill travel is closed today.

Our grooming team laid corduroy on about 70 trails last night. They stayed off of the trails with active snowmaking, and they worked in some of the new snow from the night before. Broadway and Lower Downdraft both got a fresh groom for that new snow. Our snowmaking team was also out last night, and they made snow on Lazy River, Risky Business, Tourist Trap, and Broadway. Broadway will come offline this morning, and Excalibur will come on and run through the day. Please note that these trails will have ungroomed snowmaking product, except for Broadway. 

This is the last weekend for twilight skiing this season, so stick around and ski under the lights. Tomorrow, we have an Eddy & Betty Dinner on Saturday in the South Ridge Base Lodge. DJ NBD will be playing at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River from 10AM until last chair. On Sunday, check out WinterKids Day and the 36th Annual Ski-A-Thon with Maine Adaptive.

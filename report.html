We're wrapping up another beautiful day on the mountain, and looking forward to another sizeable snowstorm arriving tomorrow. The snowmaking team will continue to deliver the goods on favorites like Risky Business before changing focus tomorrow morning. Around the Resort

Presidents' Week is here. All week long is packed with activities, including live music, fireworks, Twilight Skiing, fire dancing, the Après Aglow lighted walk, and more. Get tickets for family and friends so you can look forward to a fun vacation on the slopes.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. You can also treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures with delicious candy, or shop our exclusive logowear collection.

Looking for retail therapy or perhaps forgot some gear on your packing list? Check out our many shopping options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge. Love the boutique retail feel? Enjoy shopping at Aster & Birch at the Jordan Hotel.

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

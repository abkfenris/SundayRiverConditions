Afternoon Report
Last Updated: March 20, 2024 | 1:46 PM

There have been some flurries up at the peaks this afternoon, and hopefully, those are a precursor of what's to come. The snow was fast today with these cooler temps, and they'll continue to drop through this afternoon. With 700 acres, 13 lifts, and 115 trails across all 8 peaks, today was a fun day. We have a few inches of snow incoming tonight, with even more predicted this weekend.

This incoming weather system is bringing snow, but it is also bringing wind. High winds are in the forecast for tomorrow, and we are anticipating some wind holds throughout the day. Keep an eye on the lift and trail status below for the most up-to-date information. 

Our snowmaking team will be back on the hill tonight, and they'll be making snow on trails like Lazy River, American Express, Excalibur, and Downdraft, among others. Please note that those trails will have ungroomed snowmaking product tomorrow.  Our grooming team will be out tonight as well, and they're planning on grooming the same 73 trails as last night. 

Later this month, come check out events like Bust 'N' Burn and Spring Fest Weekend with activities for everyone in the family. Have you made your plans for the Solar Eclipse yet? Check out our Solar Eclipse Viewing Party on April 8th and watch the eclipse from the top of North Peak. See our events calendar for a full list of spring events across the mountain.

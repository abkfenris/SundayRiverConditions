December 14, 2021

Ski with options at Sunday River, we have almost 50 open groomed trails for you to carve! The sun is lighting up the mountain on this beautiful winter morning, and snowmakers have been blasting guns all night to make piles of snow to shred. With a breeze in the air, please check our lift and trail status updates as conditions improve this morning. Looking ahead, groomers continue to put stripes on the slopes, creating a great week and weekend of skiing. Celebrate the holiday season here at Winterfest this weekend, with entertainment ranging from live music to fireworks for family and friends to enjoy. This Saturday, December 18, twilight skiing will be opening with tickets at $10 for all ages!

Jordan Bowl is opening by next weekend, at which point both hotels will feature ski-in/ski-out trails for your convenience. Please note that our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. With this in mind, skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently accessible via blue terrain. Although the White Cap Lodge and Grand Summit Hotel are now ski-in/ski-out, all skier services including shopping and guest service are temporarily concentrated to Barker and South Ridge Lodges. Come carve the most open corduroy terrain in the East!

Around the Resort

Join us at Winterfest Weekend for some holiday cheer! Come on down to see fireworks, live music, and more. Twilight skiing will be opening this weekend as well, with $10 tickets for all ages!

The South Ridge Food Court is now open, along with the Barker Food Court, Waffle Cabin in Barker Basin, Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel, Camp at the Grand Summit Hotel, Shipyard Brew Haus at White Cap Lodge, and Foggy Goggle at South Ridge Lodge. Please note that White Cap Lodge will not have shopping or guest service offerings this weekend, but will have a ticket pick-up box and ski-in/ski-out access via the White Cap Quad.

Morning Report
Last Updated: February 8, 2024 | 7:35 AM

Forecasted temperatures for the day have us at 30 up top and the mid-thirties down low. We have partly sunny skies today as we get closer to the weekend, and the winds should be light. 

Our grooming team took care of over half of our open terrain last night, including almost all of our green and blue terrain. Take some relaxed turns on trails like Moonstruck or Lollapalooza, cruise down Lazy River or Northern Lights, or carve it up on classics like Right Stuff and White Heat. Three Mile Trail will remain ungroomed, and Rogue Angel will be closed today. Our snowmaking team was out on the ill last night as well, and they made snow on Vortex and Airglow. Please note that both of those trails will have active snowmaking this morning and will remain ungroomed. 

Presidents' Week is creeping up on us, and we have events planned for the whole family. There will be fireworks, a fire dancer, twilight skiing, Eddy and Betty Dinners, and more. We also have a few spots left for our Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River during President's Week. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now.

Think cold!

March 8, 2022

As blue skies start to peek through this morning, winds roll the clouds out of town. Please keep an eye on the lift and trail status as we will have delayed openings this morning. Winds are forecasted to calm as we ski into the afternoon, and we expect to have even more open terrain by tomorrow as this variable weather leaves town. As we experience wind holds today, we understand if you want to start your runs a little after 9AM first chair. You can have a relaxing morning at the Jordan Spa , or grab a bite to eat at one of our many dining options if you'd like to explore our slopeside offerings. Groomers have been working around the clock to provide you with the best and most open terrain possible in this new week. Tomorrow we are looking forward to fresh corduroy, warmer temperatures, and increasing sunshine as we get closer to the weekend.

At this time, ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires navigation of intermediate and advanced terrain. For beginner convenience, please take the Shuttle to access to the Jordan Hotel from other peaks today.

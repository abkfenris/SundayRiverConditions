December 29, 2022 | 6:40 AM

Morning Update

Happy Thursday, Sunday River. Another day closer to 2023 and another chance to get some last-minute runs in before the new year. Weather will be mild today, with temps staying in the 20s and low 30s. Clouds should roll by throughout the day, but winds should stay reasonably calm.

The Mountain Ops team got out there last night grooming fresh corduroy onto all open trails. They were able to get the snow guns going for the night. The guns will come off early this morning, and trails such as Obsession, Snowbound, Heat Off, Tempest, Jibe, Lollapalooza, and Grand Ave will be ungroomed.

Ski patrol gave the ok to open the White Heat Quad for the first time this season. Please be aware that this lift will have access to advanced terrain only.

With over 350 acres of open terrain and 55 trails to choose from, there is plenty of skiing, riding, and exploring to celebrate another year's end.

 We will see you out there!
-Team Snow

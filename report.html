Morning Report
Last Updated: February 5, 2024 | 7:35 AM

If you missed out on the incredible weather and conditions this weekend, don't worry, we've got another day of sunshine and fair temps coming right up. 

We're looking at over 830 acres of terrain over all 8 peaks to start the day. While sunshine and temps are expected to be just about perfect, please keep an eye out for wind holds throughout the morning. 

Snowmaking will be out and about, taking advantage of these cold temps this morning to resurface key terrain. Notably, American Express, Broadway, and sections of Lower Lazy River will have active snowmaking throughout the day, as well as Vortex and Airglow.

Looking ahead, White Heat is expected to close this week while the grooming team works on pushing out the whalebacks and laying down stripes. Keep an eye out for updates on that schedule.

Presidents' Week will be here before we know it, and we have events planned for the whole family. There will be fireworks, a fire dancer, twilight skiing, Eddy and Betty Dinners, and more. We also have a few spots left for our Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River during President's Week. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now.

Think cold!

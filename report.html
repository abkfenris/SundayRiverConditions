Morning Report
Last Updated: December 3, 2023 | 6:38 AM

Happy Sunday!

Today we’re looking at over 190 acres of groomed terrain for your skiing and riding pleasure. Our grooming team was hard at work throughout the night laying down our lovely corduroy, and our snowmaking team took advantage of the warmer temps to do some scheduled maintenance.

Last night we experienced some unsettled weather, so we can expect some softer turns today out on the mountain. Don’t get used to the comfortable temps of the morning, though. A cold front should be rolling in by this afternoon, and we’re expecting our favorite present: snow.

If you need a present other than snow, swing by the Sunday River Sports flagship store in the South Ridge Base Lodge. If you need to tune up last year’s present, stop by our Tune Shop for a fresh edge or a wax. With Christmas on the horizon, don’t forget to check out our schedule for Christmas Vacation Week. We’ve got an incredible lineup, and of course, the most dependable snow in New England.

Check out the South Ridge Food Court and the Foggy Goggle for all those midday snack cravings, with full hours listed in our River Guide.

T-minus 6 days until the Grand Opening of the Barker 6! We’re very excited for its grand debut, and we hope you are too. Check out the Barker 6 Grand Opening page for all of the details.

As always, keep an eye here for the latest info on our lift and trail status, as well as any other operational updates.

Think cold!

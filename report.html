Afternoon Report
Last Updated: December 24, 2023 | 1:20 PM

Merry Christmas Eve, folks!

Today has been a fun day of skiing and riding, covering over 130 acres with trails from Spruce Peak to White Cap. The grooming team will be out on the hill tonight getting those fresh stripes ready for the morning. Those folks certainly deserve some extra goodies from Santa this year. 

At this time, there is still no beginner terrain available. However, we are happy to share the South Ridge Express and the magic carpets will reopen on Tuesday, December 26. The plan is for the South Ridge Express to start spinning at 10AM with the magic carpets reopening at 8AM. Please note that beginner terrain will only be accessible out of South Ridge, where other parts of the resort will be considered advanced terrain.

With South Ridge reopening on Tuesday, that means our SnowSports School will be back in action. Offering both day lessons and season-long instructional programs, check out which lesson works for you. 

We have exciting events happening this week, from fireworks to dinner with Eddy and Betty. Check out our events page for the full list of fun for the whole family. Tonight, there will be a Christmas Eve Worship Service, starting at 6PM, in the Welcome Center at South Ridge.

Please continue to check the Mountain Report for additional operational updates as we continue to navigate the coming days.  Be sure to check out the River Guide for a full list of current hours as we open more shopping and dining options, and remember to check with area restaurants and businesses for updated hours as they work to reopen following the storm.

Many, many thanks to everyone who has supported Sunday River through this recovery process. We wouldn’t be here without everyone who helped.

We'll see you out there!

- Team Snow

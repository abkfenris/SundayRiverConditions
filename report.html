Morning Report
Last Updated: December 27, 2023 | 6:39 AM

It’s a little drizzly out there this morning, but with any luck that will clear up by the time we all finish our morning coffee. We’re looking at over 165 acres of groomed terrain and seven lifts today, with beginner terrain open at South Ridge. All open lifts are scheduled for 8AM. South Ridge reopening yesterday means that our SnowSports School reopened, so be sure to check them out for all of your lesson needs. 

Outta Here is open to access South Ridge from Barker, and Lower Lazy River is open to access Barker from South Ridge. We will have crossing guards at these junctions to allow construction to continue. Please note that only the lower section of Lower Lazy River will be open. It will not be open from the top of the South Ridge Express

In case you missed it, we have dinner with Eddy and Betty tonight, complete with a magic show. We have other great events lined up throughout the week, so be sure to check out our events page for all the info. Keep an eye on the River Guide for a full list of current hours. Remember to check with area restaurants and businesses for updated hours as they work to reopen following the storm.

Many, many thanks to everyone who has supported Sunday River through this recovery process. We wouldn’t be here without everyone who helped.

We'll see you out there!

- Team Snow

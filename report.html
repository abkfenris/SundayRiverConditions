Summer Update

Gear up for the Tough Mountain Challenge, but first, don't miss the epic Big Ass Bash on Friday, July 21! Pre-registered participants can conveniently check in at the South Ridge Base Lodge between 3PM-8PM and grab their event bib and materials. From 5PM-8PM, join the celebration filled with live music by Gorilla Finger Dub, delicious food, and non-stop family fun. The Big Ass Bash is open to all ages and free for everyone. Bring your appetite and party spirit for the ultimate pre-race festivity.

If you have not signed up for TMC yet, what are you waiting for? Same-day signup will be available on Saturday, July 22.

July is in full swing, and we're leaving the rainy early summer weeks behind. Take a scenic lift ride on North Peak, available Thursday-Sunday. Tickets are available online or at the Welcome Center for purchase. For more adventure, check out Sunday River Outfitters, offering kayaking, paddle boarding, archery, and new this season, axe throwing! Join us on Fridays from 3-7PM and Saturdays from 12-8PM. While you’re over there, check out the new Patio Bar at the Grand Summit Hotel for cocktails and a bite to eat.  

Book your tee times at Sunday River Golf Club, one of New England's most scenic courses. After your round, unwind at the Clubhouse with refreshing drinks and tasty eats. Please note that due to wet conditions, the golf course is currently restricted to cart paths only, and the driving range is temporarily closed.

For hiking enthusiasts, take a look at our summer hiking map . Please take note of any closed areas, but don't worry, there are still plenty of hiking opportunities around the resort. We've even added Trail L in the White Cap area, offering a lovely loop to enhance your journey up to Locke. 

Look ahead to the upcoming fall events that promise excitement and enjoyment. Join us for River Fest, a series of fun-filled weekends in Western Maine. Experience the Maine Brew Fest, Maine Artisan Fest, Mahoosuc Adventure Fest, and Fall Fest, where you can indulge in craft brews, sample cheeses, and even sign up to take a kaleidoscope class! Please visit our website for more information and where you can buy tickets.

Although winter is still months away, there's no shortage of activities here. Share your favorite summer moments with us on our Happy Place Found page and soak up the glorious summer sun while it’s out!

April 11, 2022

We have a stunning spring day ahead of us! Sunny trails and soft turns greet our skis this morning for 9AM first chair. Please keep an eye on the lift and trail status as winds may impact operations today. Groomers added a base of fresh corduroy to some of our favorite trails for you to enjoy. Ski nearly 200 acres of open terrain all day, as temperatures increase into the afternoon. For weekly updates on the best ways to get in your runs, head over to our Spring Schedule. Start your week with a beautiful Monday on the slopes!

Please note uphill access is closed for terrain from South Ridge, North Peak, Aurora Peak, Oz, Jordan Bowl, and trails serviced by Little White Cap Quad. All terrain requires navigation of intermediate and advanced trails, including ski-in/ski-out access to the Grand Summit Hotel. Road Runner access to White Cap Basin from other peaks is closed for the season. We've had an outstanding season skiing across all 8 peaks, and can't wait to continue next 2022-23 season!

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

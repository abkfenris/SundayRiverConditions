Weekend Update

Well, folks, it's been one heck of a season, and we couldn't thank you all enough for being a part of it. We still have one more weekend left to get some spring turns in, so let's get into it.

For this weekend, we'll be ripping it up in Jordan. Skiing and riding will be off of the Jordan 8, with ski-in, ski-out access at the Jordan Hotel, and food service available on the Sliders Deck. The warm temps have definitely taken a lot of snow from our slopes, but word on the block is that the Jordan Bowl is in great shape. We are planning to have top-to-bottom skiing on Lollapalooza, Rogue Angel, and Excalibur, with the chance for more depending on coverage. Due to late-season conditions, there will be no beginner terrain available.

Parking will be available in the Jordan overflow lot, located just before the main hotel entrance as you're traveling up the access road.

Please note that all uphill access is closed at this time. We are rolling straight into summer construction, and this means that there is a lot of equipment on the mountain. For everyone's safety, please respect this closure.

Any updates between now and Saturday will be posted here, so keep an eye out for the latest info! Have a great rest of the week!

-Team Snow

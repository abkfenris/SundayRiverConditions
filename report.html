November 30, 2021

It’s beginning to look a lot like winter here with snowmaking on Monday Mourning, Race Arena, Upper Risky Business, and Escapade, and 16 trails to enjoy from Locke to South Ridge! Cascades and Broadway, among others, are freshly groomed with corduroy for your enjoyment, accessible via Locke Mountain Triple and South Ridge Express, spinning from 9AM-3:45PM. 

Though the Barker Mountain Express remains closed today, you can have great conditions all to yourself by hiking across Jungle Road from Upper Sunday Punch. We’ll also have five features available in the terrain park on Upper Sunday Punch, too. Looking ahead to this weekend, we’re expecting to open Spruce Peak with Risky Business. Who’s joining us for a fun day on the slopes? 

All lift tickets need to be purchased online in advance. Have your RFID from last year? You can head straight to the lift! All skier services will be based out of Barker Lodge. Season Pass Office is also open at South Ridge Lodge and we will have shuttle service between Barker, South Ridge, and the hotels while the lifts spin. We are now accepting cashless forms of payment only. Please be sure to have a gift card, resort charge, credit card, or debit card available for on-site purchases. 

COVID-19 Protocols

Though we are no longer requiring guests to wear masks in general areas, we suggest masking indoors. However, some services may require you to wear a mask, so be sure to have one on hand. Please make sure to read through our COVID Winter Guide before visiting the resort to understand our operating plans and how they may affect you. Remember, we're all in this together.

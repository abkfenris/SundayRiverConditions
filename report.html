December 27, 2021

    We are enjoying another bright day slopeside skiing almost 400 acres, the largest amount of open terrain in the East. Snowmakers are filling Tempest and White Heat with piles of new snow tonight. We are adding Excalibur to our list for a total of 62 groomed trails for you to carve tomorrow morning. Consistently opening more terrain for you to explore is our number one priority, so stay tuned as we count down to the New Year with more snow and stripes. Tonight the Chondola and South Ridge Express will be spinning until 6:30PM to accommodate Twilight Skiing, continuing every night of Christmas Vacation Week. A winter wonderland illuminated by thousands of twinkling lights awaits you at the Après Aglow Walk open every night this week, and conveniently located behind the Grand Summit Hotel. Tickets for both activities can be purchased online throughout the week. Come join a ski-filled week of festivities slopeside!

    Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out accessible for your convenience. Skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently only accessible via blue terrain. To access the Jordan Hotel via green terrain, please ski Sensation in order to take the Jordan Mountain Double and then ride Jordan Bowl Express, to ski Lollapalooza. At this time, all other green trails into the Jordan Hotel are temporarily considered advanced terrain. Please note our only beginner terrain is located within the South Ridge Express Quad at the moment.

      Around the Resort

      Many restaurants and food trucks recently opened for the season, including Coffee Hound in South Ridge Lodge Food Court, and in the Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel. For retail needs head over to recently opened Aster & Birch Apparel located within the Jordan Hotel, as well as Sunday River Sports, Betty's Treats and Treasures, or unwind at the Jordan Spa.

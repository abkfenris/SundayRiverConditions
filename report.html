December 30, 2021

Counting down to the New Year with the most corduroy in the East to carve! This morning was a beautiful opening of 75 trails for you to enjoy under the sun. Tonight groomers are putting down stripes on over 400 acres of terrain. Christmas Vacation Week festivities are in full swing tonight and tomorrow, so check out activities including fireworks, magic, fire dancing and more! Twilight skiing is until 6:30PM tonight, as well as our new activity, Après Aglow, featuring thousands of twinkling lights on a trail through the forest outside of the Grand Summit Hotel. Our options across 8 peaks will have you celebrating!

    Please note Bear Paw is currently not beginner terrain, therefore no green trails are ski-out accessible from White Cap today. Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out for your convenience. To ski into the Grand Summit Hotel via green terrain, take Little White Cap Quad and ski down Moonstruck via Green Cheese. To access the Jordan Hotel via green terrain, please ride Jordan Bowl Express, to ski Lollapalooza. At this time, all other green trails into the Jordan Hotel are temporarily considered advanced terrain.

      Around the Resort

      Select services, such as lessons and rental equipment, may be limited or sold out from now through New Year's Day. Online reservations made in advance are recommended to guarantee availability.

      Many restaurants and food trucks are open for the season, including Coffee Hound in South Ridge Lodge Food Court, the Ona-Tripp Food Truck, and more. For shopping and relaxation needs, head over to recently opened Aster & Birch located within the Jordan Hotel, as well as Sunday River Sports, Betty's Treats and Treasures, or unwind at the Jordan Spa. Please note: Sliders will be closed this week with a planned reopening on January 7, but Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel is open until 8PM daily.

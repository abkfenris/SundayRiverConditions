Afternoon Report
Last Updated: December 3, 2023 | 1:15 PM

Powder alert!

The warmer temps of this morning certainly didn’t last very long. We had some softer turns out there today, but the slopes will firm right up this evening and throughout the night. And, the news everyone wants to hear: we’re in for a snow storm. Current predictions land us in the 8”-10” range, so think snow tonight.

The grooming team will make their way through the trails in batches throughout the night, so tomorrow we’ll be able to expect a delightful mix of fresh corduroy and powder. Due to the dropping temperatures, the snow making team will hit the mountain hard over the course of tonight and the next few days. Keep an eye out for terrain expansion next weekend, and don’t forget to swing by our Tune Shop to get your equipment in tip-top shape for fresh trails.

Speaking of next weekend, the Barker 6 is almost here. We’re celebrating the Grand Opening next Saturday, December 9th. Find all of the details of the celebration on the Barker 6 Grand Opening page.

With Christmas fast approaching, check out our Christmas Vacation Week lineup. We’ve got a whole host of events, great deals on lodging and lift tickets, and of course, the most dependable snow in New England. Keep an eye out for gift ideas at the Sunday River Sports flagship store, as well.

The South Ridge Base Lodge, South Ridge Food Court, and the Foggy Goggle are now open 7 days a week. Find a full list of hours in the River Guide.

As always, keep an eye here for the latest info on our lift and trail status, as well as any other operational updates.

Think cold!

- Team Snow

Morning Report
Last Updated: April 1, 2024 | 7:19 AM

Happy April Fools' Day to all that celebrate, and happy March 32nd to all that don't. We've got a stunnah of a day coming at you. When you think of 'spring skiing', today's the day that comes to mind: bright sun, a beautiful blue sky, and snow that'll soften up by the mid-morning. The winds that plagued us through the weekend have dissipated and we're left with a nice breeze today. Temps are currently in the high 20s around the base and will get up to the 40s by midday. Moral of the story: bring your sunscreen.

Please note that White Heat will remain closed today for events staff to continue their work tearing down the Bust 'N' Burn venue. A huge thank you to everyone who came out to spectate, compete, and volunteer their time!

We're exactly one week away from our Solar Eclipse Viewing Party. Head to the deck on North Peak Lodge for a chance to hold a piece of the moon, courtesy of the Maine Mineral & Gem Museum, while you watch the eclipse. It's sure to be out of this world. Spring Fest Weekend also starts this Friday, so there's tons to do around the mountain.

We'll see you out there!

TUESDAY, APRIL 20

Head out today for some midweek spring skiing out of Barker. If you're looking for more groomed surfaces, head to trails like Sunday Punch and Lazy River. For some fun bump skiing, head straight to Right Stuff. Through Friday, the Barker Mountain Express opens at 9AM, then on Saturday, April 24, we'll open at 8AM for the last day of the season. Conditions are considered for advanced skiers and riders only. Please be aware of all signage and markings as coverage may be thin in some areas. 

All skier services and food options are available at Barker Lodge, however, our shuttles are on call for any overflow parking into South Ridge. There is no ski-in/ski-out access at our hotels, so please make your way to the Barker area for access to the slopes. 

We are now skiing on Barker only. The lift will open at 9AM Monday through Friday, then at 8AM for the Season Send-off on Saturday. Our dining options are limited, so don't forget to check in so that you can plan ahead. Plus, one-night stays are available at the Grand Summit and Jordan Hotel through the rest of the season.

Happy Passholder Appreciation Month! For the month of April, we're celebrating all our passholders. If you're looking to become or renew your pass, the 2021-22 New England Pass is back on sale, granting access to Sunday River, Loon & Sugarloaf all next year. Current passholders can take advantage of renewal rates and new passholders can start using their pass now when paid in full. With options that fit your budget and schedule, lock in the lowest rates on next year's pass now through April 30. 

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines. As a reminder, face coverings are required in all public indoor and outdoor areas at the resort.

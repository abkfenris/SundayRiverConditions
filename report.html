Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 9, 2024 | 2:02 PM

The clouds of this morning didn't quite burn all the way off, but we still had some delightful weather for skiing and riding. Blue skies are just starting to peek through. We have 13 lifts opened, 852 acres of skiable terrain, and many a groomer to cruise. It was a great day today, and there's more of that in store for tomorrow. 

Our snowmaking team will be taking a break from blasting the guns through the weekend as temps continue to stay warm. They'll be back on the hill early Monday morning to continue resurfacing terrain. Our grooming team will be out tonight, and they'll be working hard to lay stripes on over half of our open trails. 

We have twilight skiing skiing tonight off of the Chondola and the South Ridge Express. Trying to plan your weekend? There are still some spots available for our new Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River for tomorrow night. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now. Don't forget: Presidents' Week is right around the corner, and we have events planned for the whole family, including fire dancing, a circus, laser tag, and more.

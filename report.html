Welcome to the Thanksgiving Thursday edition of the Mountain Report, and happy turkey day from us here at the River. It's shaping up to be a wonderful day out on the slopes, and we hope you join us. Don't forget to leave the cooking to us and visit Camp for Thanksgiving Dinner this evening.

They've been working their butts off to make winter happen, so be sure to stop and thank them whenever you get the chance.

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 21, 2024 | 1:42 PM

The clouds of this morning have mostly burned away, and now we have blue skies and sunshine for the rest of the afternoon. Temperatures warmed up as expected, and the temperature inversion is no more. Winds are light, and the skiing is fast.

Our snowmaking team will be out again tonight, and they'll continue to work on resurfacing White Heat, as well as American Express and Boots Off. Those trails will be ungroomed tomorrow. Our grooming team will give us a similar list of trails as last night, and they'll be taking the winch cat out to do some additional cleanup. Our parks team will continue working on rebuilding Flow State, as well.

Presidents' Week is continuing, and our events schedule remains jam-packed. Tonight, we have an Eddy &Betty Dinner in the Maoosuc Room from 6-8PM. Enjoy dinner and a night of fun with our favorite yetis, and enjoy a magic show with Philip the Magician. Get your tickets before they're gone! We have twilight skiing tonight as well, so stick around and ski under the lights until 6:30PM. After that, head over to the Grand Summit Hotel to check out the Après Aglow Lighted Trail from 4:30-8:30PM. We also have live music today at the Foggy Goggle, so swing by to see the Dapper Gents play from 3-6PM.  Still deciding what to do this vacation week? We have single-night stays at all resort properties this weekend through Presidents' Week, so be sure to check that out.

We'll see you out there!

- Team Snow

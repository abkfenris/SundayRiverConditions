Morning Report
Last Updated: January 2, 2024 | 7:42 AM

We like the weather that's come with the new year a lot more than the weather we ended 2023 with. These persistent cold temps have been exactly what our snowmakers have been waiting for, and if you believe the forecast, there's more coming our way. 

For today, temps are expected to reach the high 20s to low 30s, making for some wonderfully comfortable weather. The sun is also expected to dominate the sky today, so get working on those goggle tans. 

Snowmaking had another excellent night last night and will continue their efforts as long as temps allow. While most of their efforts are on closed trails, keep an eye out for some fresh snowmaking on Broadway, as well as active snowmaking on portions of Lower Downdraft and American Express.

The grooming team had another great night and has been making great progress in the Jordan Bowl. Pending a final update this afternoon, Jordan is expected to re-open tomorrow at 9 AM. Once online, we'll be back skiing and riding across all 8 peaks.

As more trails open and expansion continues, keep an eye here for the latest info from the mountain.

Next Steps

Thanks to the amazing efforts of our snowmakers, we plan to open the Jordan Bowl tomorrow, Wednesday, January 3. This will include ski-in, ski-out access to the Jordan Hotel.

The Alpeniglu is scheduled to re-open on Friday, January 5, as well as the Outpost, for convenient slopeside snacks and beverages.

Conditions have been amazing all week, and today is no different. There was a little bit of everything with was soft snow, bumps, and groomers across all eight peaks. Plus, things are about to get even better. Tonight snowmakers are hitting the slopes and firing up the snow guns. Mid-March snowmaking? That's right. We are holding onto winter but also getting ready for an awesome spring around the corner. Right Stuff, Lower Sunday Punch, and Lower Lazy River will get the goods tonight, and with sunshine and blue sky in the forecast, we are going to have an amazing weekend ahead of us.

Plus, save 15% on all resort lodging, unless you're a Passholder, then you get even more savings on lodging. With operations open daily through April 24, there is plenty of ski season ahead. The 2021-22 New England Pass is back on sale, granting access to Sunday River, Loon & Sugarloaf all next year. Current passholders can take advantage of renewal rates and new passholders can start using their pass on March 22 when paid in full. With options that fit your budget and schedule, lock in the lowest rates on next year's pass now through April 30.

We require online purchase of lift tickets and limit the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

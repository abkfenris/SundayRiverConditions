Afternoon Report

You couldn't ask for a better day than today. The sun shone bright; the wind remained calm, and fresh groomed trails softened up as the day went on. Skiers and riders alike took to the slopes early to take advantage of the beautiful weather and great terrain.

Our mountain ops team will take to the slopes tonight to clean up our trails and smooth them out for the week ahead. Looking into the week, we see some interesting weather headed our way. We are starting the week tomorrow with a mild and cloudy day. A winter weather warning begins tomorrow afternoon and goes until Wednesday morning. Let's hope this can provide another thick fresh layer of snow for us to enjoy. Winter isn't over yet, and we are still enjoying all eight peaks, good snow, and smooth terrain. Don't forget, the 2023-24 New England Passes are now available; the only chance to lock in your pass at the year's lowest prices during our one-week flash sale.

See you next time!
-Team Snow

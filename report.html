FRIDAY, MARCH 26

7:30AM Update: the White Heat Quad will have a delayed opening due to a mechanical issue. Continue to check lift status for updates. 

We're waking up to a classic Maine spring morning with some wet weather rolling through. Our groomers hit the slopes overnight to press stripes on about 50 trails, as they switch to a more limited grooming plan to preserve snow. If you're heading out there today, expect softening snow throughout the day with groomed surfaces in the morning turning to bumps by the afternoon. Looking ahead, we have sunshine returning tomorrow for an awesome Saturday on the slopes. If your legs aren’t feeling it yet, then you either haven’t been here enough this week or you’re not skiing enough bumps. 

Please note that the South Ridge takeout window will be closed today, head over to our River Guide for more dining options. 

With operations open daily through April 24, there is plenty of ski season ahead. View the entire Spring Schedule to learn more about peak access and resort services this spring.

Looking for a last minute weekend getaway? One-night stays are now available at the Jordan Hotel and Snow Cap Inn for Saturday night, March 27. 

The 2021-22 New England Pass is back on sale, granting access to Sunday River, Loon & Sugarloaf all next year. Current passholders can take advantage of renewal rates and new passholders can start using their pass now when paid in full. With options that fit your budget and schedule, lock in the lowest rates on next year's pass now through April 30.

We require online purchase of lift tickets and limit the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines.

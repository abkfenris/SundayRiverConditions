December 24, 2021

It's the Eve before Christmas and all through the mountain, snowmakers blast guns, and corduroy trails await carving. Today White Heat Quad opens, followed by North Peak Quad tomorrow! All scheduled lifts are spinning from 9AM-3:45PM today. We have over 50 open trails, and more than 300 groomed acres for you to shred all day. Terrain expansion is at the top of our list, so stay tuned as we count down to the new year by opening even more trails. If you prefer celebrating your holidays under the stars, we have Twilight Skiing every night starting this Sunday, the Après Aglow Walk opening next week, and more festivities for your enjoyment. Carve out some time to celebrate on the slopes!

Since Jordan Bowl is open, both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out access for your convenience. Skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently only accessible via blue terrain. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. Come celebrate the holidays in this winter wonderland!

    Around the Resort

    Select services, such as rentals and equipment rentals, may be limited over the upcoming vacation week. Online reservations can pick up equipment through the South Ridge tunnel.

    Betty's Treats and Treasures is now open! Based on the Betty the Yeti book, and located next to South Ridge Lodge, this shop brings our beloved mascot's dream to life. Grab a photo in the picture-perfect purple gondola Cabin outside before entering the store filled with candy, sweets, treats, and unique Sunday River gift items!

    Recently opened for the season, Sliders Restaurant is conveniently located off Lollapalooza and inside the Jordan Hotel. The Mountain Room has also opened, located within Peak Lodge. For retail needs head over to Sunday River Sports, Betty's Treats and Treasures, or unwind at the Jordan Spa.

Afternoon Report

Spring weather just doesn't really get much better than this. It's been nothing but blue skies and beautiful temps for the final weekend of the Barker Express, and we're so stoked to be sharing it with all of you.

Looking ahead, we are planning to spin the Chondola and the Spruce Peak Triple tomorrow. The mountain will be closed Tuesday-Friday while we re-group for one more hurrah. Please note, uphill access will be closed from Tuesday-Friday. We have a big summer of upgrades planned, which means lots of heavy machinery moving around the mountain. 

There are still a few more hours left to get your final ride on the quad before we call last chair for good. Come get yours before it's too late.

We'll see you out there!

-Team Snow

Afternoon Update

Good afternoon everyone. Today was a true Maine Friday, but we were able to push through thanks to our incredible Mountain Ops team, and those who braved the weather were rewarded with some very good, albeit cold skiing.

Tomorrow, we are planning for an 8 AM opening following the same lift schedule as today. The Jordan 8, Chondola, South Ridge Express, Barker Mountain Express, Spruce Peak Triple, White Cap Quad, Little White Cap Quad, and the Sundance Surface Lifts will all provide access to the slopes. That being said, winds are expected to be cranking, and disruptions to this plan are expected. Information will be updated here as soon as available.

As a reminder, all Uphill Access will be closed tomorrow. In addition, Night Skiing is canceled this weekend, as well as Apres Aglow and Fireworks. 

See you in the lodge!
-Team Snow

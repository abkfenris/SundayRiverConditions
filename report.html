Around the Resort

2022-23 New England Passes is now on sale! Check out our new payment plan options, expanded perks, and start using next year's pass this March 21.

Get on the slopes with your girlfriends at Women's Weekend! Arriving March 25-27, this women-only wellness weekend focuses on fun, fitness and above all, our female friendships. Take this opportunity to try out plenty of different pints here, and pick up your passport to start collecting stamps!

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, featuring delicious sweets and special souvenirs from our exclusive logowear collection. Check out our many retail options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Unwind and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. Our spa is paired with a boutique, where you can find beauty and wellness products, athletic wear, and more. You can find even more retail options across the resort!

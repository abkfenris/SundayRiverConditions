Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 20, 2024 | 1:46 PM

Well, today certainly didn't disappoint. Comfortable temps have moved into the area this afternoon, and the sun is shining away. It's been an incredible day on the slopes, and tomorrow is looking even better.

Our snowmaking team will be back on the hill this evening after a well-deserved break and will be taking advantage of the cold temps to pump out the cold smoke onto White Heat and Excalibur, plus some other spots. Those trails will be ungroomed in the morning and will have incredible man-made powder from top to bottom. 

The grooming team will also be back on the hill this evening and will be resurfacing trails across all eight peaks.With more sunshine in the surface, it's going to be an excellent day to rip some cord in the morning.

Presidents' Week is continuing, and our jam-packed events schedule is charging right along. Tonight, we've got the Sparks Arc Critter Encounters in the Mahoosuc Room at South Ridge starting at 5:30PM. We also have twilight skiing every night this week, so stick around and ski under the lights until 6:30PM. After that, head over to the Grand Summit Hotel to check out the Après Aglow Lighted Trail from 4:30-8:30PM. Still deciding on your vacation plans? We have single-night stays at all resort properties this weekend through Presidents' Week, so be sure to check that out.

We'll see you out there!

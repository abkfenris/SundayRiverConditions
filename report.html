Summer Update

We're well into July, and looking forward to putting the rain of the early summer weeks behind us. Scenic lift rides are in full swing, with access to North Peak running Thursday-Sunday. Sunday River Outfitters is also well underway, with kayaking, paddleboarding, archery, and new this season, axe throwing! Axe throwing will also be open Fridays from 3-7PM, and Saturdays from 12-8PM, with drinks and food available out of the Patio Bar at the Grand Summit Hotel.

Tee times are available at the Sunday River Golf Club. Join us for a round at one of the most scenic courses in New England, and don't forget to stop by the Clubhouse after for post-round drinks and eats. Please note, due to the abnormally wet conditions, the golf course is currently cart path only, and the driving range is temporarily closed.

On the events side of things, the New England Forest Rally kicks off this weekend, with Tough Mountain Challenge and Mini Mountain Challenge following it up next week, on July 22 and 23.

For those looking to earn their views and hit the hiking trails, please remember to take a look at our summer hiking map, found here. Please take note of the closed areas around the mountain. Even with some closed zones, there's still plenty of hiking around the resort. We've added Trail L in the White Cap area that can turn your journey up to Locke into a nice loop. 

It may not be winter, but there's still plenty to do up here! Share your favorite summer moments with us on our Happy Place Found page and soak up that summer sun!

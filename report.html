Afternoon Report
Last Updated: January 13, 2024 | 1:31 PM

Today brought 5-6" of snowfall to our mountain, and we couldn't be happier. The winds were lower than predicted, the snow fell longer than we thought it would, and by mid-morning, we had 526 acres of open terrain. Today was a great day.

Tonight, our snowmaking team will be hitting some key areas on this side of the mountain, as well as further west. We're hoping to open terrain on Merill Hill within the next few days. Our grooming team will be laying stripes on a similar list of trails as last night, with the potential addition of Merrill Hill. Stay tuned for new terrain in the coming days. 

This weekend is Martin Luther King Jr. Weekend, and we have events lined up for the whole family. We have twilight skiing tonight in South Ridge, as well as a fire dancer at 6:15PM and 6:45PM, and fireworks at 7PM. Head to the Jordan Spa for Sips & Sweets today from 4-5:30PM, and check out our live music at the Foggy Goggle from 3-6PM, the Barker Bar from 2-5PM, and Shipyard from 2-5PM and 7-10PM. The Alpeniglu at Sunday River has a new après experience, as well; take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some drinks, sweets, and music, then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now.

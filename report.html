Afternoon Report
Last Updated: December 26, 2023 | 1:49 PM

Today we had mostly sunny skies, comfortable temps, and over 160 acres of open terrain. We appreciate everyone’s patience with our detours around active construction as we work to repair our Mountain.

We’re looking at the same lineup of trails for tomorrow morning, with all groomed terrain. Due to warmer temperatures, there will not be any snowmaking tonight. Once temperatures cool down, our snowmaking team will be firing up the guns and working on expanding our terrain over to Jordan, so think cold.

South Ridge reopened today, and along with it, our beginner terrain and the SnowSports School. We have lessons for everyone, whether you're putting on skis for the first time or you've been snowboarding for years. Be sure to check out the SnowSports page for more info. 

We have a whole host of events planned for this week, from fireworks and dinner with Eddy and Betty to New Year’s Eve with Skosh at the Foggy Goggle. Check out our events page for more details, and keep an eye on the River Guide for a full list of current hours. Remember to check with area restaurants and businesses for updated hours as they work to reopen following the storm. 

Many, many thanks to everyone who has supported Sunday River through this recovery process. We wouldn’t be here without everyone who helped.

We'll see you out there!

- Team Snow

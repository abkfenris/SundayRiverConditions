Morning Update

Good morning my fellow Sunday River riders, and happy Sunday. The skies are clear, and the sun is just beginning to peak over the mountains on this crisp 20-degree morning.

Our Mountain Ops team took advantage of the colder temps last night and this morning. The crew turned on over 250 guns to make a fresh layer of snow. Some snowmaking will continue today on trails such as Lower Down Draft, Grand Rapids, Easy Street, and a few still closed trails. This snow will be pushed around and smoothed out by the first chair this morning, laying new stripes onto all open terrain.

It looks like we should have a bluebird day with the sun shining bright and clear skies. Winds up top are pretty gusty this morning; however, everything should calm down as the day continues. Keep an eye on the mountain report for  trail and lift status changes as Ski Patrol hit the mountain to inspect conditions.

Please note: There is currently no beginner terrain into and out of the Grand Summit Hotel.

Visiting Sunday River anytime soon? We are offering one-night stays at the Snow Cap Inn and Grand Summit hotel this weekend. Also, be sure to take a look at our dash deals sale. Here you will find the best deals on lodging and the return of our Threedom Pack. This sale ends January 9th.

See you on the slopes!
-Team Snow

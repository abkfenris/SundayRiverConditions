January 22, 2022

We are jumping around the mountain today! In anticipation of the big air lightshow Wicked Air & Après, you can catch your own air on Who-Ville, now featuring three jumps. Last night groomers put down fresh corduroy on 80 trails, including South Paw and Three Mile. Snowmakers are blasting guns on 3D and Lazy River this morning and throughout the weekend to make piles and piles of fresh snow. You can have a full day of skiing with first chair at 8:00AM, and Twilight Skiing lifts spinning until 6:30PM tonight. Make the most open terrain in the East your playground with 111 trails and 698 acres to choose from today!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl.

Afternoon Report

Today was a classic New England day with weather changing on a dime. We started the day with some nice fluffy flakes across the resort, which slowly changed over to sleet, and then a touch of that not-so-frozen precipitation. Thankfully the rain was very brief and localized to the lower elevations, so that won't cause any issues with tonight's grooming and snowmaking. 

While we are wrapping up the long weekend, we do have more events coming up. Next Monday marks the start of Senior Week running from January 23 through January 27. And as always, keep your eyes on the Mountain Report for all your resort-wide updates.

 See you up there!
-Team Snow

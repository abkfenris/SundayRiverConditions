Morning Report
Last Updated: February 6, 2024 | 7:35 AM

This sunshine just won't quit, and we are not complaining about it. Another day of perfect skies and comfortable temps is queued up for today. Let's get into it.

Snowmaking had another productive night thanks to temps dropping into the single digits. Some trails will have ungroomed snowmaking on them today, so keep an eye out for signage from the Patrol team. In addition, snowmaking on Vortex and Airglow will remain online throughout the day. 

White Heat will remain closed today. The grooming team spent the night pushing out piles and building that trail and expects to put stripes on it in the next night or two. Corduroy can be found across all 8 peaks today, so be sure to take advantage of the beautiful weather and do some exploring!

Presidents' Week will be here before we know it, and we have events planned for the whole family. There will be fireworks, a fire dancer, twilight skiing, Eddy and Betty Dinners, and more. We also have a few spots left for our Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River during President's Week. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now.

Think cold!

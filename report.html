Afternoon Report
Last Updated: December 9, 2023 | 1:23 PM

The weekend is off to a great start so far. Between the new Barker 6, some soft turns, and the appearance of Donny Pelletier, it was a day for the books. With over 250 acres of skiable terrain and comfortable temps, we had a lovely time on the mountain. 

Our snowmaking team will be giving the snowguns a break tonight due to the warmer temperatures, but they'll be out preparing for the next possible window. Our grooming team will out there pushing snow and laying stripes in anticipation of tomorrow morning. We can expect a similar lineup of trails tomorrow. 

This weekend’s events aren’t over yet, folks. Join us tomorrow for our annual Santa Sunday event. For the best view of our shredding Santas, spectators should head to South Ridge by 11AM. Pre-registered participants should check in between 7:30-10:30AM tomorrow morning at the South Ridge Base Lodge. For all event details, head to our Santa Sunday page in the River Guide.

Santa Sunday means Christmas is creeping up on us, so don’t forget to take a look at our event lineup. In addition to the most dependable snow in New England, we have fun for the whole family and great deals on lodging and lift tickets. If you’re in need of a last minute gift for the skier or rider in your life, stop by the Sunday River Sports flagship store.

As always, keep an eye here for the latest info on our lift and trail status and any other operational updates.

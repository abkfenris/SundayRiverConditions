Afternoon Update

Some festive flakes have been decorating the mountain all day, and it's just the boost we were looking for. While this will not contribute anything meaningful to accumulation, it's always great to see snowflakes flying. We're rolling it back tonight, with the grooming team tackling the usual list of suspects. Snowmaking is continuing its march towards expansion, and taking advantage of every second of cold temps we're afforded. There are even rumors of top-to-bottom skiing on White Cap tomorrow, adding our eight peak to the repertoire. 

We're charging through holiday week, but don't forget, we've got a ton of awesome events going on all week as part of our holiday week celebrations. With everything from live music to visits with Eddy and Betty, there's something for everyone.

We'll see you out there!

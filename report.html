Morning Report
Last Updated: February 10, 2024 | 6:35 AM

Happy Saturday, folks. It's going to be a warm one out there today, and we currently have a temperature inversion on the mountain, with temps in the mid-forties up top and high twenties down in the base. That should flip by this afternoon, and we're expecting temperatures in the high thirties at the summits and low forties in the base areas. With the sun just peeking through the clouds throughout the day, this looks like a great day to hit the slopes. 

Our grooming team has been on the slopes all night, setting us up with some lovely corduroy on over half of our open terrain. All of our green terrain on White Cap, South Ridge, Merrill Hill, Aurora, and Jordan will be groomed, as well as the majority of our blue terrain. Three Mile Trail, Polaris, and Backside remain ungroomed. Upper Vortex was groomed out last night, so head over that way for some top-to-bottom stripes. Shock Wave is ungroomed and advanced terrain. Be sure to check out our lift and trail status below for the most up-to-date information throughout the day. 

We have twilight skiing skiing tonight off of the Chondola and the South Ridge Express. Stop by the Jordan Spa for Sips & Sweets today from 4-5:30PM, and enjoy some complimentary prosecco and sweets while you browse and mingle.  There's live music at three different locations across the mountain today; Lexi James will be at the Foggy Goggle from 3-6PM, Shipyard will have Chase Job from 2-PM and A Tribute to Chris Stapleton and Miranda Lambert from 7-10PM, and Brad Hooper will be at the Barker Bar from 2-5PM. There are still some spots available for our new Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River tonight. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now. Don't forget: Presidents' Week is right around the corner, and we have events planned for the whole family, including fire dancing, a circus, laser tag, and more. 

Think cold!

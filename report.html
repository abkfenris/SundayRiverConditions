Afternoon Report

One more report and that will do it for January! It's hard to believe that we've already flown through the first month of the year, but 3 feet of snow will do that to you. We're heading into February with nearly 800 acres of skiing and riding across all 8 peaks. It's certainly a great time to be here in Western Maine.

Looking ahead, we're settling into quite a blustery cold stretch of weather. We're Mainers, we don't mind some cold, but be sure to take all the necessary precautions when on the hill. 

 See you out there!
-Team Snow

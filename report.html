Thank you for a great season.

We're now closed for skiing and riding, which includes uphill access. Please be aware that our team will be working on cleaning up the resort, so heavy equipment and machinery will be active on the mountain over the next few weeks.

It's never too early to start planning for next winter. If you're looking to become or renew your pass, the 2021-22 New England Pass is on sale, granting access to Sunday River, Loon & Sugarloaf all next year. Current passholders can take advantage of renewal rates. With options that fit your budget and schedule, lock in the lowest rates on next year's pass now through April 30. Passes will go off sale completely as of May 1. 

Looking foreward, we have a fun summer planned ahead. The Sunday River Golf Club is scheduled to open on May 21. Tee times for the entire season are available and the earlier you book, the more you'll save. We'll also be open for scenic lift rides, which are always free for 2021-22 passholers, plus the local mountain bike trails, endless hikes, and iconic swimming holes will keep us busy all summer long. Stay tuned for more warm-weather fun.

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: December 4, 2023 | 1:01 PM

Fresh powder? Yes, please.

We had 12+ inches of powder today by the time lifts were spinning, and we still had some flurries throughout the morning. While all open trails were groomed last night, our more advanced terrain was groomed early, meaning that there was still a chance to play in the pristine powder. With comfortable temps, low wind, and most importantly, new snow, it was a fantastic morning to hit the slopes.

Mountain Ops will be hard at work again tonight. The Snowmaking Team will be blowing as much snow as they can, wherever they can. Keep an eye out for new terrain by next weekend. Our grooming team will be at it again, and we can plan on some fresh corduroy for tomorrow.

This coming Saturday is the grand opening of the Barker 6. Celebrations start at 8:30 AM, and all of the details can be found on the Barker 6 Grand Opening page.

Don’t forget to take a look at our Christmas Vacation Week plans. We’ve got a whole host of events, great deals on lodging and lift tickets, and of course, the most dependable snow in New England. Keep an eye out for gift ideas at the Sunday River Sports flagship store, as well.

As always, keep an eye here for the latest info on our lift and trail status, as well as any other operational updates.

Think cold!

- Team Snow

February 8, 2022

Over 2 feet of flakes have covered all 8 peaks in the past week! The flurries are still flying as Mother Nature tops off our trails with 5-8” of new snow. Take a new run each time, with all 135 glades and trails open for you to have lots of options. After three weeks we already have 53” of accumulation, making for fantastic conditions across the mountain. Groomers are putting stripes on nearly 90 trails for you to carve into tomorrow morning at 9AM first chair. Explore a total of almost 2,000 acres to take tree skiing turns with our boundary-to-boundary skiing. As the snowy system rolls out of town later this afternoon, we could expect some winds to impact lifts before last chair. Please check the lift and trail status as we keep you updated while the flurries fly. Ski through your week on piles of fresh snow!

Around the Resort

Presidents' Week is less than two weeks away! From February 18-27 we have exciting activities including fireworks, live music, fire dancing, and more. Get tickets for family and friends so you can look forward to a fun vacation on the slopes!

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. You can also treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures with delicious candy, or shop our exclusive logowear collection.

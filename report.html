Morning Report
Last Updated: December 28, 2023 | 6:39 AM

Today we have 24 open and groomed trails, serviced by 7 lifts. Some warmer temperatures make for some softer snow; perfect for laying right into those edges on the slopes. We’re expecting temperatures in the upper thirties with little to no wind. Who’s up for first chair?

Please be aware that crossing guards will be posted at Outta Here and Lower Lazy River to assist with travel between Barker and South Ridge. We appreciate everyone’s patience with our active construction as we continue repairs.

Tonight, we have fire dancing and fireworks at South Ridge. We have other great events lined up for this week, including a New Year’s Eve Party at the Foggy Goggle. Check out our Christmas Vacation Week page for more details, and be sure to keep an eye on the River Guide for a full list of current hours. Remember to check with area restaurants and businesses for updated hours as they work to reopen following the storm.

Thank you to everyone who has supported Sunday River through this recovery process. We wouldn’t be here without everyone who helped.

We'll see you out there!

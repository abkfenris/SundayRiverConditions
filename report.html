Good morning and happy Saturday, Sunday River. Today, we have over 610 acres and 19 scheduled lifts for your skiing and riding pleasure. We're looking at temperatures in the mid to high teens at the summits and temps in the mid-twenties down in the base areas, cloudy skies, and a chance of flurries. Thank snow!

For groomed terrain, Bear Paw and Three Mile Trail rejoined the list. The grooming team also took care of all the new snow on Lower Downdraft, Grand Rapids, Broadway, Lollapalooza, Tourist Trap, and Lower American Express. They also laid stripes on all of Merrill Hill, as well, as part of the weekend lineup of groomed terrain. Those classic cruisers will be prime this morning with corduroy out of yesterday's powder, so check out trails like Right Stuff or Obsession for some first tracks carving. We will have active snowmaking on White Heat, Upper and Lower Vortex, Upper Downdraft, and the upper section of American Express today, but all of those snow guns should come offline sometime this morning.

For some more live entertainment tonight, party with Skosh at the Foggy Goggle from 3-6PM, or listen to the Dapper Gents Duo from 2-5PM at the Barker Bar. Find the full music schedule on our Après and Nightlife page. Stop by the Jordan Spa for Sips & Sweets today from 4-5:30PM today, and enjoy some prosecco and a sweet treat while you mingle.

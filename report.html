Tonight, the grooming team is looking to put a little TLC on terrain throughout the mountain.  This weekend, lift access is scheduled to be out of White Cap, Barker, North Peak, Spruce Peak, South Ridge, and the Jordan Bowl, with beginner terrain accessible off the South Ridge Express. As we move into the season's final days, please note that the Jordan Hotel is still ski-in/ski-out, but the Grand Summit is just ski-in. For additional operational details, please visit the Spring Schedule. 

This weekend we've got a full slate of activities! Tomorrow is the crowd favorite, Pond-A-Palooza, starting at 11AM in South Ridge. Registration is first come, first served, so if you want to make a splash (so to speak), get there early to secure your spot. Then on Sunday, we've got the Great Tailgate in Barker, and our annual Passholder Appreciation Lunch in South Ridge.

We'll see you out there!

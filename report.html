December 26, 2021

The sun is rising on the most open terrain in the East, with over 60 trails for you to carve today. Scattered flurries are forecasted to greet our slopes throughout the day, so stay tuned. Snowmakers blasted guns on White Heat last night and yesterday, and Moonstruck, Green Cheese, and Cutoff are groomed and open for the season! Added to our growing list of open lifts this morning, at 8AM North Peak Express and Little White Cap Quad are spinning, shortly followed by Quantum Leap Triple and Locke Mountain Triple at 9AM. The Chondola and South Ridge Express will be spinning until 6:30PM to accomodate Twilight Skiing, continuing every night of Christmas Vacation Week. Tonight, thousands of twinkeling lights welcome you to an enchanting walk in the woods at the opening of the Après Aglow Walk. Tickets for both activities can be purchased online for your convenience. Options await you at the mountain!

Since Jordan Bowl is open, both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out access for your convenience. Skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently only accessible via blue terrain. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. Come ski in a winter wonderland!

Looking for a last-minute getaway? One-night stays are available at the Jordan Hotel for Sunday night (December 26) for a limited number of rooms. Book online while they last.

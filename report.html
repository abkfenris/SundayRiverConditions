SATURDAY, FEBRUARY 13

We've kicked off Presidents' Week with a full day of endless mountain views and beautiful skiing and riding across eight peaks. Our groomers will head out tonight on their normal routes to reset the slopes and press the perfect stripes. Mother Nature might be rolling through later this evening to deliver a couple inches of snow, making for some awesome conditions for tomorrow. Keep it up out there, as there is a lot to enjoy, and continue skiing tonight until 6:30PM with Twilight Skiing. End your night on the slopes with a fireworks show at 7PM at South Ridge. 

We have quite the line-up for Presidents’ Week with twilight skiing every night, fireworks, and Valentine’s Day specials. Plus, let’s not forget the best part of all, that we are 100% open with fantastic conditions. As you plan your trip please keep in mind lift tickets are limited and days are close to selling out, so plan ahead if you want to hit the slopes next week. Or book lodging with us and guarantee access to full-day lift tickets. Please note that kids ski school lessons are now available to book online with limited availability.

One-night stays are now available through Thursday, February 18, for the Jordan Hotel and Snow Cap Inn. 

We are requiring online lift ticket purchase and setting limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, half-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines.

Mountain Report
Last Updated: October 13, 2023

While our heart is set on winter, we still have a few days left to enjoy out on the golf course. Sunday River Golf Club will be closing for the season on Sunday, October 15.  There are still some tee times available, and you can book yours online. And if you haven't made the trip for dinner, make sure to cross enjoying a delicious meal at the Club House off your summer bucket list. Chef Brian and Chef Chad are serving some beautiful dishes at one of the prettiest dining locations around. 

In case you missed it, earlier this week the Mountain Operations team completed our annual snowmaking test. 4 HKD Impulse tower guns sprung to life atop the Jordan Bowl, signaling the official kick-off to the winter countdown. We can't wait for prolonged cold temps to settle into Western Maine, and we'll be ready to go when it does.

If you haven't yet heard, our opening plans for this season have changed. This year, we're heading west and will be opening in the Jordan Bowl. It's a huge shift that we couldn't be more excited about, and will make for an unprecedented early-season experience.

We can't wait to see you for the first turns of the season. 

Think cold!

- Team Snow

Morning Report

Mother nature teased us last night with just a light dusting of snow early at night. Hopefully, this is a taste of what's in store for the rest of the week as snow storms are coming. The Mountain Ops team took matters into their own hands and created a fresh layer of snow overnight and this morning on trails such as Obsession, American Express, and Sunday Punch to make things more interesting. The rest of the usual trails were groomed out and may have a nice dusting still on the surface when you get here.

The weather today is a bit windy as gusts up top pass through. Monitor the mountain report for possible wind holds until everything calms down by lunchtime. These winds will push clouds by throughout the day, and temps will range in the high-20s to mid-30s. We still see a solid chance for snowfall starting late tonight and continuing into Thursday, leaving a solid layer of fresh snow for us all to enjoy and a great reason to take advantage of our one-night stays now available for Presidents' Week. All properties, all week; grab yours before they're gone! 

Be sure to check out all we have going on and off the slopes this week for our Presidents' Week holiday, and check out one of our many dining options on the resort after a long day of skiing and riding.

We'll see you out there!

-Team Snow

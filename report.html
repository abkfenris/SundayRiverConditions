What a fantastic Friday, folks. The sun was shining and the snow was fast. By the time we got out there, the sun had softened some of that corduroy, making it prime time to shred. How are those early-season goggle tans coming?

He’ll be signing posters, as well, so swing on by to meet him tomorrow afternoon.

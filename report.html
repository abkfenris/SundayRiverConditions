Around the Resort

Presidents' Week is here. All week long is packed with activities, including live music, fireworks, Twilight Skiing, the Après Aglow lighted walk, and more. Get tickets for family and friends so you can look forward to a fun vacation on the slopes!

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. You can also treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures with delicious candy, or shop our exclusive logowear collection.

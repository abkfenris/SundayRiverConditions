Morning Report
Last Updated: November 18, 2023 | 6:45 AM

Good morning, and welcome to the first Saturday of the 2023-24 season! We're in the last day of this stretch of warm weather, and we're happy to report that the forecasted rain overnight was more of a drizzle and is forecasted to move out of the area nice and early, setting us up for an amazing day of soft turns out in Jordan.

Overnight, the groomers resurfaced Lollapalooza, Grand Ave, Boots On, and Boots Off, while our snowmakers took another well deserved night off. Looking ahead, we have have one more warm day coming up tomorrow before a stretch of cold temps move into the area. Our snowmaking team is ready to take advantage once that happens, with all eyes set on expanding our trail count. 

In addition to our downhill terrain, a 4-feature hike park is set up in Jordan, with a variety of boxes and rails.

If you need ticket assistance, a Guest Service station is located in the bottom terminal of the Jordan 8. All Ikon Pass holders who need assistance must visit the Welcome Center in the South Ridge Base Lodge.

Please note that early-season, variable terrain will be present on all trails. Lastly, don't forget to check out our Early Season Guide.

Locke Mountain will serve as the resort's dedicated early-season training venue for our partners Gould Academy and Bates College, as well as pre-booked ski teams only. All public access to snow surfaces will be through the Jordan Bowl. 

Please note that as crews work to expand the resort, all uphill travel is prohibited. The current Uphill Route Status can be found at the bottom of the Mountain Report. Also, all construction areas remain off-limits. Thank you in advance for allowing our crews the space to safely expand our terrain and complete the last steps of exciting new projects.

As always, keep an eye here for the latest info on our lift and trail status, as well as any other operational updates.

Think cold!

- Team Snow

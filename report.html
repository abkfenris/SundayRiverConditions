Afternoon Report
Last Updated: January 24, 2024 | 1:18 PM

We had snow flurries off and on throughout the day today, resulting in up to 1" of snow accumulation. With comfortable temps and low winds, today was a great day to get in some midweek turns. 

Our snowmaking team has been working throughout the day, and they'll continue blowing snow into this evening. The snow guns will shut off early tonight with the rising temperatures, and they'll stay off for a couple of days until the warm snap ends. Our grooming team will clean up a similar lineup of trails as last night, with the exception of some advanced terrain such as Airglow, Tempest, Right Stuff, and Rogue Angel. 

We're officially midway into Senior Week, and we have a whole list of events for you to enjoy, including a comedy show, live music, and karaoke. Find all of the details on our Senior Week event page. Looking to step up your game and improve your skills? Lessons are available all week with our incredible Snowsports School instructors.  With White Heat opening soon, you'll want your skills as sharp as your edges. Our next Eddy & Betty Dinner is coming up, as well: hang out with your favorite Yetis this weekend and enjoy a host of fun activities. We also have a special deal on one-night stays at the Snowcap Inn, this weekend only. Check it out before it's gone.

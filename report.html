April 10, 2022

Calm skies and sunshine are reaching the peaks. Groomers put a great base on nearly 50 trails for you to ski at 8AM first chair. Temperatures are staying cooler this morning, and will steadily increase into the afternoon. Please check the lift and trail status, since winds have a slight chance of impacting operations today. As you shred over 300 open acres, be sure to get laps on South Ridge and North Peak, as today is the last day of the season for lifts servicing this terrain. For all our spring skiing updates, please check out the Spring Schedule. Enjoy a slopeside lunch between your runs at South Ridge, for our Passholder Appreciation Lunch. Stop by the Sunday River Sports End of Season Sale at South Ridge Lodge to experience some serious passholer discounts. Ski away your Sunday, as you wrap up another fantastic spring weekend!

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! All uphill access is closed for terrain from Aurora Peak, Oz, Jordan Bowl, and trails serviced by Little White Cap Quad. Beginner terrain is exclusively located within South Ridge and North Peak. We've had an outstanding season skiing across all 8 peaks, and can't wait to continue next 2022-23 season!

Please note Road Runner access to White Cap Basin will be open until tomorrow, April 11. Ski-in/ski-out access for the Grand Summit Hotel requires navigation of intermediate and advanced terrain. For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: March 29, 2024 | 3:01 PM

Today's soft snow meant peak spring skiing, just without the sun. The snow got softer as the day went on, and by this afternoon, the turns were lovely. The breeze did pick up this afternoon, and we're anticipating some higher sustained winds through tonight. Keep an eye on the lift and trail status below for the most updated info tomorrow morning.

Our grooming team will set out early tonight, and they'll be returning to their full grooming plan. All of the beginner terrain on South Ridge, Little White Cap, and North Peak will be groomed. Most of our intermediate and some of our advanced terrain will be groomed, as well. Check out trails like Vortex and Obsession for a more advanced carve, or head to Lollapalooza and Green Cheese for a relaxed cruise. White Heat will remain closed for Bust 'N' Burn tomorrow, and the competition can be viewed from the bottom of the trail. 

Bust 'N' Burn heats start tomorrow at 10:15AM, and you don't want to miss it. The competition will start with the Junior Division, then Open Division Qualifying Runs. Don't forget to swing by the Foggy Goggle after for the Awards Party and bracket reveal. Check out all of the details here. 

This Sunday, head to the top of North Peak for the Easter Sunday Sunrise Service with Sunday River Christian Ministry, and then head to South Ridge for an Easter egg hunt, open to all. We've got Spring Fest Weekend coming up, and don't forget to check out the details for our Solar Eclipse Viewing Party. 

Thinking about a season pass for next year? Buy now, ski now has officially started. Check out your season pass options here and carve it up for the rest of the season.

We'll see you out there!

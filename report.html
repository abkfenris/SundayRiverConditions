January 21, 2022

Blue skies and sun shines across 8 peaks this morning, with nearly 700 acres of skiable terrain! Come catch first chair at 9:00AM, and choose any of the 78 trails groomers put fresh corduroy on last night. If you're looking to stay slopeside, enjoy Twilight Skiing tonight until 6:30PM. We have a full weekend of exciting events including a big air lightshow, live music, a ski film for a great cause, and more. The most open terrain in the East awaits your skis!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl.

December 27, 2021

    We are starting off the week with newly opened terrain. Nearly 70 open trails now include groomed 3D, Express Lane, and Upper T72. Snowmakers are blasting guns on Lower Tempest and Excalibur today as we expand. Almost 400 acres of groomed terrain is ready to carve this morning, and shred until twilight! The Chondola and South Ridge Express will be spinning until 6:30PM to accommodate Twilight Skiing, continuing every night of Christmas Vacation Week. Thousands of twinkling lights lead you into a New Year at the Après Aglow Walk, conveniently located behind the Grand Summit Hotel. Tickets for both activities can be purchased online throughout the week. With lifts spinning into tonight, you'll have ample time to ski as many options as you desire!

    Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out accessible for your convenience. Skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently only accessible via blue terrain. Skiing into the Jordan Hotel is considered advanced terrain at this time. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at the moment.

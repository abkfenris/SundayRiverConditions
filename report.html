SATURDAY, APRIL 16

Happy Saturday! Mother Nature wasn't done with winter and rolled through yesterday with some April snow showers. Groomers reported up to 6" of new snow up top and it's been a fun day out there in a winter wonderland. This weekend, enjoy skiing or riding on terrain off of Barker and Spruce, with lifts spinning at 8AM. Keep it up until last chair at 4PM and continue bopping around in the fresh snow. We'll see you at first chair tomorrow. 

All skier services and food options are available at Barker Lodge. There is no ski-in/ski-out access at our hotels, so please make your way to the Barker area for access to the slopes. Conditions are for advanced skiers and riders only. Be aware of signage and markings on trails as coverage may be thin in areas. 

With operations open daily through April 24, there is still plenty of time to ski. View the entire Spring Schedule to learn more about terrain and resort services this spring. Our dining options will be changing weekly as we approach April 24, so don't forget to check in so that you can plan ahead. Plus, one-night stays are available at the Grand Summit and Jordan Hotel through the rest of the season.

Happy Passholder Appreciation Month! For the month of April, we're celebrating all our passholders. If you're looking to become or renew your pass, the 2021-22 New England Pass is back on sale, granting access to Sunday River, Loon & Sugarloaf all next year. Current passholders can take advantage of renewal rates and new passholders can start using their pass now when paid in full. With options that fit your budget and schedule, lock in the lowest rates on next year's pass now through April 30. 

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines. As a reminder, face coverings are required in all public indoor and outdoor areas at the resort.

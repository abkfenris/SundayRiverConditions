January 10, 2022

The grooming team is getting down to business tonight, adding South Paw and Wildfire to the mix for corduroy creation. We're expecting cold temps and a strong breeze again tomorrow, so be prepared with lots of layers, frequent breaks throughout the day, and having the Sunday River app handy to check the lift and trail status throughout the day, as wind at the higher elevations may cause delays. 

Through Thursday this week, the White Heat Quad and the Aurora Quad will not spin, but the terrain they service is open for uphill traffic with a valid lift ticket or season pass. Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails.

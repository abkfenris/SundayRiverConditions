Afternoon Report
Last Updated: April 14, 2024 | 12:30PM

It has turned into a beautiful day across the eight peaks. The sun is out, the skies are blue, and the vibes are great - this is what spring skiing is all about.

Today is the final day of the season for the Chondola, South Ridge Express, and White Heat Quad. Tomorrow, for Patriot's Day, terrain is scheduled to be open off of Barker 6, Spruce Peak Triple, and Jordan 8. It will also serve as the Barker 6 send-off, so get those extra laps in this afternoon and tomorrow while you can. For additional information for spring operations, please visit our Spring Schedule. 

DJ Will Weinbach, will be spinning the hits in Barker until 2PM. If you haven't made your way to South Ridge for the Passholder Appreciation Lunch, you still have time. The grills will shut down at 1PM.  

We will see you out there!

January 11, 2022

The benefit of these cold January Maine days is that it makes for beautiful corduroy. Today is just that. Perhaps start off with an extra cup of coffee and let the sun do its work before layering up and hitting the slopes. Speaking of corduroy, Wildfire and Southpaw saw the cats for the first time last night, pressing out beautiful lines top-to-bottom. If you need a warm-up, South Ridge and Barker base lodges are open. Or for something extra special, check out the new hot cocoa bar at Betty's Treats & Treasures next to the South Ridge base lodge until 4:30PM today. 

Through Thursday this week, the White Heat Quad and the Aurora Quad will not spin, but the terrain they service is open for uphill traffic with a valid lift ticket or season pass. Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails. 

Around the Resort

It's Kids' Week here at Sunday River, with deals on lodging, lift tickets, and lessons, plus fun events like Apres Aglow, fireworks, a fire dancer, and a magic show. Then, we're gearing up for a super fun Martin Luther King, Jr. Weekend on the mountain, with tap takeovers, fireworks, twilight skiing, and more. 

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

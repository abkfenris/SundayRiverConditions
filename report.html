Afternoon Report

Good afternoon everyone, and welcome to the conclusion of another fantastic Monday. Warm temps made for wonderful skiing throughout the day, and this great start to the week is only getting better. All indications are pointing to a sizeable storm impacting the area Wednesday night into Thursday, and there are even whispers of a second storm following close on it's heals. If that's not enough reason to call out sick with some powder fever, we don't know what is.

The Mountain Ops team isn't taking it easy, despite the forecast. Snowmaking will be back online tonight, as we continue to work on our trails. Grooming will also be back out, re-striping the hill for your shredding pleasure. 

The storm looks like it is coming in Wednesday night, which is a great reason to take advantage of our one-night stays now available for Presidents' Week. All properties, all week; grab yours before they're gone! 

We'll see you out there!

Morning Report
Last Updated: January 12, 2024 | 7:47 AM

Temperatures today will be in the high twenties to low thirties before dropping down into the teens tonight. We'll start the day with partly cloudy skies, but it sounds like the sun will be out by mid-morning. We have over 350 acres of open terrain today, twilight skiing tonight, and snow expected tomorrow. Sounds like a great way to dive into the weekend. 

Our snowmaking team ran our system to full capacity last night, and they'll continue making snow throughout the day. Who Ville will open this afternoon, and we're expecting around 16 features in the park.  

This weekend is Martin Luther King Jr. Weekend, and we have fun for the whole family. We have fireworks, fire dancing, Twilight Skiing, Sips & Sweets, live music, the Après Aglow Lighted Trail, and more. The Alpeniglu at Sunday River has a new après experience, as well; take a cabin cat down from Sliders, enjoy some drinks, sweets, and music, then take a cabin cat back up.

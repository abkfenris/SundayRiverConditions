Early Summer Update

We're well into June, and with that, our teams have been hard at work to prep for our slate of summer activities. Sunday River Outfitters has kicked off their season with kayaking, paddleboarding, archery, and new this season, axe throwing! Sunday River Golf Club has been open for nearly a month now along with the Clubhouse for meals, drinks, and any apparel needs in the Pro Shop. Stay and Play Packages are up and running as well. Book a tee time and you're automatically qualified to save up to 15% on lodging. 

Coming up next we have scenic lift rides kicking off on June 30. You can view our updated summer trail map here. Please take note of the closed areas around the mountain. Even with some closed zones, there's still plenty of hiking around the resort. We've added Trail L in the White Cap area that can turn your journey up to Locke into a nice loop. 

On the events side of things, first up is the New England Forrest Rally on July 14-15 and Tough Mountain Challenge and Mini Mountain Challenge July 22 and 23. TMC tickets go offsale June 22 and can be purchased here.

It may not be winter, but there's still plenty to do up here! Share your favorite summer moments with us on our Happy Place Found page and soak up that summer sun!

-Team Snow

Hiking Trail Conditions

With the amount of recent and forecasted rain, as well as construction in multiple zones, there are some updates to the hiking trails.

Hiking trails currently open: I, J, D, E, A, H, K, and B. Trails C, M, O, and L are open, but need a little TLC.

Hiking trails currently closed: G

As you are hiking, please be cautious of wet slabs and erosion in steeper sections. Trails are quite muddy due to all the rain, but once we get a break in the weather, things will dry out.

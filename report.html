TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 16

Even though we didn't get the powder day we hoped for, it was still an awesome day kicking up sugary snow. With 1-2" on the ground, we can't complain. Plus, we have another storm heading our way in just a couple of days. Remember, it's never too early to start snow dancing. Until then, we will enjoy the sunshine and sweet corduroy tomorrow. Tonight, the groomers will hit the slopes to press in the new snow on all eight peaks. Get here early to slash up the morning stripes or make your way through the glades. If you're just learning, we suggest parking at South Ridge for access to the best beginner terrain. 

Lifts open at 8AM every day this week. Twilight skiing will continue tonight through Saturday for Presidents’ Week with access to the Chondola and South Ridge Express until 6:30PM. As you plan your trip, keep in mind lift tickets are limited and days are close to selling out, so plan ahead if you want to hit the slopes this week. Or book lodging with us and guarantee access to full-day lift tickets, including one-night stays through Thursday, February 18, at the Jordan Hotel and Snow Cap Inn. Please note that kid's ski school lessons are now available to book online with limited availability.

We require online lift ticket purchase and set limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines.

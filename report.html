Afternoon Report
Last Updated: December 7, 2023 | 1:04 PM

With blue skies and little wind, today was an excellent day for some midweek turns. Temps in the mid-twenties meant some fast snow out there on the hill, and we sure had fun carving up that corduroy. The sun was shining down on us by this afternoon, and we can expect some similar weather tomorrow.  

Our Mountain Ops team remains hard at work getting the mountain ready for us every day. Our snowmakers will be busy throughout the night, and we're planning for some more terrain expansion by this weekend. The grooming team will be follwoing behind, laying stripes on most of our open terrain for tomorrow morning.

Join us for celebrations starting Saturday at 8:45AM, with the first chair heading up at 9AM. Donny Pelletier will be out on the slopes on Saturday, so keep an eye out for Maine's finest athlete.

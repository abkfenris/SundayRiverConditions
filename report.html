Summer Update

Congratulations to all the fearless competitors who conquered the 13th Tough Mountain Challenge! You've braved the dirtiest challenge and emerged victorious. If you had a blast and are already looking forward to the next adrenaline-pumping event, we've got great news for you! Sign-ups for next year's Tough Mountain Challenge are now live. Don't miss your chance to secure a spot and challenge yourself again.

If you're eager to continue exploring the great outdoors, take a hike up one of our hiking trails or enjoy a scenic lift ride on North Peak. Tickets are available online or at the Welcome Center for purchase. Also, brand new for this summer, check out Axe Throwing at The Patio Bar at the Grand Summit Hotel.

Don’t forget to visit Sunday River Sports to take a look at the retail sale going on. We have select winter outwear for 40% off and skis, snowboards, and boots for 30% off. 

Looking ahead, we've got a full slate of fall events that promise excitement and enjoyment. Join us for River Fest, a series of fun-filled weekends in Western Maine, featuring Maine Brew Fest, Maine Artisan Fest, Mahoosuc Adventure Fest, and Fall Fest. Indulge in craft brews, sample cheeses, and even sign up to take a kaleidoscope class! Visit our website for more information and where you can buy tickets.

Although winter is still months away, there's no shortage of activities here. Share your favorite summer moments with us on our Happy Place Found page and soak up the glorious summer sun while it’s out!

Hope you're all enjoying this sunny and warm winter day today! The snow is skiing beautifully and there's barely a cloud in the sky. Lifts will keep spinning till 4PM, so come join us if you haven't yet! This afternoon, we've officially hit over 800 acres of open terrain. Tonight, groomers will lay down stripes on over 80 trails, giving you lots of corduroy to carve on. And it looks like Mother Nature is bringing us a refresh Thursday night into Friday. Around the Resort

Look forward to fun events this weekend, including a Halloween-themed party!

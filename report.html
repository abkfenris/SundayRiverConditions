Take tree skiing turns! Mother Nature keeps delivering this afternoon, as we enjoy 12-16" already on the slopes since last night. Our glades are a winter wonderland, with flurries falling as we kick up clouds of snow. Nearly 100 acres have been added to our list of open trails since first chair, including Tightwire, Tin Woodsman, Flyin' Monkey, Rocking Chair, Northern Exposure, and more. Around the Resort

OutRyders is partnering with Sunday River to bring you Pride Weekend, this February 4-6! New England's largest LGBTQ+ ski and snowboard club is all about great friends, mountain memories, social activities, and great deals. Look forward to fun events this weekend, including a Halloween-themed party Saturday night! Plus, give telemark skiing a try with the FreeHeeler's Demo Tour on Sunday.

The Après Aglow lighted walk is open this weekend for you to enjoy thousands of twinkling lights while sipping on delicious hot cocoa. You can also treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, open daily for you to indulge in delicious sweets. Find the perfect souvenir, from Sunday River logowear to mugs and more, so that you can fondly remember your moments on the mountain.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments!

Event Update 9:35AM: Red Bull Winer Vibes will keep the party going until 7PM tonight.

Temps warmed up quite a bit throughout the day, and we're sitting solidly in the mid-twenties. The blue sky is making an appearance, and the sun is just peeking out. The day isn't over yet, though, and there are still plenty of turns to make. 

Snowmaking will be off tonight, and the team will be back Tuesday night. Presidents' Week is here, and we have a whole host of events. Red Bull Winter Vibes will go from 12-7PM in South Ridge today, so swing by to listen to a live DJ, try Red Bull-infused cocktails, and check out the photo booth.  For some more live music tonight, listen to the Jim Brady from 2-5PM at Shipyard. We have twilight skiing every night this week, so stick around and ski under the lights until 6:30PM. After that, head over to the Grand Summit Hotel to check out the Après Aglow Lighted Trail from 4:30-8:30PM. Still deciding on your vacation plans? We have single-night stays at all resort properties this weekend through Presidents' Week, so be sure to check that out.

We'll see you out there!

Morning Report
Last Updated: December 1, 2023 | 7:36 AM

Welcome to the first day of December. To kick off the new month, Aurora will be joining the line-up for the weekend, with more terrain opening tomorrow. In case you haven't heard, the South Ridge Base area, including the Chondola and the South Ridge Express, will debut for the season tomorrow, December 2. Thanks to our Mountain Ops team, we'll have over 50 acres coming online, in addition to the two new lifts. 

For today, the grooming team was busy re-striping all open terrain, with Northern Lights receiving top-to-bottom corduroy for the first time this season. Until tomorrow, please be aware that there is no terrain connecting Jordan Bowl and Locke Mountain and you will need to pick your peak. With the ongoing Barker 6 construction, parking will be very limited at Barker.

In addition to new terrain coming online tomorrow, Demo Day will be Slopeside at South Ridge from 8AM-2PM. Demo the hottest gear from brands like Blizzard, Stockli, Atomic, and more. 

Lastly, be sure to keep December 9th open on your calendar. It's finally here - the Grand Opening of the Barker 6. For more information on the celebration, visit the Barker 6 Grand Opening page.

As always, keep an eye here for the latest info on our lift and trail status, as well as any other operational updates.

Think cold!

Morning Report
Last Updated: April 6, 2024 | 6:30 AM

Okay, say it with me, “April snow brings more…” fun! See, you get it! This morning, we received another inch of fresh powder up top, bringing our snow total to 15 inches in the last three days.

If you’re looking to finish the season strong or try something new to look forward to the next, book a lesson online or in person and receive a 15% discount.

As a reminder, all new New England Passes automatically qualify for immediate access all Spring long, so buy now, ski now, and maximize the value of your pass now!

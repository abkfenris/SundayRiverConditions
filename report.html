February 6, 2022

Another bluebird in the books! Ski any trail this afternoon since all of our terrain is now 100% open. Choose from 135 stunning glades and trails covered in 18” of powder from the recent storm. This morning we enjoyed fresh corduroy made from almost 3 feet of snow we received in the past week. Groomers are putting down stripes tonight on trails including White Heat, Vortex, and 3D. If you’re looking for some natural terrain, we have nearly 2,000 acres for you to explore with our boundary-to-boundary skiing. Catch first chair at 9AM tomorrow morning to get a head start on tree skiing turns. Please keep an eye on the lift and trail status as we may experience some winds this afternoon, due to the snowy system leaving town. The best way to spend your Sunday is on the slopes!

Around the Resort

OutRyders is partnering with Sunday River to bring you Pride Weekend going on now. New England's largest LGBTQ+ ski and snowboard club is all about great friends, mountain memories, social activities, and great deals.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. You can also treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures with delicious candy, or shop our exclusive logowear collection.

Morning Report
Last Updated: March 31, 2024 | 6:35 AM

With mostly sunny skies and temperatures in the mid-forties, today will feel like spring skiing for sure. The wind is still blowing a little bit, but it's lower than yesterday, and right now lifts are scheduled to spin as normal. The snow will be fast this morning, but it should soften up throughout the morning as the sun comes out. Catch some soft snow and some sunshine later this morning and watch the Bust 'N' Burn Dual Mogul Championships, starting at 10:45AM. Stay for the on-hill viewing party, and then stick around for the Awards ceremony and celebrate your favorite athletes.  Check out all of the details here. 

Our grooming team took care of 78 trails last night, including all of our beginner terrain, most of our intermediate terrain, and some of our advanced terrain. Check out trails like Cyclone and Borealis for some relaxed turns, or try out trails like Tempest and Obsession for some more advanced carves. Looking to try your hand at some natural terrain? Head to 3D or Shock Wave for some all-natural bumps. 

 Looking ahead, we've got Spring Fest Weekend coming up, and don't forget to check out the details for our Solar Eclipse Viewing Party. Head to the deck of the North Peak Lodge for a chance to hold a piece of the moon, courtesy of the Maine Mineral & gem Museum, while you watch the eclipse.

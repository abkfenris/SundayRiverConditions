December 10, 2021

Snow is falling on the slopes at Sunday River this morning! Corduroy galore, our open terrain on Locke, Barker, Spruce, and South Ridge is groomed, with even more coming soon. We look forward to opening even more trails for the season soon, including Jibe, Lower Starlight, Road Runner, Snowbound, Sirius, Aludra, and Lights Out. Catch the first chair to fresh snow, with lifts spinning from 9AM-3:45PM today!

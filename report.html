Today, we're looking at temps in the high twenties to low thirties and cloudy - but dry - skies. We have over 350 acres of terrain for your skiing and riding pleasure, with 11 open lifts. It should be firmer out there this morning, ideally softening up as temps warm up and the sun peaks out over the next few hours.

For even more adventure, check out the whalebacks on Tempest.

Take a cabin snowcat down from Sliders, and enjoy some drinks, sweets, and music before taking the cabin cat back up.

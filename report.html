Afternoon Report

What a day! Pond-a-palooza was a splash this morning and the groovy tunes from our second Spring Apres Concert are now filling the air in South Ridge Basin. Crying' Out Loud is on stage until 3:30PM and Under the Covers is on deck for their set from 4-6PM. Take a break between sets to get some sunny laps in. The snow has softened up nicely this afternoon and there's not a cloud in the sky. We hope to see you tomorrow for some Easter activities and the Passholder Appreciation lunch. See you out there!

Mountain Notes:

If you're looking to get over to Jordan from South Ridge, ski on over to Spruce Peak Triple and take Sirius > Aludra > Cyclone > State Fair. Kansas will bring you back from Jordan to South Ridge when you're ready to head back East.    

Spring brings with it some of the best conditions of the year, but also the most variable condition. Please note that trail conditions may vary and change rapidly throughout the day.

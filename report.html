WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 16

We've been blessed with a classic bluebird day today. The sun has been shining all day, warming us up and providing that sweet Vitamin D. The groomers have been sweet, and we have a strong base throughout our glades for some fun tree skiing. If you haven't made it up to the mountain yet this week, this afternoon wouldn't be a bad time to start! A reminder that there may be some snow coming our way later this week, so keep on snow dancing. Twilight skiing is open until 6:30 tonight, so stick around for some skiing under the lights. Join us afterward at South Ridge for two Fire Dancer shows at 6:15 and 6:45PM, followed by a spectacular fireworks display at 7PM. In case you're going to bed early tonight, a reminder that lifts open at 8AM tomorrow, and first chair is the early bird's reward. 

Twilight skiing will continue tonight through Saturday for Presidents’ Week with access to the Chondola and South Ridge Express until 6:30PM. As you plan your trip, keep in mind lift tickets are limited and days are close to selling out, so plan ahead if you want to hit the slopes this week. Or book lodging with us and guarantee access to full-day lift tickets, including one-night stays through Thursday, February 18, and then Saturday, February 20, at the Jordan Hotel and Snow Cap Inn. Please note that kid's ski school lessons are now available to book online with limited availability. 

We require online lift ticket purchase and set limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines.

See you out there!

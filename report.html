January 12, 2022

We all owe Mother Nature a huge thank you for finally allowing the temperatures to hit double digits. The weather is looking like it will stay consistent overnight which will allow for some snowmaking on White Cap and Barker Mountain. The groomers are coming out in full force tonight too, so you all have some fresh corduroy to shred tomorrow morning. Every day we will continue to expand available terrain to make sure everyone can find their happy place.

The White Heat Quad and the Aurora Quad will not spin tomorrow but will be back on Friday. The terrain they service remains open for uphill traffic with a valid lift ticket or season pass. Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails. 

Around the Resort

It's Kids' Week here at Sunday River, with deals on lodging, lift tickets, and lessons, plus fun events like Apres Aglow, fireworks, a fire dancer, and a magic show. Then, we're gearing up for a super fun Martin Luther King, Jr. Weekend on the mountain, with tap takeovers, fireworks, twilight skiing, and more. Need to get away? One-night stays just opened up for this Saturday night at the Snow Cap Inn and Jordan Hotel.

Looking ahead, Wicked Air & Après is returning to Barker on January 22. This must-see event combines big air and 3D project lights into one show. 

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

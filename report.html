December 6, 2021

It’s beginning to look a lot like December, so bring your Gore-Tex for some mixed wintery weather! Lock Mountain Triple, Barker Mountain Express, South Ridge Express, and Spruce Peak Triple are all spinning from 9AM-3:45PM today. The Chondola and its terrain may have a delayed open this morning, so check back here for the latest as we keep you posted. We have a plethora of dinning options now open for your convenience, from Camp to Foggy Goggle!

All lift tickets need to be purchased online in advance. Have your RFID from last year? You can head straight to the lift! Skier services are available in both the Barker and South Ridge Lodges, as well as shuttle service between these lodges and the hotels while the lifts spin. We are now accepting cashless forms of payment only. Please be sure to have a gift card, resort charge, credit card, or debit card available for on-site purchases.

Around the Resort

The South Ridge Food Court is now open, along with the Barker Food Court, Waffle Cabin in Barker Basin, Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel, Camp at the Grand Summit Hotel, and Shipyard Brew Haus at White Cap Lodge. Foggy Goggle is now open for the season! Plus, get your retail therapy in at Sunday River Sports, and your relaxation on at the Jordan Spa. Please note that the Tune Shop is closed this weekend, but you can pick up skis at the repair counter in Sunday River Sports.

For the latest updates whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

COVID-19 Protocols

Though we are no longer requiring guests to wear masks in general areas, we suggest masking indoors. However, some services may require you to wear a mask, so be sure to have one on hand. Please make sure to read through our COVID Winter Guide before visiting the resort to understand our operating plans and how they may affect you. Remember, we're all in this together.

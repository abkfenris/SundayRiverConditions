March 12, 2022

Incoming snow on the slopes! Mother Nature is ready to let the flurries fly on nearly 700 acres of open terrain this morning. Forecasts are calling for a range of totals, and we could see up to 6-14" on our peaks as we ski into tonight. Catch first chair at 8AM to carve into more than 80 corduroy trails before fresh flakes cover the terrain. Then take powdery turns across 8 peaks until 4PM, and head over to North Peak for Twilight Skiing until 6:30PM as the snow falls. Please note as the snow picks up later today, winds may impact lift operations this afternoon going into tonight. Since winds are a possibility, we've removed the cabins off the Chondola for the weekend. We will keep you updated on all the best options to take your runs, via the lift and trail status. 

Our excellent lineup of events is here, and we couldn't be more excited. Come shred with the pros today at the Red Bull Slide-In Tour! Enjoy an epic day including a free ride clinic, crew show, and Red Bull afterparty. In addition to this event we have live music at Barker Bar and the Foggy Goggle, fireworks in the South Ridge skies, an enchanting walk in the forest, and much more. Get ready for a great slopeside Saturday!

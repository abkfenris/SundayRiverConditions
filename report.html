MONDAY, FEBRUARY 15

We need your help. The first of two storms rolls in this evening and forecasts are calling for 5-9" of new snow before the storm heads out tomorrow afternoon. Then we're looking at beautiful sunshine for stash hunting midweek, followed by another storm Thursday night into Friday. So here is where you come in: whether it's wearing your pajamas inside out, snowdancing your buns off, or praying to Ullr, we need you to act on your superstition of choice. This week has the potential for up to 2 feet of snow before the weekend, so let's get those PJ's flipped, dance moves busted, and snow gods smiling. 

Lifts open at 8AM every day this week. Tonight the groomers will get out there before the snow finishes and plan on taking another lap around South Ridge and North Peak. With new snow falling during the day, beginner terrain may appear ungroomed as the snow accumulates over time. Twilight skiing continues through Saturday for Presidents’ Week with access to the Chondola and South Ridge Express until 6:30PM. As you plan your trip, keep in mind lift tickets are limited and days are close to selling out, so plan ahead if you want to hit the slopes this week. Or book lodging with us and guarantee access to full-day lift tickets, including one-night stays through Thursday, February 18, at the Jordan Hotel and Snow Cap Inn. Please note that kids ski school lessons are now available to book online with limited availability.

We require online lift ticket purchase and set limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines.

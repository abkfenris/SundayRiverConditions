December 25, 2021

What is better than Christmas? Christmas with corduroy. Options await you with almost 60 groomed trails and over 350 open acres of terrain for you to carve today. We filled the slopes with piles of snow last night for even more trails opening soon, and have active snowmaking on White Heat this morning. White Heat Quad and North Peak Quad opened recently for the season, and are scheduled to spin with 10 other lifts from 8AM to 3:45PM. If you prefer celebrating your holidays under the stars, we have Twilight Skiing and the Après Aglow Walk both opening for the week tomorrow, tickets being sold online now. Even more Christmas Week festivities can be found here.

Since Jordan Bowl is open, both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out access for your convenience. Skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently only accessible via blue terrain. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. Come ski in a winter wonderland!

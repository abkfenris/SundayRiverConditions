SATURDAY, FEBRUARY 13

Yesterday we were enjoying endless mountain views, 80+ beautiful groomers, and were still exploring in the glades. The best part? We get to do it all over again today. Kick off Presidents' Week by hopping on first chair at 8AM and making your way across all eight peaks. Our groomers hit the slopes overnight on their normal routes, but added some stripes on Quantum Leap and Black Hole. For some fast carving, head to trails like Risky Business, Right Stuff, and Rogue Angel. If you're looking to bop through the trees, head over to Jordan Bowl. For those who are just learning, stick to the terrain on South Ridge and off of the Little White Cap Quad. There's a lot to enjoy out there and you can continue to ski or ride tonight until 6:30PM with Twilight Skiing. End your night on the slopes with a fireworks show at 7PM at South Ridge. 

We have quite the line-up for Presidents’ Week with twilight skiing every night, fireworks, and Valentine’s Day specials. Plus, let’s not forget the best part of all, that we are 100% open with fantastic conditions. As you plan your trip please keep in mind lift tickets are limited and days are close to selling out, so plan ahead if you want to hit the slopes next week. Or book lodging with us and guarantee access to full-day lift tickets. 

Please note that kids ski school lessons are now available to book online with limited availability.

We are requiring online lift ticket purchase and setting limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, half-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Morning Report

Happy Monday and happy Presidents' Day to all of our skiers and riders! We've got a great day on tap with some mild temps as the day progresses and some freshly groomed trails. Right Stuff, White Heat, and Rogue Angel got some snowmaking attention the last couple of nights and all that new snow has been groomed in and should make for a great surface. Most ungroomed and glade runs will remain closed, but patrol will be monitoring their conditions, and fingers crossed that the warmer temps will soften things up and maybe open a few more areas.

The weather looks, you guessed it, warm. Temps should reach mid 30's under a layer of clouds that will hopefully burn off. Could be a great day to hang on the Foggy Goggle deck with a beer and some nachos.

Other than that, Rocking Chair will remain closed until March 6. We have cats actively grooming the slope for a ski and snowboard cross event, and the trail will not be open until after the event. 

Remember to check out everything we have happening this week for Presidents' Week. From Twilight Skiing all week to bands, fireworks, and live entertainment, there is always something going on during the holidays here at Sunday River.

We are seeing some promising signs of snowfall at some point later in the week, which is a great reason to take advantage of our one-night stays now available for Presidents' Week. All properties, all week; grab yours before they're gone! 

We'll see you out there!

-Team Snow

Boy oh boy, it's a fast one out there. Hope you've got your carving skis on, because these conditions are perfect for laying into those edges. The sun is shining, temperatures warmed up some, and the breeze from this morning is dying down. What a way to wind down the day.

Tonight, our snowmaking team will continue resurfacing terrain. They're planning on making snow on trails such as Tourist Trap, Lollapalooza, and Wildifre, and we can expect some snow guns to stay online through tomorrow.

December 22, 2021

The snow continues to fall and we are happy. As of right now there is 3" of freshies up at the top.  Snowmaking will start back up later this evening on T72, White Cap and many more. Our grooming team is planning on working throughout the night to make sure tomorrow will be great.Whether you're skiing, snowboarding or using a Sno-Go just make sure to have yourself some fun out there. After you get off the slopes head on over to the Summit Hotel and walk through the magical Après Aglow. 

    Since Jordan Bowl is open, both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out access for your convenience. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. With this in mind, skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently accessible via blue terrain. Rental snowboards are now back in stock. Come enjoy all the festivities slopeside!

Morning Report
Last Updated: March 3, 2024 | 6:45 AM

Yesterday was fun out on the slopes, and we have no reason to think that today won't be any different. The snow will soften throughout the morning with temps possibly reaching into the 50s by the afternoon. The sun should make its appearance throughout the day making for a great Sunday on snow!

Despite some unsettled weather moving through the area last night, our grooming team prepared the mountain in great shape for today. They worked on the new snow on Lollapalooza, Amex, and Boots On/Off. In the early morning, grooming continued with several passes on green and blue terrain across the eight peaks.

If you haven't reserved a lesson with the SnowSports School yet this year, take a walk over and talk with the team. Lessons are a great tool for everyone, at all ability levels, to sharpen skills and learn something new. 

As always, check out the events calendar for the lineup of activities and performances throughout the rest of the month. From the DJ series, to dinner with Eddy and Betty, to Bust 'N' Burn, there's something for everyone! 

We'll see you out there!

- Team Snow

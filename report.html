April 3, 2022

What a fantastic morning on the mountain! Blue skies and sunshine have warmed up the slopes as we ski nearly 400 acres of open terrain. Groomers are adding a fresh corduroy base to more than 60 trails for you to shred at 9AM first chair tomorrow. Light and refreshing flurries are forecasted to dust the peaks overnight. In the days ahead, Mother Nature is keeping the trails cool at night and warming slightly during the day so we can stay slopeside. Enjoy the festivities today, from live music to great bites and brews, for the last day of Spring Fest Weekend. Look forward to a new week full of warm temperatures and spring turns!

For the best ways to get in your runs, check out the Sunday River Spring Skiing Schedule. Please note access to other peaks from the Grand Summit Hotel requires navigation of intermediate and advanced terrain. Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. All uphill access to Jordan Bowl trails is closed. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

Around the Resort

Use your 2022-23 New England Pass today, with the convenience of our new payment plan options and expanded perks!

We are bringing the beach to the slopes this weekend! Come celebrate the return of spring at Spring Festival complete with parties, music, and great spring skiing and riding.

Registration is free, but in order to secure a spot, head to the Welcome Center at South Ridge Lodge to sign the dotted line and get your bib number.

The second Spring Après Concert Series is hitting the slopes April 9! Enjoy a free slopeside show after a day full of spring skiing and pond skimming.

One-night stays are now available at the Snow Cap Inn. Join us for convenient, budget-friendly lodging options.

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, featuring delicious sweets and special souvenirs from our exclusive logowear collection. Check out our many retail options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Unwind and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. Our spa is paired with a boutique, where you can find beauty and wellness products, athletic wear, and more. You can find even more retail options across the resort!

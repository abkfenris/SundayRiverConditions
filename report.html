Today, we’re looking at temperatures in the mid-teens up top and low twenties at the base, with mostly sunny skies. We’re starting this lovely Sunday with 716 acres of skiable terrain and 17 lifts scheduled. We have some wind in the forecast this morning, so be sure to check back here for the most updated lift and trail status.

Our snowmaking team made snow on Upper Sunday Punch and Lower American Express last night, and they’ll keep blowing snow on Obsession and Snowbound. Please be aware that Upper Sunday Punch and American Express will have active snowmaking early this morning, and both trails will remain ungroomed with whalebacks.  Our grooming team pushed out Jungle Road last night, laid corduroy on Cascades, and gave Three Mile Trail another pass, in addition to the usual lineup of groomed terrain. 

On Saturday evenings through the rest of the season, check out our new après experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River. Starting at 5:30PM, take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down to the igloo from the Sliders deck, and enjoy some sips, sweets, and music. Then take the Cabin Cat back up to Sliders. Tickets are on sale now.

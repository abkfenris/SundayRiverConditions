Afternoon Report
Last Updated: December 6, 2023 | 2:10 PM

It doesn't really get much better than today. The sun has been shining, temps have come up into the comfortable mid-20s, and the snow is skiing like it's March. Pinch us, we must be dreaming. Luckily, we get to do it all over again tomorrow. 

For this evening, our snowmakers will continue their push towards expansion, taking advantage of yet another night of cold. All eyes are set on expansion, and we're ready for a big bump in terrain once Barker opens this weekend. The grooming team will also be out on the hill, making more of that magical corduroy fresh for the morning.

Looking ahead, Saturday is the grand opening of the Barker 6. Celebrations kick off at 8:45AM, with the first chair heading uphill at 9AM. Donny Pelletier will be in town, and rumor has it will be lapping the new chair looking for people better than him. Probably won't find any though. All of the details, including how you can win a seat on the first chair, can be found on the Barker 6 Grand Opening page.

Don’t forget to take a look at our Christmas Vacation Week plans. We’ve got a whole host of events, great deals on lodging and lift tickets, and of course, the most dependable snow in New England. Keep an eye out for gift ideas at the Sunday River Sports flagship store, as well.

As always, keep an eye here for the latest info on our lift and trail status and any other operational updates.

Think cold!

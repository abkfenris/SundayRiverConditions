Morning Update

Good morning Sunday River riders and rippers; mother nature is testing our team once again. Yesterday's snowfall did not stop, and the snow kept coming down until late into the night. At around 2 AM, a warm front came in, bringing unsettling weather. These warm temps and scattered light showers will continue on and off throughout the day. The 4-6" of snow we received in the last 24 hours will help protect the rest of the snowpack, and should get us through today with softer playful trail conditions.

Our Mountain ops team got to it early in the night to groom out all open terrain on the resort besides Lost Princess. Keep an eye on the mountain report today as ski patrol makes their way onto the hill to inspect trail conditions,  drop ropes and open new trails.

Best to bring the extra waterproof gear today if you want to get out there and make the most of the freshly fallen snow, But a couple of trash bag ponchos will do just fine. One night stays are now available for MLK Weekend at the Grand Summit and Snow Cap Inn.  Availability is limited. 

If your staying around for the week with your little rippers make sure to head to Laser Tag at the Ballroom at Grand Summit Hotel tonight from  5-7PM!

Please note: There is currently no beginner terrain into and out of the Grand Summit Hotel.

See you on the slopes!

-Team Snow

Morning Report

Good morning all. It's a busy week, and it's about to get even busier. While we still have all the fun of Presidents' Week events to keep us busy, the real story this week is the snow. We're expecting 1-3" tonight, with more on the way tomorrow night into Friday. When all's said and done, we're looking at the potential for over a foot of snow in the next 4 days. 

For today, however, we're looking at another beautiful day of sunshine and warm temps. The grooming team worked their magic last night, resurfacing all our open, groomed terrain. In addition, the snowmakers were out and about last night, putting down fresh snow on trails like American Express and Sunday Punch. Snowmaking will be coming offline as temps rise, but be aware that active snowmaking may be present this morning.

Looking ahead, the big storm is coming in Wednesday night, which is a great reason to take advantage of our one-night stays now available for Presidents' Week. All properties, all week; grab yours before they're gone! 

We'll see you out there!

-Team Snow

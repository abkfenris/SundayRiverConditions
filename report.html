Afternoon Report
Last Updated: April 10, 2024 | 2:30 PM

Another spring day of skiing and riding is coming to an end. The trails softened as the day went on, and clouds stayed around most of the day. Snow groomers will take us to the slopes tonight to clean up the trails for another day ahead.
Looking into the forecast, we see some warm temps, cloudy skies, and a chance for some not-so-nice weather as tomorrow continues.

Looking ahead, we've got a weekend full of classic Spring events. Pond-a-palooza will be taking place on Saturday, with the Great Tailgate and Passholder Appreciation lunch on Sunday! 

Please be cautious while skiing into the South Ridge basin. Active construction is going on in preparation for POND-A-PALOOZA. Please ski towards our Surface Lifts and around the South Ridge Express to access the Chondola entrance.

Spring operations schedule:

Midweek operations will only consist of Jordan, Barker, the Chondola, and Spruce, which means we no longer have true beginner terrain available. Our full Spring operations schedule can be found here.

January 16, 2022

Sunshine, blue skies, and double-digit temperatures by the afternoon make for Sunday Funday on the slopes this long weekend. Grab first chair at 8AM to enjoy fresh corduroy from White Heat to Lollapalooza. Trust us, you won't want to miss this corker of a day on the mountain. Shimmy into an extra layer or two, slap on some SPF, and cruise on 75 groomed trails and 469 acres of skiable terrain. Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl. 

Now, tomorrow is going to look a lot different. We don't want to jinx it, but there's a foot of snow in the forecast. Get jiggy with it and keep doing those snow dances. We could all use a powder day!

Around the Resort

Don't miss 100,000 lights in the forest tonight at Après Aglow. Tickets are available for this half-mile trail at the Grand Summit Hotel from 4:30-8:30PM. 

We've got some fun events coming up! Wicked Air & Après is returning to Barker on January 22. This must-see event combines big air and 3D project lights into one show. 

Senior Week is January 24-28 - lots of deals for the over-65 club, and plenty of fun events and ski meet-ups to join. 

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

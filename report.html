Summer Update

It's time to conquer the 13th Tough Mountain Challenge! This might just be the dirtiest challenge to date. If you haven't signed up yet and fear you're missing out on the fun, don't worry! Same-day signup is available in the morning, so you can still join in on the epic adventure.

After you finish racing, don't forget to visit Sunday River Sports for more Tough Mountain gear and to take a look at the retail sale going on. We have select winter outwear for 40% off and skis, snowboards, and boots for 30% off. 

For those who are interested in Barker construction updates, we have a viewing area up Broadway, just past obstacle 18 on the course. For your safety, and others around you, please keep in mind that the Barker basin, and surrounding trails,  are an active construction site. We ask that all guests please respect and follow the safety signs in the area. 

While you're here, take a scenic lift ride on North Peak. Tickets are available online or at the Welcome Center for purchase. Also, brand new for this summer, check out Axe Throwing at The Patio Bar at the Grand Summit Hotel.

Looking ahead, we've got a full slate of fall events that promise excitement and enjoyment. Join us for River Fest, a series of fun-filled weekends in Western Maine, featuring Maine Brew Fest, Maine Artisan Fest, Mahoosuc Adventure Fest, and Fall Fest. Indulge in craft brews, sample cheeses, and even sign up to take a kaleidoscope class! Visit our website for more information and where you can buy tickets.

Although winter is still months away, there's no shortage of activities here. Share your favorite summer moments with us on our Happy Place Found page and soak up the glorious summer sun while it’s out!

January 23, 2022

We carved into Sunday morning on the slopes! Snow guns are now blasting on Escapade after making snow on 3D and Lazy River all weekend. Groomers are working across 8 peaks tonight, putting stripes on trails including Paradigm, and Lazy River where snowmakers made piles and piles of snow. Lifts are spinning until 3:45PM this afternoon to end your day with some great turns, and you can catch first chair at 9AM tomorrow morning. Look forward to forecasted flurries early this week, and a following snowy system in sight. We have lots of trails to choose from on the most open terrain in the East!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl. 

Around the Resort

Senior Week starts tomorrow, January 24-28! Look forward to lots of deals for the over-65 club, and plenty of fun events and ski meet-ups to join.

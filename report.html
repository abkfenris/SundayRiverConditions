March 1, 2022

Take Tuesday turns on stunning stripes! Groomers pushed out all that freshly made snow on Ecstasy last night, and replaced it with beautiful corduroy for you to carve this morning. Catch 9AM first chair and enjoy more than 80 groomed trails, or ski plentiful powder stashes. Snowguns blasted all night on trails including Obsession, Tourist Trap, Cascades, and Lower Starlight for you to shred them all-natural today. Choose from 115 glades and trails on 727 open acres this morning. Looking ahead, we are forecasted for light, refreshing flurries as we ski into the weekend. As we enter a new month, check out our long list of exciting events, and Après & Nightlife, celebrating everything we love about life on the slopes. Start March on the mountain at your Happy Place, skiing across 8 peaks!

Around the Resort

Check out the Red Bull Slide-In Tour March 12 to celebrate snowboard culture in the East! The pros will be offering a progression clinic at Who-Ville terrain park, a free ride session, and an epic crew show. You can also join us for some post-riding brews and Red Bull at the official afterparty, hosted by the Foggy Goggle.

Sign-up for the Master of the Mountain Challenge, coming to the slopes March 12! Participants are asked to ski to a series of trails on Sunday River’s slopes, aiming for a controlled-but-aggressive pace. The goal is to come as close to the pre-determined target time as possible. Only one winner will be crowned the Master of the Mountain!

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, featuring delicious sweets and special souvenirs from our exclusive logowear collection. Check out our many retail options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Unwind and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. Our spa is paired with a boutique, where you can find beauty and wellness products, athletic wear, and more.

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

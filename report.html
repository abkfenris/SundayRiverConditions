A beautiful slopeside sunrise lights up the stripes across the mountain this morning. Choose from nearly 400 acres of open terrain to ski at first chair, with lifts spinning from 9:00AM-3:45PM today. Groomers worked all last night and into this morning to make sure there was ample corduroy to carve on more than 60 trails. The Friday forecast is in our favor. We are dancing in our ski boots celebrating the upcoming system, so stay tuned tomorrow for when the flurries fly!

We have an exciting weekend lined up, with Maine Dead Project bringing Grateful Dead songs to the stage at Shipyard Brew Haus. Test out the latest and greatest high-quality independent ski and snowboard equipment this Saturday at Indie Demo Day. Looking ahead, Après Aglow is open for Kid's Week next week, along with many other great activities! 

Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out for your convenience. To ski into the Grand Summit Hotel, please take Little White Cap Quad and ski Moonstruck via Green Cheese. When skiing into the Jordan Hotel, beginner terrain is limited to Jordan Peak via Lollapalooza, and skiing in from a different peak is currently considered advanced.

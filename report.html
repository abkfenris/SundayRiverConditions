December 28, 2021

    Happy Tuesday, Sunday River family! It's gonna be another killer day on the mountain. Snowmakers were hard at work last night, filling Tempest and White Heat with piles of new snow. Groomers put down stripes on 62 trails, adding Excalibur to the list for the first time last night! It's the perfect day for some early morning carving. Lifts start spinning at 8AM, so grab a coffee and head on up here. Park skiers and riders: Lower T72 is scheduled to open today! Shoutout to our hardworking Parks crew. Tonight the Chondola and South Ridge Express will be spinning until 6:30PM to accommodate Twilight Skiing, continuing every night of Christmas Vacation Week. A winter wonderland illuminated by thousands of twinkling lights awaits you at the Après Aglow Walk open every night this week, and conveniently located behind the Grand Summit Hotel. Tickets for both activities can be purchased online throughout the week. Come join a ski-filled week of festivities slopeside!

    Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out accessible for your convenience. Skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently only accessible via blue terrain. To access the Jordan Hotel via green terrain, please ski Sensation in order to take the Jordan Mountain Double and then ride Jordan Bowl Express, to ski Lollapalooza. At this time, all other green trails into the Jordan Hotel are temporarily considered advanced terrain. Please note our only beginner terrain is located within the South Ridge Express Quad at the moment.

      Around the Resort

Grab a photo in the picture-perfect purple gondola Cabin outside before entering the store filled with candy, sweets, treats, and unique Sunday River gift items!

      Many restaurants and food trucks recently opened for the season, including Coffee Hound in South Ridge Lodge Food Court, and in the Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel. For retail needs, head over to recently opened Aster & Birch located within the Jordan Hotel, as well as Sunday River Sports, Betty's Treats and Treasures, or unwind at the Jordan Spa.

January 19, 2022

Flurries are in the forecast this morning! We have a light layer due across 8 peaks, with temperatures warming slightly for a full day of skiing. Groomers have put down fresh corduroy on 77 trails including Vortex for you to carve today. With 11" of fresh snow, we have a selection of recently opened glades to choose from, included in our 102 open trails to start the day. Terrain expansion is at the top of our list, so check back here as we open more trails moving into the weekend. All scheduled lifts are spinning from 9AM-3:45PM for you to grab first chair or get in some afternoon turns. Come carve nearly 600 acres of open terrain! 

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl. 

Around the Resort

We've got some fun events coming up! Wicked Air & Après is returning to Barker this Saturday, January 22. This must-see event combines big air and 3D project lights into one show. 

Senior Week is January 24-28 - lots of deals for the over-65 club, and plenty of fun events and ski meet-ups to join.

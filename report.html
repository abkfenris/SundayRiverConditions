November 16, 2022 | 1:05 PM

We could not be more excited to announce that daily operations for the 2022-23 winter season begin tomorrow, November 17, at 9:00 AM for New England Pass, Ikon Pass, and Ticket Pack holders. Daily lift ticket access will begin Saturday, November 19 at 8:00 AM. Thanks to our amazing snowmakers and a helping hand from Mother Nature, we will be spinning the Locke Mountain Triple, with loading required from the mid-station. Advanced terrain will be available on T2 and Upper Sunday Punch. Downloading will be required. Last chair will be thrown at 3:15PM, so be sure to get here early enough to get your first turns of the season in.

Snowmaking operations may be active at any time on the mountain, so be prepared to ski under the snow guns. A buff or something of the sort is perfect for wiping off the goggles! At this time, there is no beginner terrain available on the mountain. In addition to this, due to limited terrain availability, no uphill traffic is permitted at this time. Our team is working exceptionally hard to take advantage of the prolonged cold and open additional terrain as fast as possible.

Holiday availability is filling up fast, and with cold temps forecast through Thanksgiving, expansion is on the horizon.

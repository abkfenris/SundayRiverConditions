Mountain Report
Last Updated: September 19, 2023

Gear up for an exhilarating uphill adventure with Uphill Will and the Mahoosuc Adventure Fest! Rain or shine, join us for a weekend made for adventure!

Do you have what it takes to scale Mt. Washington in six hours? Or perhaps conquer Mt. Whitney in 12? Dare to attempt the Everest Challenge in just 24 hours? Registration for all challenges closes this Thursday at midnight.

Beyond the races, Mahoosuc Adventure Fest offers an array of entertainment for the whole family. Dive into the Maine Beer Box, boasting an impressive 78 taps. Plus, with the exclusive River Fest Mug, enjoy an extra 4 ounces for free with each pour. Watch and learn from timber sport demonstrations, cheer on the racers, groove to live music, and enjoy bites from food trucks and Sunny's!

For more details on River Fest and the upcoming events, which includes a kaleidoscope making class, pottery demonstrations, an antique car cruise-in, and more, please visit our website. 

As we transition into fall, scenic lift rides will now run on Saturdays and Sundays, from 10AM - 4PM, with 3:30PM being the last time to purchase a ticket for the day. 

Lastly, don't forget to share your summer joy with us on Happy Place Found – we love seeing your adventures!

April 2, 2023 | 6:20 AM

Morning Report

Good morning spring shredders. Springfest Sunday has arrived, and the party keeps going. Our groomers could only get onto the hill early this morning due to the high temperatures overnight. They are finishing their grooming plan as the sun rises over the distant mountains. Snow conditions will be firm but perfectly smooth to start the day.

We are still seeing high winds in our area for the morning. Gusts are forecasted to be as high as 50 mph. As lifts begin to spin this morning, we will be doing constant wind checks at our peaks. Please check the mountain report as you make your way up today, as we do expect some windholds for the morning. We will update our lift status and mountain report as soon as we are notified of any holds. There will also be no uphill access today.

After a day of lapping our 8 peaks head down to white cap for another afternoon at Springfest! Due to the weather, we'll be moving the party upstairs in White Cap lodge.  We have a lot still going on here at Sunday River; as we wind down the season be sure to check out our Spring operations schedule and end-of-season events.

See you out there!
-Team Snow

A Note:

Spring brings with it some of the best conditions of the year, but also the most variable condition. Please note that trail conditions may vary and change rapidly throughout the day.

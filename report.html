December 2, 2021

December is already bringing us holiday cheer with a fresh dusting of snow and new terrain on Spruce Peak! Enjoy new snowmaking on Risky Business, and plenty of groomers topped with fresh snow from Mother Nature. We also made snow on Escapade, Lower Grand Rapids, Lower Down Draft, Sirius, Aludra, and Lights Out last night. Spruce Peak Triple, Locke Mountain Triple and South Ridge Express are also running from 9AM-3:45PM, and Barker Mountain Express remains closed. You can access Barker Mountain’s terrain on-foot via Jungle Road from Upper Sunday Punch. Come celebrate all this snow with us today!

All lift tickets need to be purchased online in advance. Have your RFID from last year? You can head straight to the lift! Skier services are available in both the Barker and South Ridge Lodges, as well as shuttle service between these lodges and the hotels while the lifts spin. We are now accepting cashless forms of payment only. Please be sure to have a gift card, resort charge, credit card, or debit card available for on-site purchases. 

COVID-19 Protocols

Though we are no longer requiring guests to wear masks in general areas, we suggest masking indoors. However, some services may require you to wear a mask, so be sure to have one on hand. Please make sure to read through our COVID Winter Guide before visiting the resort to understand our operating plans and how they may affect you. Remember, we're all in this together.

Santa Sunday is this Weekend!

Start celebrating your holiday cheer with hundreds of Santas shredding down South Ridge! This Sunday, December 5, come see or be one of over 200 skiing Santas. Pre-register with a $20 donation online in order to be a Santa, with all proceeds going to the River Fund. If you choose to be in full Santa-attire to participate in our photoshoot festivities, you will receive an all-day lift ticket. Rather leave the beard at home? Spectators are welcome to South Ridge by 11AM, for the best view of all the Santas dashing down the slopes. Please direct any questions you may have about this event to events@sundayriver.com. As a santa on the slope or spectator at South Ridge, we hope to see you there!

Around the Resort

Currently, dining options at the resort include the Barker Lodge Food Court and Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan, open daily for breakfast and lunch, as well as Camp at the Summit, open for breakfast daily, and serving dinner Thursdays through Tuesdays. Dining at South Ridge Lodge will open for the weekend! For full details, visit the River Guide.

See you on the slopes!

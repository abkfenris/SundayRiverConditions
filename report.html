Pre-Season Report
Last Updated: November 11, 2023

The snow is piling up, and we're one step closer to opening day. Our dedicated Mountain Ops team has continued to work across the Jordan Bowl and Locke Peak, with all eyes set on opening.

Be sure to keep your eyes here for any and all updates as we continue to work towards our opening day. We can't wait to be back on snow with you all.

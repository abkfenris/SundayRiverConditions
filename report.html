THURSDAY, JANUARY 28

Yesterday Mother Nature gave us a sweet surprise with 4” of new snow. Conditions have been amazing, but they just got even better. With this new snow groomed into the slopes, you can expect incredible carving. Plus, ropes have been dropping left and right so we now have more open glades including Blind Ambition and Celestial. Snowmakers must have the can’t stop won’t stop motto in their mind because last night they fired up the snow guns on Risky Business, South Paw, Broadway, Who-Ville, Lower Sunday Punch, and Easy Street. It is a wintery paradise on the mountain right now, and you should definitely hop on first chair at 9AM to enjoy it.

This winter, your car is your base lodge. We're here to help you make the most of it by giving away over $1,000 in gear from YETI to help you boot up in the morning, grab a quick lunch between laps, and kick back for après.

There are plenty of food options around the resort for spreading out, but please remember that capacity is limited. Take a look at the River Guide to discover locations, hours, online ordering, and how to make a reservation. 

We are requiring online lift ticket purchase and setting limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, half-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Looking for some last-minute weekend plans? We are allowing one-night stays at the Snow Cap Inn this weekend only. Book lodging online and guarantee lift ticket access by purchasing tickets at check-in. 

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines.

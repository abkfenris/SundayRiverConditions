January 3, 2022

It is a beautiful morning on the mountain! We have the most options in the East for you to select from, with over 70 open trails and 410 acres to enjoy. Snowmakers are blasting guns on American Express and Tempest today to cover even more terrain in newly made snow. The sun is shining on fresh corduroy, atop 65 trails to carve. Lifts will be spinning from 9AM-3:45PM, so grab first chair to start your week off on the right ski boot!

Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out for your convenience. To ski into the Grand Summit Hotel via green terrain, take Little White Cap Quad and ski down Moonstruck via Green Cheese. To access the Jordan Hotel via green terrain, please ride the Jordan Bowl Express, and ski Lollapalooza. At this time, all other green trails into the Jordan Hotel are temporarily considered advanced terrain.

Around the Resort

Grab a bite at all our open restaurants and food trucks on the mountain, including Coffee Hound in South Ridge Lodge Food Court, the Ona-Tripp Food Truck, and more. For shopping and relaxation needs, head over to Aster & Birch located within the Jordan Hotel, as well as Sunday River Sports, Betty's Treats and Treasures, or unwind at the Jordan Spa. Please note: Sliders will be closed this week with a planned reopening on January 7, but Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel is open until 8PM daily. 

For the latest updates whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

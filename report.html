Morning Report
Last Updated: January 4, 2024 | 7:43 AM

Today the Aurora Quad will join our lineup of lifts, making for 10 lifts, 37 trails, and 255 acres of groomed terrain. We're looking at temperatures in the mid-thirties this morning, although they'll fall throughout the afternoon and stay cold for the next few days. Cross your fingers: we might have some frozen precipitation on the horizon.

Our grooming team hit all open terrain last night, and Excalibur joins the list of groomed trails. The corduroy is fresh and the snow should be firm. Our snowmaking team was out and about last night, blowing snow over on Aurora. Keep an eye out for new terrain in the coming days. We also opened the uphill route on Jordan this morning, so slap on those skins and get after it. 

This weekend, check out Indie Demo Day, where you'll have the chance to demo some of the top independent ski and snowboard brands. Swing by the White Cap Base Lodge between 8AM and 12PM to register for the event. The full list of indie brands is coming soon. 

Temps are cold and holding, and we'll keep expanding.

Think Cold!

Next Steps

Following the opening of the Jordan Bowl, the Aurora Quad is planned to re-open today, January 4 for the season.

The Chondola is currently loading cabins only. We are expecting to be able to re-open the chair side of the lift Friday, January 5.

The Alpeniglu is currently scheduled to re-open on Friday, January 5, as well as the Outpost, for convenient slopeside snacks and beverages.

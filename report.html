January 28, 2022

Today is the carve before the storm. Nearly 80 trails with fresh corduroy await your skis for first chair at 9AM this morning. Flurries should be falling on all 8 peaks today in preparation for the days ahead. Look forward to more than 100 open trails and full days of shredding with Twilight Skiing lifts spinning from 3PM to 6:30PM today and Saturday. Mother Nature knows we want a powder weekend, and this incoming system has quite the forecast. Saturday we have a predicted 5-7" of snow starting at 8AM for first chair, and an added 7-10" Saturday night into Sunday morning. What better way to spend your weekend, then skiing piles and piles of fresh snow!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl. 

Around the Resort

Senior Week is here, from January 24 to 28! Enjoy lots of deals for the over-65 club, and plenty of fun events and ski meet-ups to join.

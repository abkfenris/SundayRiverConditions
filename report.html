Morning Report

Well, you know what they say about the weather in New England. We're experiencing the full spectrum in the last week, from fresh snow to spring corn, and now we're getting the wind. As of this writing, winds are affecting our ability to safely operate high mountain lifts, and are forecasted to increase in intensity throughout the day.

Our team is on the hill assessing lifts and will continue to do so throughout the day. Keep your eyes on the Mountain Report for all of the most up-to-date info as we move through the day. 

See you out there!
-Team Snow

Spring brings with it some of the best conditions of the year, but also the most variable condition. Please note that trail conditions may vary and change rapidly throughout the day.

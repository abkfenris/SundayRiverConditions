January 24, 2022

Beautiful terrain and blue skies greet us this morning slopeside! Snowmakers blasted guns all night on trails including Escapade and 3D. Groomers took all that freshly made snow and put corduroy across 8 peaks. Catch first chair at 9AM and carve into 78 groomed trails. We have flurries in the forecast for tomorrow, followed by a snowy system. This week we have exciting activities available for Senior Week including the Après Aglow lighted walk, a comedy show, karaoke and more. Nearly 700 acres await your skis on the most open terrain in the East!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl.

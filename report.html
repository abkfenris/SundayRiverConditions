Next Steps

Thanks to the amazing efforts of our snowmakers, we have tentative plans to open the Jordan Bowl on Wednesday, January 3. This will include ski-in, ski-out access to the Jordan Hotel. The Alpeniglu is scheduled to re-open on Friday, January 5, as well as the Outpost, for convenient slopeside snacks and beverages. Snowmaking temps will be crucial, but the forecast looks promising, and our snowmakers are working hard.

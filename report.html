March 30, 2022

Good morning folks, and welcome to another beautiful Wednesday on the mountain. Today is shaping up to be an absolute stunner, with sunshine all day, and warmth making its way back to the Mahoosucs this afternoon. The grooming team put fresh cord down on 66 trails for your enjoyment. Carve it up for first chair at 9AM, and then hang around into the afternoon for soft turns. The remnants of yesterday's winds are still hanging around this morning, which could impact lift services to the higher peaks. We are expecting the breeze to diminish throughout the day, so keep an eye here for the most up-to-date lift and trail information.

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. All uphill access to Jordan Bowl trails is closed. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

We're excited to welcome the Can-Ams to Sunday River this week, featuring the top junior athletes of Canada and the Americas in a multi-day ski racing event. In the meantime, please keep in mind T2 is closed as our team works to prepare for the event. 

Around the Resort

Use your 2022-23 New England Pass today, with the convenience of our new payment plan options and expanded perks!

We are bringing the beach to the slopes this weekend April 1-3! Come celebrate the return of spring at Spring Festival complete with parties, music, and great spring skiing and riding.

What's spring at Sunday River without pond skimming? Join us in your favorite costume April 9, for the annual Pond-A-Palooza and look forward to a chance to win prizes. Registration is free, but in order to secure a spot, head to the Welcome Center at South Ridge Lodge to sign the dotted line and get your bib number.

The second Spring Après Concert Series is hitting the slopes April 9! Enjoy a free slopeside show after a day full of spring skiing and pond skimming.

One-night stays are now available at the Snow Cap Inn. Join us for convenient, budget-friendly lodging options.

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, featuring delicious sweets and special souvenirs from our exclusive logowear collection. Check out our many retail options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Unwind and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. Our spa is paired with a boutique, where you can find beauty and wellness products, athletic wear, and more. You can find even more retail options across the resort!

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

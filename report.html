November 25, 2021

Happy Thanksgiving from snowy Sunday River! The snowmakers continued to fire the guns on Monday Mourning, Tourist Trap, Risky Business, Lazy River, Outta Here, Aludra, Sirius, Lights Out, Easy Street, Broadway, and Cascades last night. Temperatures will hang in the high 30s today with partly sunny skies, so while you won't see snowmaking, we guarantee that you'll see plenty of happy folks carving turns down a groomed Right Stuff, Cascades, Ecstasy, Jungle Road, and Sunday Punch, with terrain park features on Upper Sunday Punch. Lifts are spinning from 8AM-3:45PM, and all terrain is considered advanced. Once you're done taking runs, be sure to head over to Camp's Thanksgiving Buffet from 12-8PM at the Grand Summit Hotel. Reservations are strongly recommended.

We're now open to day tickets, ticket packs, and season passes, which should be purchased online in advance. Have your RFID from last year? You can head straight to the lift! All skier services will be based out of Barker Lodge. Season Pass Office is also open at South Ridge Lodge and we will have shuttle service between Barker, South Ridge, and the hotels while the lifts spin. We are now accepting cashless forms of payment only. Please be sure to have a gift card, resort charge, credit card, or debit card available for on-site purchases. 

COVID-19 Protocols

Though we are no longer requiring guests to wear masks in general areas, we suggest masking indoors. However, some services may require you to wear a mask, so be sure to have one on-hand. Please make sure to read through our COVID Winter Guide before visiting the resort to understand our operating plans and how they may affect you. Remember, we're all in this together.

Morning Report

Friday is finally here, giving us another fresh 3" of snow overnight. Groomers got out early to smooth out moguls and pack in the snow from yesterday. With a new blanket on top, today's conditions should be excellent.

We are starting the morning at around 15 degrees, with clouds passing by as it begins to clear up. Temps should stick below 20 for the day, and moderate winds are in the forecast. Keep an eye on the mountain report as we begin to get lifts going for the morning, as these winds may put us on hold in a few areas.

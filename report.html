Temperatures are currently in the single digits across the mountain, and they're forecasted to rise to low twenties up top and mid-twenties in the base areas. Our snowmaking team was off the hill last night, and they'll be back in action Tuesday night.

We have single-night stays at all resort properties this weekend through Presidents' Week, so be sure to check that out.

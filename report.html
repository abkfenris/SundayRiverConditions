April 5, 2022

We've had a fantastic morning skiing soft turns under sunny blue skies! Spring is here and the weather is gorgeous today as we get in runs until last chair. Groomers are adding a great base to nearly 50 trails tonight, greeting your skis tomorrow morning. More warm temperatures arrive tomorrow at 9AM first chair, with mixed weather forecasts developing in the days ahead. Be sure to take soft turns throughout the week on North Peak and South Ridge via the Chondola and South Ridge Express, as they will be closing for the winter 2021-22 season after this weekend. For our seasonal updates, please check out the spring skiing schedule. Look forward to a weekend full of exciting slopeside events, including fire dancing, live music, and the pond skim!

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! All uphill access is closed for terrain from Aurora Peak, Oz, Jordan Bowl, and trails serviced by Little White Cap Quad. Beginner terrain is exclusively located within South Ridge and North Peak. We've had an outstanding season skiing across all 8 peaks, and can't wait to continue next 2022-23 season!

Please note ski-in/ski-out access for the Grand Summit Hotel requires navigation of intermediate and advanced terrain. For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

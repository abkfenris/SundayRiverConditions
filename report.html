Afternoon Report
Last Updated: January 28, 2024 | 1:25 PM

With comfortable temps, little wind, and soft turns, today was a great way to end the weekend. We had 822 acres of skiable terrain, 18 lifts, and corduroy for days. With a chance of flurries and a bit of sun, tomorrow should be just as good.

Our grooming team will be out there as well, and tomorrow we can expect stripes on a lineup of trails similar to today. Our parks team will continue working to build and open more features across the mountain.

December 25, 2021

We asked Santa for more snow, and he delivered! Flurries have been falling all morning on the mountain, with no sign of slowing down into the afternoon. Groomers and snowmakers are working hard to deliver new corduroy runs, putting stripes on White Cap's Moonstruck, Bear Paw, Green Cheese, Cutoff, and more for tomorrow. Make turns on over 60 open trails and more than 362 acres of terrain as we expand. Tomorrow morning Little White Cap Quad, Locke Mountain Triple, North Peak Express, and Quantum Leap Triple will be added to our list of spinning lifts. Twilight Skiing is opening from 3:00PM-6:30PM starting tomorrow, every night for the duration of Christmas Vacation Week. If you're looking for leisure in this winter wonderland, head to the Après Aglow Walk, also opening tomororw night for the duration of this week's festivities. Tickets for both activities can be purchased online for your convenience. Brighten your break with fireworks, fresh snow, and the most corduroy to carve in the East!

Since Jordan Bowl is open, both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out access for your convenience. Skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently only accessible via blue terrain. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. Come ski in a winter wonderland!

Looking for a last-minute getaway? One-night stays are available at the Jordan Hotel for Sunday night (December 26) for a limited number of rooms. Book online while they last.

February 5, 2022

Your happy place awaits today. We are 100% open, thanks to yesterday's 18" of fresh snow, bringing our total for the last week to nearly 3 feet. The stashes are endless in the trees, thanks to our boundary-to-boundary skiing. Groomers put stripes on more than 80 trails for you to carve into beautiful corduroy for first chair at 8AM. Choose from 135 trails and glades to ski all day, then join us back on North Peak for Twilight Skiing from 4-6:30PM tonight. We're also planning on opening the Oz Quad later today. Plus, Vortex, Airglow, Black Hole, 3D, White Heat, and South Paw are all-natural terrain today so you can shred through their piles of snow. With the snowy system leaving town, please keep an eye on the lift and trail status as we may experience some winds today. 

Around the Resort

Eddy and Betty the Yeti are making a special appearance at Betty's Treats & Treasures at 11:30PM today. While you're there, head on into Betty's for a delicious treat or shop the exclusive logowear collection. 

OutRyders is partnering with Sunday River to bring you Pride Weekend going on now. New England's largest LGBTQ+ ski and snowboard club is all about great friends, mountain memories, social activities, and great deals. Look forward to fun events this weekend, including a Halloween-themed party Saturday night at the Foggy Goggle.

The Après Aglow lighted walk is open this weekend for you to enjoy thousands of twinkling lights while sipping on delicious hot cocoa. Then on Sunday, give telemark skiing a try with the FreeHeeler's Demo Tour.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments.

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

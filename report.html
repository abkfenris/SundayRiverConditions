March 4, 2022

Sunshine and stunning stripes! Cooler temperatures are keeping our fresh corduroy pristine all-day under these great blue skies. After recent flurries, we have more than 80 groomed trails for you to carve into starting at 9AM first chair. This morning you can take all-natural turns as well, and find plenty of powder stashes to begin your weekend. Spanning across 8 peaks, we are starting today with 120 glades and trails, and nearly 800 acres of open terrain. Please keep an eye on the lift and trail status as winds may impact lifts today. Take as many turns as you can under blue skies with scheduled day lifts spinning until 4PM, then head to North Peak to maximize your time on the slopes with Twilight Skiing under the stars until 6:30PM. Check out our great list of activities for family and friends this weekend, including winter wonderland walks, music, and more. Start your weekend with rays all day, skiing dreamy corduroy!

 

Around the Resort

A great lineup of slopeside activities awaits you this weekend. Take a relaxing stroll at the Après Aglow lighted walk, conveniently located behind the Grand Summit Hotel. Gaze in awe of the trees wrapped in thousands of twinkling lights, as you sip on hot cocoa. You can also enjoy live music at one of several venues across the mountain, and extend your time on the slopes with Twilight Skiing tonight and tomorrow night.

Check out the Red Bull Slide-In Tour March 12 to celebrate snowboard culture in the East! The pros will be offering a progression clinic at Who-Ville terrain park, a free ride session, and an epic crew show. You can also join us for some post-riding brews and Red Bull at the official afterparty, hosted by the Foggy Goggle.

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, featuring delicious sweets and special souvenirs from our exclusive logowear collection. Check out our many retail options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Unwind and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. Our spa is paired with a boutique, where you can find beauty and wellness products, athletic wear, and more.

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

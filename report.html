Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 6, 2024 | 1:10 PM

We're tipping into another excellent afternoon of skiing and riding. Temps are continuing to warm across all eight peaks, and it's just fantastic out there.

For this evening, snowmaking will continue to take advantage of the cold nights, making snow in a few key areas and stockpiling while temps persist. We're looking at a bit of a warm-up coming later this week, so they're planning on taking a few well-deserved down days until the next cold snap.

Grooming will have a busy schedule, putting stripes back across the mountain. The big news - White Heat is expected to open as a groomed trail tomorrow for the first time this season. Getting there for first tracks is going to be a must.

Presidents' Week will be here before we know it, and we have events planned for the whole family. There will be fireworks, a fire dancer, twilight skiing, Eddy and Betty Dinners, and more. We also have a few spots left for our Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River during President's Week. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now.

Think cold!

Morning Report

Happy Snow Day! Snow moved in last night, dropping a healthy 2" at the base, with Mountain Ops reporting 3-4" across the peaks. We were only expecting an inch or two out of this first round, so this overperformance is wonderful to see. We're expecting a break in the action this morning, with the meat of the storm coming in throughout the afternoon. This second wave could drop as much as 6-8" if everything goes to plan.

For today, skiers and riders can expect wonderful soft turns across all eight peaks. Grooming had a successful night as well, smoothing out 80 trails prior to snow moving in. Wind has the potential to impact lift operations today. We are currently in good shape with all scheduled lifts planning to spin for 9 AM, but as always, keep your eyes on the Mountain Report for the most up-to-date lift status.

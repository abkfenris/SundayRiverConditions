Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 12, 2024 | 3:06 PM

Another Monday in the books. We had some amazing pockets of sunshine throughout the day that kept things nice and toasty. The weather ahead looks to be getting back to more normal February conditions. Snowmaking will kick back on tonight to resurface trails across the resort. The plan is to keep some of the guns running after 9AM tomorrow, so you'll likely encounter active snowmaking in the earlier morning hours as temps allow. We've got a slight chance of snow tomorrow, but our eyes have shifted to later this weeke to hopefully pick up some fresh accumulation heading into Presidents' Week.

And speaking of Presidents' Week, it's right around the corner, and we have events planned for the whole family. We have fireworks, fire dancing, a circus, laser tags, Eddy and Betty dinners, and more. Red Bull will also be at Sunday River this coming weekend to host Winter Vibes, an outdoor concert series you won't want to miss. Our new Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River still has spaces available for next weekend as well, so check that out before they're gone. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now. 

We'll see you out there!

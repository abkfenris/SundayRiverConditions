Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 10, 2024 | 1:14 PM

What a beautiful day to hit the slopes. The temperature inversion of this morning turned itself around, and it's currently in the high forties at the base areas. The sun is out, the turns are soft, and outdoor après is calling our names. Today is the perfect opportunity to work on those goggle tans. 

Our snowmaking team is waiting out the warmer weather, and they should be back in action early Monday. In the meantime, our grooming team will be laying corduroy on 80 trails tonight, giving us stripes on over half of our open terrain. Tomorrow, it will likely be a little cooler and partly sunny. 

The fun doesn't stop when the sun goes down, and we have twilight skiing tonight out of South Ridge. Stop by the Jordan Spa for Sips & Sweets today from 4-5:30PM, and enjoy some complimentary prosecco and sweets while you browse and mingle.  There's live music at three different locations across the mountain today; Lexi James will be at the Foggy Goggle from 3-6PM, Shipyard will have Chase Job from 2-PM and A Tribute to Chris Stapleton and Miranda Lambert from 7-10PM, and Brad Hooper will be at the Barker Bar from 2-5PM. A few spots are still available for our new Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River tonight, so check that out before they're gone. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now. Don't forget: Presidents' Week is right around the corner, and we have events planned for the whole family, including fire dancing, a circus, laser tag, and more. 

Think cold!

Not bad for April, if we don't say so ourselves. Yesterday's snow has allowed Patrol to drop ropes left and right, and we are now skiing and riding on over 800 acres of terrain across all 8 peaks. Sheesh. 

The grooming team will be out again tonight and is planning to lay stripes on over 70 trails. The woods should still be good tomorrow, so beeline for the River for some final turns in the woods before they're done for the season.

We're expecting an awesome weekend of Spring skiing, so why not ski for free? All new New England Passes automatically qualify for immediate access all Spring long, so buy now, ski now, and maximize the value of your pass now!

In other news, Monday's eclipse is fast approaching, and with forecasts calling for sun and 56 as a high, it's shaping up to be a once-in-a-lifetime experience. We've got lodging specials all weekend to celebrate, culminating in our Solar Eclipse Viewing Party. Head to the deck on the North Peak Lodge for a chance to hold a piece of the moon, courtesy of the Maine Mineral & Gem Museum, while you watch the eclipse. It's sure to be out of this world. Spring Fest Weekend also starts this Friday, so there's tons to do around the mountain.

We'll see you out there!

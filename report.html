Mountain Report
Last Updated: October 17, 2023

Our eyes and hearts are set on winter! 

In case you missed it, earlier last week the Mountain Operations team completed our annual snowmaking test. 4 HKD Impulse tower guns sprung to life atop the Jordan Bowl, signaling the official kick-off to the winter countdown. We can't wait for prolonged cold temps to settle into Western Maine, and we'll be ready to go when it does.

If you haven't yet heard, our opening plans for this season have changed. This year, we're heading west and will be opening in the Jordan Bowl. It's a huge shift that we couldn't be more excited about, and will make for an unprecedented early-season experience.

We can't wait to see you for the first turns of the season. 

Think cold!

- Team Snow

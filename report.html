Today’s bluebird is brought to you by 18” of new snow and sunshine across 8 peaks! Choose from any trail, since 100% of our terrain is now open. Around the Resort

OutRyders is partnering with Sunday River to bring you Pride Weekend going on now. New England's largest LGBTQ+ ski and snowboard club is all about great friends, mountain memories, social activities, and great deals. Look forward to fun events this weekend, including a Halloween-themed party Saturday night at the Foggy Goggle.

The Après Aglow lighted walk is open this weekend for you to enjoy thousands of twinkling lights while sipping on delicious hot cocoa. Then on Sunday, give telemark skiing a try with the FreeHeeler's Demo Tour.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. You can also treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures with delicious candy, or shop our exclusive logowear collection.

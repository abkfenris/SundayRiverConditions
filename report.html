February 3, 2022

We'll be straight with you: today is bringing us soft snow, grey skies, and a chance of drizzle with temperatures in the 30s. But it's all expected to turn a corner tonight with forecasts calling for a foot of snow overnight into Friday morning. Until then, grab your Gore-Tex layers, enjoy some smooth and buttery turns on over 800 acres of skiable terrain, and get ready for yet another powder day. The grooming team spent the evening putting stripes on 81 trails, and there are still goods in the woods from last weekend's 14" of snow. 

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl.

Around the Resort

OutRyders is partnering with Sunday River to bring you Pride Weekend this February 4-6! New England's largest LGBTQ+ ski and snowboard club is all about great friends, mountain memories, social activities, and great deals. Look forward to fun events this weekend, including a Halloween-themed party! Plus, give telemark skiing a try with the FreeHeeler's Demo Tour on Saturday.

The Après Aglow lighted walk is open this weekend for you to enjoy thousands of twinkling lights while sipping on delicious hot cocoa. You can also treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, open daily for you to indulge in delicious sweets. Find the perfect souvenir, from Sunday River logowear to mugs and more, so that you can fondly remember your moments on the mountain.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments!

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

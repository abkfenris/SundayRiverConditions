December 21, 2021

Terrain expansion is how we count down to the holidays! Today a groomed Upper Grand Rapids opens, with Exit Left to follow. We have 50 open trails covered in freshly made snow and stripes for you to choose from. Snowmakers are blasting guns on Ecstasy, Right Stuff, and Sundance all day to make piles and piles of snow. Christmas Vacation Week is coming up, with more snow in the forecast as an excellent present for all! Check out the all-week festivities across the resort, including fireworks, twilight skiing, and the opening of the Après Aglow walk. Lifts are spinning from 9AM-3:45PM today for you to enjoy all this corduroy!

    Since Jordan Bowl is open, both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out access for your convenience. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. With this in mind, skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently accessible via blue terrain. Rental snowboards are now back in stock. Countdown to even more holiday celebrations filled with snow and skiing!

April 16, 2022

Things may be a little wet out there today, but the snow is soft and plentiful for this mid-April weekend. First chair opens at 8AM to access Spruce, Barker, White Cap, and White Heat chairs. Then after your runs, head to the Barker parking lot for The Great Tailgate.

All skier services, including ticket purchases and food options, are at Barker base lodge. Guest Services is open in the Welcome Center at South Ridge. There are no rentals or lessons available. Please note uphill access is closed for South Ridge, North Peak, Aurora Peak, Oz, Jordan Bowl, and terrain serviced by Little White Cap Quad. All terrain is intermediate and advanced, including ski-in/ski-out access to the Grand Summit Hotel. Access to White Cap Basin via Road Runner is closed and Lower Tempest has some walking required. 

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

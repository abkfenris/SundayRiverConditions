Morning Report
Last Updated: December 7, 2023 | 7:52 AM

We had a great day yesterday, and we’ll have another great day today. We’ll have some classic December weather today, with the colder temps of this morning warming up throughout the day. With partly cloudy skies and temperatures in the mid-twenties, you might even be able to start working on that goggle tan.

Our grooming team laid out some stripes for us last night on almost all of our terrain, but if you’re seeking some ungroomed trails, head over to Lower Airglow, Borealis, and Aludra. The newest addition to our open terrain, Jim’s Whim, is groomed and waiting for some turns. Our snowmaking team was hard at work last night, and there will be snowmaking on a few trails today, including Upper Sunday Punch. Keep an eye out for more terrain as we head into this weekend.

We’re getting closer and closer to the grand opening of the Barker 6. Join us for celebrations staring at 8:45AM, with the first chair heading up at 9AM. Keep an eye out for Donny Pelletier; he’ll be out on the slopes on Saturday, showing us all up. He might even be signing some posters, bub. Check out all of the details on the Barker 6 Grand Opening page.

Don’t forget to take a look at our Christmas Vacation Week plans. We’ve got a whole host of events, great deals on lodging and lift tickets, and of course, the most dependable snow in New England. Keep an eye out for gift ideas at the Sunday River Sports flagship store, as well.

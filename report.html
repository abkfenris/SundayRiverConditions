Morning Report

Good morning snow lovers, happy Wednesday. Snowmaking was back on the slopes last night and this morning, continuing to take advantage of these colder temps at night. The grooming team took to the slopes overnight also, pressing fresh stripes into all of the standard trails.

If your lucky enough to have the day off from work today, make the most of this nice weather and make your way up to Sunday River for a day of fast and fun turns.

We'll see you out there!

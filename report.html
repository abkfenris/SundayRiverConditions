Afternoon Report

839 acres of open terrain, 131 trails, light winds, and soft snow? Yes, please. The winds of this morning wound down by late morning, and all of our scheduled lifts are currently spinning. Check out glades like Celestial and Gnarnia for some fun in the trees, or try your hand at some bumps on trails like American Express and Tempest.

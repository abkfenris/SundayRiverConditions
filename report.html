January 18, 2022

The most open terrain in the East is covered in corduroy after 11" of fresh snow! Choose from over 570 acres and 100 trails today. The forecast calls for blue skies and some wind, so please check the lift and trail status throughout the day as we keep you updated. Groomers put down stripes across 8 peaks for you to carve.

SUNDAY, JANUARY 31

Sunday funday has arrived and with it we have sunshine, eight open peaks, and endless stripes. Ropes have been dropping left and right so you can take your pick from perfect stripes to hidden pow stashes in the glades such as Poppy Fields, Blind Ambition, and Celestial. The snowcats spent the night prowling the slopes for pristine corduroy from White Cap to Jordan Bowl. The snow is pure hero snow out there, dry, squeaky, and carves like a dream. If you are new to skiing and riding, head to South Ridge for the best learning terrain. Plus, there is also great green terrain out of the Grand Summit Hotel for beginners. Lifts will start spinning at 8AM today. 

Looking ahead, it is time to start dancing as we keep our eyes on a mid-week storm heading our way that could potentially drop a foot or more of snow.

This winter, your car is your base lodge. We're here to help you make the most of it by giving away over $1,000 in gear from YETI to help you boot up in the morning, grab a quick lunch between laps, and kick back for après. Enter by February 1 for your chance to win.

There are plenty of food options around the resort for spreading out, but please remember that capacity is limited. Take a look at the River Guide to discover locations, hours, online ordering, and how to make a reservation. The Mountain Room will be closed today. 

We are requiring online lift ticket purchase and setting limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, half-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines.

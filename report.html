January 13, 2022

Good morning Sunday River skiers and riders! Happy Thursday, AKA the warmest day of this week. If there was any day this week to play hooky and go skiing instead, today is the day! We've got to savor these two digit temps, because they will be gone by this weekend. Layering lots is recommended! Temps allowed for snowmaking last night, giving a refresh to Sunday Punch, Express Lane, and Jibe. Groomers lay down stripes all across the mountain, including newly-groomed Salvation. Grab a coffee at South Ridge or a hot chocolate with all the toppings at Betty's Treats and Treasures before hopping on first chair at 9AM. Time for some Thursday turns!

The White Heat Quad is scheduled to come back online this morning. The Aurora Peak Quad will remain closed today, but terrain it services remains open for uphill traffic with a valid lift ticket or season pass. Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails. 

Around the Resort

It's Kids' Week here at Sunday River, with deals on lodging, lift tickets, and lessons, plus fun events like Après Aglow, fireworks, a fire dancer, and a magic show. Then, we're gearing up for a super fun Martin Luther King, Jr. Weekend on the mountain, with tap takeovers, fireworks, twilight skiing, and more. Just make sure you bundle up! It's going to be a a chilly weekend on the mountain, so please dress appropriately if you will be skiing with us. Need to get away? One-night stays just opened up for this Saturday night at the Snow Cap Inn and Jordan Hotel.

Looking ahead, Wicked Air & Après is returning to Barker on January 22. This must-see event combines big air and 3D project lights into one show. 

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

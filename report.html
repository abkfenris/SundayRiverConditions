April 11, 2022

It's been a beautiful spring morning on the mountain! Blue skies, sunshine, and warm temperatures keep us skiing until last chair. Please check the lift and trail status as winds may impact operations this afternoon. Enjoy nearly 200 acres of open terrain after lunch, or catch first chair at 9AM tomorrow. Forecasts are calling for alternating sunshine and intermittent precipitation as we ski further into a new week of spring. For weekly updates on the best ways to get in your runs, head over to our Spring Schedule. Groomers are adding a base of fresh corduroy to some of our favorite trails so you can take Tuesday turns tomorrow!

Please note uphill access is closed for South Ridge, North Peak, Aurora Peak, Oz, Jordan Bowl, and terrain serviced by Little White Cap Quad. All terrain is intermediate and advanced, including ski-in/ski-out access to the Grand Summit Hotel. Access to White Cap Basin via Road Runner is closed. We look forward to another great season skiing across all 8 peaks!

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

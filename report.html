March 15, 2022

A spectacular day on sunny slopes! We are enjoying Tuesday turns on 116 open glades and trails this beautiful afternoon. Nearly 800 acres of available terrain await your skis after lunch, or catch first chair at 9AM for fresh corduroy on more than 80 groomed trails. Temperatures have increased since this morning, and we are looking forward to continued welcoming weather as we ski through the week. A few flurries are forecasted overnight, with another stunning day arriving tomorrow. The next few days are looking great for getting in as many runs as you want under the sun, and we couldn't be more excited. The Chondola cabins are back so you and your crew can have your own view as you ride up to North Peak. Look forward to all our exciting March events coming up including live music, great brews, and fantastic activities for all!

Around the Resort

2022-23 New England Passes are now on sale! Check out our new payment plan options, expanded perks, and start using next year's pass this March 21.

Get on the slopes with your girlfriends at Women's Weekend! Arriving March 25-27, this women-only wellness weekend focuses on fun, fitness and above all, our female friendships. From skiing and yoga classes to spa and shopping experiences, reserve your spot to have an unforgettable slopeside weekend.

Craft Beer Weekend is coming to you March 25-27. This exciting event kicks off the launch of the Western Maine Brew Passport, a collaboration between Sunday River and breweries across Western Maine. Take this opportunity to try out plenty of different pints here, and pick up your passport to start collecting stamps!

The first Spring Après Concert Series of the season arrives March 26! Come enjoy free live music after a full day of skiing, featuring great bands. The stage is conveniently located so you and your group can grab refreshments after a great day on the slopes.

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, featuring delicious sweets and special souvenirs from our exclusive logowear collection. Check out our many retail options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Unwind and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. Our spa is paired with a boutique, where you can find beauty and wellness products, athletic wear, and more. You can find even more retail options across the resort!

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

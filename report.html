Mountain Report
Last Updated: October 4, 2023

If you feel like you blinked and summer was over, we're right there with you. It's truly hard to believe that it's already October, Fall Fest is this weekend, and the 2023-24 ski season is right around the corner. Speaking of skiing, have you picked up your New England Pass yet? Prices increase next Wednesday, October 11, at midnight, so don't wait to lock in your winter at Fall rates.

But back to Fall and the coming weekend. We've got a full slate of events - rain or shine - including classics like the North American Wife Carrying Championship, the New England Cornhole Championship, and the Blue Mountain Arts & Crafts Fair. This will also be the final weekend of Scenic Chairlift Rides for the Fall, so don't miss your chance to get some beautiful foliage views.

In other news, we're hiring! Join us this Friday from 1-4PM at the Discovery Center in South Ridge for a hiring event with managers from departments resort-wide. Learn about the mountain, chat with the team, and get hired on the spot.

Please note, the EV charging stations in South Ridge are not available at this time as we work towards adding more to Lot 3. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. 

Lastly, don't forget to share your fall joy with us on Happy Place Found – we love seeing your adventures!

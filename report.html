Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 7, 2024 | 1:37 PM

What a day for soaking up the sun and spending some time on the slopes. With bluebird skies and comfortable temps, today was a fabulous day for skiing and riding. Anyone have their goggle tan yet?

Our snowmaking team will continue to resurface some trails across the resort, including Vortex and Airglow, and they'll begin setting up to resurface a few more trails. Our grooming team will take care of our usual list of trails, including the majority of our green and blue trails. They'll also work on the new snow on Borealis and Rogue Angel. Three Mile Trail will remain ungroomed.

Presidents' Week will be here before we know it, and we have events planned for the whole family. There will be fireworks, a fire dancer, twilight skiing, Eddy and Betty Dinners, and more. We also have a few spots left for our Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River during President's Week. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now.

Think cold!

- Team Snow

February 1, 2022

Take your Tuesday turns on 799 acres of terrain this afternoon. We have been adding to our growing list of open trails today, and since this morning we have dropped ropes on Downdraft and Ruby Palace. The sun is shining on the slopes today, making it a perfect time to carve into 81 corduroy trails. Groomers are putting down fresh stripes tonight across all 8 peaks, and Mother Nature is bringing in more snow as we ski into the weekend. An incoming storm system is forecasted for Thursday night into Friday, setting us up for a great week ahead.

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl.

Around the Resort

OutRyders is partnering with Sunday River to bring you Pride Weekend this February 4-6! New England's largest LGBTQ+ ski and snowboard club is all about great friends, mountain memories, social activities, and great deals. Look forward to fun events this weekend, including a Halloween-themed party!

The Après Aglow lighted walk is open this weekend for you to enjoy thousands of twinkling lights while sipping on delicious hot cocoa.

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, open daily for you to indulge in delicious sweets. Find the perfect souvenir, from Sunday River clothes to mugs and more, so that you can fondly remember your moments on the mountain.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments!

Morning Report

Mother Nature might be sending some snow our way soon, but that doesn't mean we can't help her out in the meantime. We currently have active snowmaking across Barker, Spruce, South Ridge, and Jordan, and those snow guns blasting certainly make a lovely sight to see. Temps are currently in the low to mid-twenties, and they should warm up to the mid to high thirties by this afternoon. We're looking at some cloudy skies today, with possible snow flurries this afternoon - and more snow incoming in the next few days.

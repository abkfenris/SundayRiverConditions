SATURDAY, APRIL 3

Happy Saturday! The sun is coming up and there are sights of snow guns blasting Right Stuff and Sunday Punch. Between snowmaking in April, sunshine, and mild temps to come later today, it's going to be a beautiful spring day on the mountain. Spring skiing is always a treat, but there's more in store this weekend with our Spring Scavenger Hunt. Keep your eyes peeled today and tomorrow as you go from peak to peak, and good luck! 

Lifts are scheduled to open at 8AM. Our groomers pressed stripes on 60 trails, so head out for first chair this morning to carve it up and then ski in the softened snow all afternoon. Head to Sunday Punch and Right Stuff to get first tracks in the snowmaking snow. With some awesome weather for this weekend, you'll want to make the last minute decision to stay into tomorrow, with one-night stays now available at the Grand Summit and Jordan Hotels through the rest of the season. We have limited skier services at the White Cap base lodge, so head to South Ridge or Barker to start your day.

With operations open daily through April 24, there is plenty of ski season ahead. View the entire Spring Schedule to learn more about peak access and resort services this spring. Our dining options will be changing weekly as we approach April 24, so don't forget to check in so that you can plan ahead. 

Don't miss out on our on-mountain savings at our retail shops around the resort, including up to 40% off at our Sunday River Sports Flagship shop at South Ridge and 30% off at the Jordan Spa Boutique.

Happy Passholder Appreciation Month! For the month of April, we're celebrating all our passholders. Join us for spring savings, a scavenger hunt, fireworks, and a passholder picnic. If you're looking to become or renew your pass, the 2021-22 New England Pass is back on sale, granting access to Sunday River, Loon & Sugarloaf all next year. Current passholders can take advantage of renewal rates and new passholders can start using their pass now when paid in full. With options that fit your budget and schedule, lock in the lowest rates on next year's pass now through April 30.

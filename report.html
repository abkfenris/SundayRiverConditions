March 19, 2022

Pack your Gore-Tex this afternoon to take soft turns, on nearly 700 acres of open terrain. After a calm morning, increasing temperatures have brought us mixed weather scattered into tonight. Ski across the peaks with scheduled day lifts spinning until 4PM, then head to North Peak for Twilight Skiing until 6:30PM tonight. Weather may impact lift operations today, so please keep an eye on the lift and trail status for the best ways to enjoy the mountain. The skies are forecasted to dry overnight, with some sunshine greeting the slopes by sunrise.

Temperatures are staying warm so you can continue to shred the trails with ease Sunday morning at 8AM first chair. Our fantastic grooming team is making a great base of fresh corduroy tonight, on some of our greatest hits for you to enjoy tomorrow. Take a break between your runs to grab bites and brews into tonight, at our many fantastic slopeside dining options. Be sure to check out exciting upcoming events this weekend and beyond, including live music, the Après Aglow lighted walk, Craft Beer Weekend, and more!

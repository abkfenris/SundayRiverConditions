THURSDAY, APRIL 22

Old Man Winter has returned, bringing a range of weather conditions our way. In addition to a dusting of new snow with flakes still falling, we also experienced some icing overnight, followed by strong winds. As a result, we'll start the day with the Locke Mountain Triple, with skiing on T2 and Sunday Punch. If we're able to get the Barker Mountain Express spinning later today, we'll also have Lazy River, Tourist Trap, Right Stuff, and Ecstasy. Stay tuned to the lift and trail status throughout the day for the most up-to-date information. 

Today and tomorrow, we'll open at 9AM, and then on Saturday, April 24, we'll open at 8AM for the last day of the season. Conditions are considered for advanced skiers and riders only. Please be aware of all signage and markings as coverage may be thin in some areas. 

All skier services and food options are available at Barker Lodge, however, our shuttles are on call for any overflow parking into South Ridge. There is no ski-in/ski-out access at our hotels, so please make your way to the Barker area for access to the slopes. Our dining options are limited, so don't forget to check-in so that you can plan ahead. Plus, one-night stays are available at the Grand Summit and Jordan Hotel through the rest of the season.

Happy Passholder Appreciation Month! For the month of April, we're celebrating all our passholders. If you're looking to become or renew your pass, the 2021-22 New England Pass is back on sale, granting access to Sunday River, Loon & Sugarloaf all next year. Current passholders can take advantage of renewal rates and new passholders can start using their pass now when paid in full. With options that fit your budget and schedule, lock in the lowest rates on next year's pass now through April 30. 

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines. As a reminder, face coverings are required in all public indoor and outdoor areas at the resort.

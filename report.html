Afternoon Report

As another great Sunday of skiing and riding comes to an end, plenty of guests still take to the slopes to grab some more runs. Conditions held up throughout the day and became soft enough to carve down easily. Runs with made snow from overnight and this morning were even softer and more playful, creating excellent conditions on those few trails. 

Snowmaking will be turned off for the night as temps will not drop enough for a quality product. Groomers will get onto the trails when best tonight, smoothing out the freshly made snow and redrawing those clean stripes onto most of the mountain. 

The weather tomorrow looks similar to today, with temperatures reaching up to the high 30s, clouds passing by all day, and light winds. There is a chance for colder temperatures and even snowfall sometime in the middle of the week. Something we can all get excited about. 

Remember to check out everything we have happening this week for Presidents' Week. From Twilight Skiing all week to bands, fireworks, and live entertainment, there is always something going on during the holidays here at Sunday River.

We are seeing some promising signs of snowfall at some point later in the week, which is a great reason to take advantage of our one-night stays now available for Presidents' Week. All properties, all week; grab yours before they're gone! 

We'll see you out there!

-Team Snow

December 13, 2021

The slopes are striped, the holidays are near, and we have the most open terrain in the East to celebrate all of this cheer! Snowmaking continues to blast throughout the mountain, to open even more trails for your enjoyment. Groomers put corduroy on even more trails last night including Dream Maker and Sundance, adding to the abundance of groomed open terrain we have for you. Jordan Bowl is opening by next weekend, at which point both hotels will feature ski-in/ski-out trails for your convenience. Come ring in the holidays with us at Winterfest this weekend, we have activities aplenty for family and friends. This Saturday, December 18, twilight skiing will be opening with tickets at $10 for all ages! Barker, South Ridge, the Chondola, Spruce, and White Cap Quad are all spinning from 9AM-3:45PM today.

Please note that our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. With this in mind, skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently accessible via blue terrain. Although the White Cap Lodge and Grand Summit Hotel are now ski-in/out, all skier services including shopping and guest service are temporarily concentrated to Barker and South Ridge Lodges. With corduroy trails galore and so much more, come enjoy our vast selection of skiable terrain!

Around the Resort

Join us at Winterfest Weekend for some holiday cheer! Come on down to see fireworks, live music, and more. Twilight skiing will be opening this weekend as well, with $10 tickets for all ages!

The South Ridge Food Court is now open, along with the Barker Food Court, Waffle Cabin in Barker Basin, Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel, Camp at the Grand Summit Hotel, Shipyard Brew Haus at White Cap Lodge, and Foggy Goggle at South Ridge Lodge. Plus, get your retail therapy in at Sunday River Sports, and your relaxation on at the Jordan Spa. Please note that White Cap Lodge will not have shopping or guest service offerings this weekend, but will have a ticket pick-up box and ski-in/ski-out access via the White Cap Quad. 

For the latest updates whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

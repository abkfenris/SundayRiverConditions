December 31, 2021

We are skiing into 2022 with over 400 acres of open terrain! Celebrate on the slopes with 75 open trails, the most in the East. Start the day slopeside with fresh stripes on more than 60 trails. The New Year is almost here, and we have lifts spinning from 8:00AM-8:00PM with festivities galore for you to explore. Christmas Vacation Week activities are in full swing today going into tonight, so check out events including fireworks, live music, fire dancing and more! Twilight skiing is until 8:00PM tonight, and our new activity Après Aglow goes until 8:30PM, featuring thousands of twinkling lights on a trail through the forest outside of the Grand Summit Hotel. Come carve fresh corduroy across 8 peaks!

Both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out for your convenience. To ski into the Grand Summit Hotel via green terrain, take Little White Cap Quad and ski down Moonstruck via Green Cheese. To access the Jordan Hotel via green terrain, please ride Jordan Bowl Express, to ski Lollapalooza. At this time, all other green trails into the Jordan Hotel are temporarily considered advanced terrain.

Around the Resort

Select services, such as lessons and rental equipment, may be limited or sold out from now through New Year's Day. Online reservations made in advance are recommended to guarantee availability.

Many restaurants and food trucks are open for the season, including Coffee Hound in South Ridge Lodge Food Court, the Ona-Tripp Food Truck, and more. For shopping and relaxation needs, head over to recently opened Aster & Birch located within the Jordan Hotel, as well as Sunday River Sports, Betty's Treats and Treasures, or unwind at the Jordan Spa. Please note: Sliders will be closed this week with a planned reopening on January 7, but Northern Lights Cafe at the Jordan Hotel is open until 8PM daily. 

For the latest updates whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

December 22, 2021

Fresh snow and fresh groomers. Mother Nature and our snowmaking team are putting in the work to make sure we all have a white Christmas this year. With 1" on the ground already and more to come, today is going to be nothing but fun on the slopes. The resort now has 50 trails open and over 300 acres to explore.. From 9AM-3:45PM today our lifts will spinning, and after the you get off the slopes make sure to check out the magical Après Aglow.  As the snow continues throughout the day we will be updating the Mountain Report so everyone can stay up to date with current conditions. Now go have some fun out there!

    Since Jordan Bowl is open, both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out access for your convenience. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. With this in mind, skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently accessible via blue terrain. Rental snowboards are now back in stock. Come enjoy all the festivities slopeside!

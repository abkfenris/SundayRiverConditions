Morning Report
Last Updated: January 15, 2024 | 6:17 AM

Good moring everyone, hope you're all able to come out and take advantage of the long weekend, because conditions are excellent. We had a brief but powerful snow squall come through yesterday evening around 4:30 that gave us a couple extra inches of light fluffy snow, and while it won't make for a powder day, you might be able to find some little pockets to play in across the resort. For the trails that receive a later groom, that new snow got processed into the cord and will be a sweet surface for you to make your first turns of the day.

Besides that, it's business as usual. The natural terrain that was open yesterday will remain open, with the chance of a few rope drops throughout the day. Everything that normally gets groomed was tackled last night, and snowmaking continues to cover the mountain in a nice white blanket. We will also have Merrill Hill today at 9AM with skiing off Double Refraction.

There will not be twilight skiing tonight. Our next evening for twilight skiing will be next Friday, January 19. Looking for a fun activity for next weekend? The Alpeniglu at Sunday River has a new après experience, as well; on Saturdays, take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some drinks, sweets, and music, then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now. 

Think Cold!

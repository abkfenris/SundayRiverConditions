Afternoon Report

Today we had a mix of cloudy and sunny skies, temperatures in the high teens to the low twenties, and 630 acres of skiable terrain. The snow last night combined with the efforts of our snowmakers allowed us to open 70 acres of terrain throughout the day, with more coming throughout the week.

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: January 2, 2024 | 4:00 PM

What a day to have a day. Temps reached a comfortable level, the skies are clear, and the snow is ripping. Not too many complaints to be had today.

The cold is already descending back on the mountain, and our snowmakers are continuing their efforts. These temps have really boosted productivity, and new terrain is expected to be coming along almost daily. 

Speaking of new terrain, the Jordan Bowl will re-open for the season tomorrow! The Jordan 8 will begin spinning at 9AM, accessing Rogue Angel, Lollapalooza, and Excalibur. Excalibur will not be groomed. Access to the basin will be via Boots On and Boots off from the Parking Lot, or via Northern Lights to Firestar if you're coming from Spruce Peak. Kansas will provide access back to North Peak.

Temps look to be holding cold, and we're marching towards expansion.

Think Cold!

- Team Snow

Next Steps

Following the opening of the Jordan Bowl, the Aurora Quad is planned to re-open Thursday, January 4 for the season.

The Chondola is currently loading cabins only. We are expecting to be able to re-open the chair side of the lift Friday, January 5.

The Alpeniglu is scheduled to re-open on Friday, January 5, as well as the Outpost, for convenient slopeside snacks and beverages.

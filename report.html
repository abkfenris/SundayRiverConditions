SATURDAY, APRIL 3

Happy Saturday! With overnight snowmaking, sunshine, and now some softening snow, it's a special spring day on the mountain. Keep it up out there and enjoy the blue skies and making your way from peak to peak. Celebrate the weekend at 8PM tonight with fireworks at South Ridge and then rest those legs for another fun day out there tomorrow. 

Lifts are scheduled to open at 8AM tomorrow. Our groomers will head out tonight to press stripes on 64 trails, smoothing things out for tomorrow's turns. Our snowmakers our taking a well deserved break and will not be firing up the snow guns tonight. To test out the first groom on the manmade snow, head to Right Stuff and Sunday Punch. With some awesome weather for this weekend, you'll want to make the last minute decision to stay into tomorrow, with one-night stays now available at the Grand Summit and Jordan Hotels through the rest of the season. We have limited skier services at the White Cap base lodge, so head to South Ridge or Barker to start your day.

With operations open daily through April 24, there is plenty of ski season ahead. View the entire Spring Schedule to learn more about peak access and resort services this spring. Our dining options will be changing weekly as we approach April 24, so don't forget to check in so that you can plan ahead. 

Don't miss out on our on-mountain savings at our retail shops around the resort, including up to 40% off at our Sunday River Sports Flagship shop at South Ridge and 30% off at the Jordan Spa Boutique.

Happy Passholder Appreciation Month! For the month of April, we're celebrating all our passholders. Join us for spring savings, a scavenger hunt, fireworks, and a passholder picnic. With options that fit your budget and schedule, lock in the lowest rates on next year's pass now through April 30.

Good morning skiers and riders! We're kicking off our first weekend of operation with expanded terrain. Barker Mountain Express will be spinning from 8 AM until 4 PM with skiing off of Right Stuff, Jungle Road, and Ecstasy. While the snow has made for some amazing skiing, early season conditions do exist and snowmaking will be happening throughout the day on Lower Sunday Punch and Right Stuff. T2, Upper Sunday Punch, and Ecstasy will not have active snowmaking today.

The Locke Mountain Triple is also spinning today but there will be no midstation loading.

Our team continues to work exceptionally hard to take advantage of the prolonged cold and open additional acreage as quickly as possible.

February 26, 2022

A bluebird afternoon on 8” new snow, plus snowmaking! We have added to our growing list of open terrain, including Goat Path, Shock Wave, and Lost Princess. Piles of freshly made powder span across trails including Lower Sunday Punch and Risky Business for all-natural turns this afternoon. Snowguns are starting on Cascades, Ecstasy, and Obsession today, and will continue into tomorrow. Tonight, groomers are putting fresh stripes on more than 80 trails including Risky Business, so you can carve in at 8AM first chair. Enjoy taking as may runs as possible, with day lifts spinning until 4PM, then head over to North Peak for Twilight Skiing under the stars until 6:30PM. Powder stashes awaits your skis this weekend!

Sunday River Rental Shop will be open to walk-ins with limited availability as the day progresses. To have the best selection, please reserve all equipment at least 48 hours in advance.  

Around the Resort

Presidents' Week is here. All week long is packed with activities, including Twilight Skiing, fireworks, live music, fire dancing, the Après Aglow lighted walk, and more. Get tickets for family and friends so you can enjoy a fun vacation on the slopes.

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, featuring delicious sweets and special souvenirs from our exclusive logowear collection. Check out our many retail options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge.

Take a relaxing stroll at our serene Après Aglow lighted walk. This half-mile winter wonderland by the slopes is adorned with thousands of beautiful twinkling lights, conveniently located behind the Grand Summit Hotel. Sip on hot cocoa as you take in the view with family and friends.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Unwind and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. Our spa is paired with a boutique, where you can find beauty and wellness products, athletic wear, and more.

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

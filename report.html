January 21, 2022

We had a beautiful morning skiing 111 open trails! Blue skies light up this afternoon, and Twilight Skiing illuminates the slopes until 6:30PM. Start tomorrow at first chair, with lifts spinning from 8AM-6:30PM. Groomers are putting down fresh corduroy tonight across 8 peaks for you to enjoy. Snowmakers are blasting guns on Lazy River and 3D in the morning, and continuing all weekend to make piles and piles of snow. We have exciting events including a big air lightshow, live music, a ski film for a great cause, and more this weekend. You have ample choices to carve on the most open terrain in the East!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl.

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: March 18, 2024 | 1:16 PM

It's been another magnificent Monday out on the mountain. Don't be fooled, there's still a ton of great skiing left to be had this season. 

The grooming team will be taking to the hill tonight and will continue to work their magic across all 8 peaks. Please note, Lazy River below Three Mile will be closed tomorrow for an on-hill project. Access to Spruce from the top of the Barker 6 will be via Three Mile.

We're also following a few more snowmaking windows later this week and into next. The team is planning on taking advantage of every chance they get, so be sure to thank a snowmaker the next time you see them on the hill.

Check out our Solar Eclipse Viewing Party on April 8th and watch the eclipse from the top of North Peak. See our events calendar for a full list of spring events across the mountain.

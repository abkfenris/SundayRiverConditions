TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 23

Mother Nature worked throughout the night to set us up for a fun day today with 2-3" of fresh snow. Conditions have been phenomenal and this little refresh will be the icing on the cake. Groomers were also hard at work overnight on their normal routes, doing what they do best to perfect the slopes. Head out for first chair at 9AM today for some midweek bliss and enjoy smooth carving and making your mark on the new snow.

We require online lift ticket purchase and set limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines.

See you out there!

Morning Update

The shortest days of the season are behind us, and the holidays are coming. Today's forecast brings us mostly cloudy skies and temps sticking around the high 20s at the base. By the end of the day, we may start to see some snowfall, and the winds should stay reasonably calm.

Our Mountain Ops team went out to do their thing last night, grooming all open terrain possible and blowing snow around Aurora and Jordan Bowl in preparation for the weekend and the Jordan 8 opening day. The North Peak Lodge opened recently, and more dining options on the mountain are beginning to open their doors for the season. If you are making your way here for the holidays, be sure to check out everything we have going on all around the resort during this Christmas week.

See you out there!
-Team Snow

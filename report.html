Morning Report
Last Updated: April 10, 2024 | 7:30 AM

Good morning, and happy Wednesday. The sun is just beginning to peek through the clouds, and our mountain is looking as beautiful as ever.  Trail groomers worked through the night to clean up and smooth out all those skied-up trails from days prior. Thanks to their efforts, trail conditions will be excellent early in the day, gradually softening as the day progresses and clouds start to roll in.

Please be cautious while skiing into the South Ridge basin. Active construction is going on in preparation for POND-A-PALOOZA. Please ski towards our Surface Lifts and around the South Ridge Express to access the Chondola entrance.

Spring operations schedule:

Midweek operations will only consist of Jordan, Barker, the Chondola, and Spruce, which means we no longer have true beginner terrain available. Our full Spring operations schedule can be found here.

Ski-in access to the Grand Summit Hotel is still in play but keep an eye here for updates as the snowpack melts.

Looking ahead, we've got a weekend full of classic Spring events. Pond-a-palooza will be taking place on Sunday, with the Great Tailgate and Passholder Appreciation lunch on Sunday! 

We'll see you out there!

- Team Snow

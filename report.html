Last Updated: September 11, 2023

The clock is ticking down to the Maine Brew Fest! Join us this weekend, rain or shine, for the kickoff event on September 16th and 17th, and choose from over 100 beers and ciders from over 30 Maine breweries. It's the perfect way to kickstart River Fest, a full weekend series packed full of fall festivities. This includes beer, food, music, and a variety of activities that you can enjoy with your family and friends throughout September and October. Experience the Maine Brew Fest, Mahoosuc Adventure Fest, Maine Artisan Fest,  and Fall Fest, all set amidst a breathtaking fall backdrop. For more details on River Fest and how you can enjoy the Maine Beer Box (and its 78 taps), an antique car cruise-in, a kaleidoscope-making class, an arts & crafts fair, and more, please visit our website.

As we transition into fall, scenic lift rides will now run on Saturdays and Sundays, from 10AM - 4PM, with 3:30PM being the last time to purchase a ticket for the day. 

Lastly, don't forget to share your summer joy with us on Happy Place Found – we love seeing your adventures!

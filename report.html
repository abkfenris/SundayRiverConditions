Morning Report
Last Updated: December 10, 2023 | 6:44 AM

Happy Sunday, folks. Today we have over 250 acres of skiable terrain and 9 lifts spinning. We have some light fog this morning on the hill, but don't let that stop you from getting out there. With temps in the mid-thirties this morning, turns will be soft and lovely.

Our grooming team laid some corduroy down for us last night on the majority of our open trails. If you're looking for an extra challenge, head to Aludra, Boralis, and Airglow for some ungroomed terrain. We have a new pop-up park at the end of Easy Street today, as well, so keep an eye out for that. Please be aware that there is currently no beginner terrain off of the Chondola. 

Don't forget to join us for Santa Sunday today! Spectators should head to South Ridge by 11AM. Pre-registered Santas should check in between 7:30-10:30AM this morning at the South Ridge Base Lodge. For all event details, head to our Santa Sunday page in the River Guide. Please note that the South Ridge Express will be closed from around 10:50AM until about 11:30AM as we get our Santas up the mountain, but it will reopen shortly after.  

Additionally, the AlpenIglu at Sunday River will not be open today. Full hours are coming soon.

Santa Sunday means Christmas is coming soon, so take a look at our full event lineup. In addition to the most dependable snow in New England, we have fun for the whole family and great deals on lodging and lift tickets.

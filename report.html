February 25, 2022

We are making snow alongside Mother Nature! Flurries are currently flying on the mountain as forecasts call for 6-10” of snowfall totals into tonight. We already have fresh snow falling across 8 peaks before first chair at 8AM. Snowguns blasted all night and are continuing into this morning, on trails including Risky Business and Dream Maker. Today, snowmakers are adding trails to their list, including Right Stuff and Lower Sunday Punch. Shred all day, then head to South Ridge for Twilight Skiing until 6:30PM tonight, to take as many powdery turns as possible. Groomers put corduroy on 80 trails to make a beautiful base for all this new snow. After skiing in a winder wonderland all today, hit the slopes for a bluebird tomorrow as forecasts call for sunshine after snow. A powder weekend is the best way to top off your vacation!

Please note the Chondola will be on mechanical hold for the day.

Around the Resort

Presidents' Week is here. All week long is packed with activities, including live music, fireworks, Twilight Skiing, fire dancing, the Après Aglow lighted walk, and more. Get tickets for family and friends so you can look forward to a fun vacation on the slopes.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Relax, unwind, and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. You can also treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures with delicious candy, or shop our exclusive logowear collection.

Looking for retail therapy or perhaps forgot some gear on your packing list? Check out our many shopping options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge. Love the boutique retail feel? Enjoy shopping at Aster & Birch at the Jordan Hotel.

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

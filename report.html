Mountain Report
Last Updated: September 29, 2023

Artisan Fest is here! Come join us for a weekend filled with food, art, and fun. Watch skilled artists demonstrate pottery and blacksmithing skills, or take a kaleidoscope-making class that you can only find here at Sunday River. 

Don't miss out on the tasting tent where you can sample wines, spirits, and cheeses from local Maine businesses. And if you love trying different kinds of beer, we've got a Maine Beer Box with 78 taps of Maine brews! While you're here, check out the antique car cruise-in on both Saturday and Sunday. Visit the Artisan Fest page  for a full list of events and activities for the weekend! 

As we transition into fall, scenic lift rides will now run on Saturdays and Sundays, from 10AM - 4PM, with 3:30PM being the last time to purchase a ticket for the day. 

Please note, the EV charging stations in South Ridge are not available at this time as work towards adding more to Lot 3. We apologize for any inconvience this may cause. 

Lastly, don't forget to share your fall joy with us on Happy Place Found – we love seeing your adventures!

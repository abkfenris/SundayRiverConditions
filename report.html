Happy Wednesday, folks! The sun is shining this morning, warming up the snow for perfect corduroy turns. Groomers were out all night laying down those stripes you all love, including on fan-favorite trails American Express, Risky Business, Lazy River, and more. Come shake off the midweek blues on the slopes. Check out the best ways to get in your runs with our weekly updates on the Spring Schedule. Look forward to more slopeside celebrations arriving this weekend, including The Great Tailgate. Even still, our Mountain Ops team are no amateurs when it comes to recovering from weather events - so don't worry, we'll be doing everything we can to keep the slopes skiing beautifully for all of you.

All terrain is intermediate and advanced, including ski-in/ski-out access to the Grand Summit Hotel. Access to White Cap Basin via Road Runner is closed. We look forward to another great season skiing across all 8 peaks!

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

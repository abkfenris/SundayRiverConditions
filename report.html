Morning Report
Last Updated: March 15, 2024 | 9:04 AM

9AM Update: Our Mountain Ops team is reporting about 4" of snow at the top of White Cap. 

Big flakes are currently falling from the sky, and our grooming team reports about 1" of accumulation at the peaks. It might not be a major storm, but it's certainly picturesque. It's currently in the low to mid-thirties across the mountain, and it shouldn't warm up too much. The snow should end around noon, and the cloudy skies are here to stay for the rest of the day.

January 17, 2022

As the storm heads out, leaving behind nearly a foot of fresh snow, we're appreciating our legs today. The endless refills kept us going all day long, especially as ropes dropped across the resort, granting us access to a dozen new trails, including Last Tango, Wizard's Gultch, and Enchanted Forest. Better rest up because tomorrow we get to enjoy all of those stashes under blue skies. 

Lifts open at 9AM tomorrow with the most open terrain the in East on 100 trails. Groomers are heading out in full force to press out perfect corduroy. The forecast is calling for some wind, but it's too early to tell if it will impact lift status. Check back in the morning for more details. 

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl. 

Around the Resort

We've got some fun events coming up! Wicked Air & Après is returning to Barker next Saturday, January 22. This must-see event combines big air and 3D project lights into one show. 

Senior Week is January 24-28 - lots of deals for the over-65 club, and plenty of fun events and ski meet-ups to join.

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: February 5, 2024 | 12:20 PM

It has been another absolute stunner of a day with daylight still left to get out there and enjoy it. 

Tonight, snowmaking will resume across the eight peaks. Trails with active snowmaking this evening, like Airglow or Broadway, will not be groomed for the morning, which will be noted in tomorrow's report. 

Looking ahead, White Heat will be closed tomorrow while the grooming team works on pushing out the whalebacks and putting on fresh stripes.

Presidents' Week will be here before we know it, and we have events planned for the whole family. There will be fireworks, a fire dancer, twilight skiing, Eddy and Betty Dinners, and more. We also have a few spots left for our Après Experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River during President's Week. Take a PistenBully Cabin Cat down from Sliders, enjoy some sips, sweets, and music, and then take the Cabin Cat back up. Tickets are on sale now.

Morning Report
Last Updated: December 5, 2023 | 7:44 AM

The mountain sure looks good covered in white. From White Cap to Jordan, over a foot of new snow is on the ground, and it has truly made for a winter wonderland. This fresh snow will make for incredible conditions today, so let's get into it.

We'll be opening with 27 trails covering 203 acres, with the chance of more trails opening throughout the day pending approval from patrol. The grooming team took to the hill last night, converting yesterday's powder into pristine corduroy for today's first chair. Our snowmakers were out in force taking advantage of the cold temps overnight to add to Mother Nature's work. Forecasts look cold over the next few days, which means a long sustained window for our snowmakers to take advantage of. All eyes are set on expansion.

This coming Saturday is the grand opening of the Barker 6. Celebrations start at 8:30 AM, and all of the details can be found on the Barker 6 Grand Opening page.

Don’t forget to take a look at our Christmas Vacation Week plans. We’ve got a whole host of events, great deals on lodging and lift tickets, and of course, the most dependable snow in New England. Keep an eye out for gift ideas at the Sunday River Sports flagship store, as well.

January 12, 2022

Well the weather is starting to warm up on this Wednesday and we are all for it. Make sure to enjoy your coffee this morning and take your time to layer up as we see temperatures starting to rise around 11AM. With the sun scattered between the clouds, it is a great day to shred our newly snow blown trails including Sunday Punch, Risky Business, and Whoville. All of our base lodges are open and accessible via freshly groomed terrain. If you're in the mood for a little extra workout, grab an uphill pass and explore all of our peaks.

The White Heat Quad and the Aurora Quad will not spin, but the terrain they service is open for uphill traffic with a valid lift ticket or season pass. Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails. 

Around the Resort

It's Kids' Week here at Sunday River, with deals on lodging, lift tickets, and lessons, plus fun events like Apres Aglow, fireworks, a fire dancer, and a magic show. Then, we're gearing up for a super fun Martin Luther King, Jr. Weekend on the mountain, with tap takeovers, fireworks, twilight skiing, and more. 

Looking ahead, Wicked Air & Après is returning to Barker on January 22. This must-see event combines big air and 3D project lights into one show. 

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: December 22, 2023 | 2:35 PM

Before we dive into plans for the weekend, we want to extend another heartfelt thank you to all the dedicated crews who have worked tirelessly the past four days. The amount of support we have received has been truly overwhelming, and we cannot wait to welcome you back to the mountain. 

Tomorrow, lifts will open at 8AM with advanced terrain only. Lift access will be out of the Barker and White Cap Basins via the Barker 6, Locke Triple, Spruce Triple, and White Cap Quad. We are expecting to have 19 trails and over 110 acres open, pending final trail checks from Patrol in the morning. 

Access on December 23 and 24 will be limited to season passholders, ticket pack holders, Ikon passholders, and guests with pre-purchased lift tickets. For those staying directly at the Grand Summit, Jordan, or Snow Cap Inn through the resort, Stay & Save lift tickets will be available for purchase during your stay.   

Barker Lodge will be open for all services, including dining out of the cafeteria and the Barker Bar. South Ridge will not have ski access, but the Sunday River Sports flagship store will be open for last-minute Christmas shopping. Dining will be available at the South Ridge Café and the Foggy Goggle, and the Tune Shop and Rental Shop will also be open.  

Please keep in mind, while some local restaurants and shops are reopening and eager for business, others are still feeling the impact of Monday’s storm. Please plan ahead, check hours in advance, and practice kindness and compassion this holiday season.  

Thank you once again for your patience, support, and positive energy. Sunday River is not just a mountain, it's a community, and we're grateful to share this winter paradise with each and every one of you. 

We’ll see you tomorrow! 

- Team Snow

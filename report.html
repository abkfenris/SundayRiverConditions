January 17, 2022

Flurries are flying across 8 peaks this morning! The most terrain in the East is having a powder day, with an expected 8-12" of accumulation. All night and into this morning groomers put down stripes on 78 trails including South Paw, to prepare for this winter wonderland. Mother Nature is working with us today, as the forecast calls for snow from open to close. Come grab first chair at 8AM for a day full of fresh flakes. We have nearly 90 open trails today for you to shred!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl. 

Around the Resort

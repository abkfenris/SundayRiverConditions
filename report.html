Summer Update

We're well into June, and with that, our teams have been hard at work to prep for our slate of summer activities. Sunday River Outfitters has kicked off their season with kayaking, paddleboarding, archery, and new this season, axe throwing! Sunday River Golf Club is up and running along with the Clubhouse for meals, drinks, and any apparel needs in the Pro Shop. Stay and Play Packages are up and running as well. Book a tee time and you're automatically qualified to save up to 15% on lodging. 

Coming up next we have scenic lift rides kicking off on June 30 and you can view our updated summer trail map here. Please take note of the closed areas around the mountain. Even with some closed zones, there's still plenty of hiking around the resort. We've added Trail L in the White Cap area that can turn your journey up to Locke into a nice loop. 

On the events side of things, first up is the New England Forest Rally on July 14-15 and Tough Mountain Challenge and Mini Mountain Challenge July 22 and 23.

It may not be winter, but there's still plenty to do up here! Share your favorite summer moments with us on our Happy Place Found page and soak up that summer sun!

Hiking Trail Conditions

Annie once sang about the sun coming out tomorrow, and maybe she was right, but for the month of June, we've been left singing in the rain. Trails are predictably wet, but still hikeable. If you're participating in TMC in July, then you're in luck! Because you will be encountering mud, water, and some slippery slabs on many trails and this can serve as a great training opportunity.

Hiking trails currently open: I, J, D, E, A, H, K, B, C, M, O, and L

Hiking trails currently closed: G

As you go out on your hike, please make sure you're prepared by knowing your route, having water/snacks, and wearing appropriate footwear. Additionally, closed zones remain in effect across the resort. Please obey all signs and keep an eye out for construction vehicles driving up the mountain roads.

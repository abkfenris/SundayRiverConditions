Afternoon Report

Boy oh boy is it an amazing day. The sun is shining, the snow is soft, and thanks to this last snowstorm, we were able to open over 150 acres of mostly natural terrain today. Be sure to check out our events calendar for a full list of events across the mountain. Coming up this Saturday, we have our first DJ for the Year of the Après DJ Series. Come on down to South Ridge from 12-4PM this Saturday for the Slopeside Mountain Mixer, with DJ DeeRock and Jerry Kay will be jamming away. With food and hand-crafted cocktails, Saturday should be a party. Coming up, check out events like Bust 'N' Burn and Spring Fest Weekend to find events for everyone in the family.

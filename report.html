April 24, 2022

Welcome to the last day of the 2021-22 winter ski season. It's been an incredible season, and there's no better way to say good-bye than a perfect spring day, dabbled with sunshine and scenes from the Barker Bar deck. Be sure to pick up your $0 ticket before hitting the slopes.

All skier services, including ticket purchases, dining options, and ski shop essentials, are at Barker base lodge. Guest Services is open in the Welcome Center at South Ridge, if you need assistance. Please note uphill access is prohibited on all trails marked as closed.

Only a few days remain to lock in $0 down at the lowest rates on next year's New England Pass. If you are an existing passholder, this is your last chance to take advantage of the renewal discount before it ends on April 30. 

Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23.

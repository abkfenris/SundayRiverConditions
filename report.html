January 25, 2022

A great day is made of fresh snow, blue skies, and corduroy terrain! We had 2" of flurries fall across 8 peaks to start today. Carve into 79 groomed trails this afternoon with last chair at 3:45PM, or enjoy fresh stripes tomorrow morning at 9AM. We have over 100 open trails for you to choose from with more available as we ski through the week. The forecast this weekend calls for piles of snow, so look forward to even more options on the most open terrain in the East!

Please note that ski-in access to the Jordan Hotel requires the navigation of intermediate trails to reach Jordan Bowl. 

Around the Resort

Senior Week is here, from January 24 to 28! Look forward to lots of deals for the over-65 club, and plenty of fun events and ski meet-ups to join. 

The Après Aglow lighted walk is open this week, so come enjoy thousands of twinkling lights in a winter wonderland, while sipping on hot cocoa!

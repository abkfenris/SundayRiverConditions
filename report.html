Around the Resort

Craft Beer Weekend is here! This exciting event kicks off the launch of the Western Maine Brew Passport, a collaboration between Sunday River and breweries across Western Maine. Take this opportunity to try out plenty of different pints here, and pick up your passport to start collecting stamps!

The first Spring Après Concert Series of the season arrives tomorrow! Come enjoy free live music after a full day of skiing, featuring great bands. The stage is conveniently located so you and your group can grab refreshments after a great day on the slopes.

One-night stays are now available at the Snow Cap Inn. Join us for convenient, budget-friendly lodging options.

Join us at Jordan Bowl this weekend, March 26-27 for the Jordan Express Lift's final days before Jordan 8 Lift construction begins! We'll have music blasting and opportunities to win a prize or two for skiers that scan their tickets/passes at the Jordan RFID gate.

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, featuring delicious sweets and special souvenirs from our exclusive logowear collection. Check out our many retail options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge.

The Jordan Spa has your back after a full day on the slopes. Unwind and enjoy a massage, facial, and more rejuvenating treatments. Our spa is paired with a boutique, where you can find beauty and wellness products, athletic wear, and more. You can find even more retail options across the resort!

For the latest updates on available shopping, dining, and activities whenever you visit us, head to the River Guide.

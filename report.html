December 24, 2021

The sun is shining all day on 57 open trails and over 350 acres for you to enjoy. We are decking the slopes with piles of snow tonight, as snowmakers blast guns to add more terrain. Today White Heat Quad opened, followed by North Peak Quad tomorrow. Santa is coming to the mountain with almost 60 groomed trails for you to carve in the morning. If you prefer celebrating your holidays under the stars, we have Twilight Skiing and the Après Aglow Walk both opening for the week this Sunday, and even more festivities.

Since Jordan Bowl is open, both hotels are now ski-in/ski-out access for your convenience. Skiing into the Grand Summit Hotel is currently only accessible via blue terrain. Please note our only beginner terrain is available within the South Ridge Express Quad at this time. Come ski in a corduroy wonderland!

Morning Report

Good morning and welcome to your Monday on the mountain. It's been an absolutely beautiful stretch of weather with incredible skiing, and we've been enjoying every second of it. The grooming team had a successful night on the hill, resurfacing their standard slate of trails with fresh stripes for your enjoyment. It's shaping up to be another excellent day on the mountain. 

While it has been an amazing week of sunshine and soft snow, winter is far from over here in Western Maine. Snow showers are forecasted to move in throughout the evening today, with heavy snow expected to roll in sometime tomorrow morning. 4-8" is currently expected, but models have had a very hard time with this storm, and we are right on the line of getting up to a foot, so consider our fingers crossed.

Afternoon Report
Last Updated: January 3, 2024 | 1:48 PM

With 255 acres, 9 lifts, and 37 trails, Sunday River had the most open terrain in New England today, and we're going to keep expanding. 

Today we had temps in the low to mid-thirties, and we're looking at a similar forecast tomorrow. It will cool down enough to crank those snowguns, so our snowmaking team will be back out there tonight. We can expect a similar lineup of trails from our grooming team tomorrow, with the likely addition of Excalibur. In addition to the Jordan 8 today, we'll be spinning the Aurora Quad tomorrow. Keep an eye out for additional terrain in the coming days.  

This weekend, check out Indie Demo Day, where you'll have the chance to demo some of the top independent ski and snowboard brands. Swing by the White Cap Base Lodge between 8AM and 12PM to register for the event. The full list of indie brands is coming soon. 

Temps are cold and holding, and we're marching towards expansion.

April 3, 2022

A beautiful sunrise lights up the peaks this morning. Groomers added a great corduroy base to more than 60 trails for you to ski at 8AM first chair. Temperatures are forecasted to warm into this afternoon so you can take soft turns on nearly 400 acres of open terrain. We are looking forward to more spring skiing today, followed by a chance of light refreshing flurries overnight. Looking ahead, the new week brings more warm afternoons with cooler nights to keep those snow stashes on the slopes. Enjoy live music, fantastic food, and great brews with stunning views to wrap up your Spring Fest Weekend!

Please note access to other peaks from the Grand Summit Hotel requires navigation of intermediate and advanced terrain. Construction for the Jordan 8 has begun! For the remainder of the 2021-22 season, please take the Shuttle to access other peaks from the Jordan Hotel. All uphill access to Jordan Bowl trails is closed. Look forward to the Jordan 8 coming to you winter 2022-23!

Around the Resort

Treat yourself at Betty's Treats & Treasures, featuring delicious sweets and special souvenirs from our exclusive logowear collection. Check out our many retail options across the mountain, including Sunday River Sports, conveniently located at South Ridge Lodge.

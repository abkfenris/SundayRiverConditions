Morning Report
Last Updated: January 5, 2024 | 7:41 AM

It's a chilly one today, folks. It's currently 1 degree F at the top of Jordan and 11 degrees F in the Barker Basin, so be sure to bundle up. Today we'll be skiing and riding on 261 acres over 40 trails and 10 lifts, with more trails potentially coming online throughout the day. We'll be loading chairs on the Chondola today in addition to the gondola cabins. 

You know who else loves the cold weather? Our snowmakers. Our snowguns were blasting across the mountain last night, and they'll stay online throughout the day today as well. Please be aware that we have active snowmaking on Broadway, Sundance, Lazy River, Risky Business, Right Stuff, and Whoville. In addition to those trails, Ecstasy, Downdraft, and Upper Airglow will be ungroomed. The remaining open terrain all has fresh stripes, thanks to our grooming team.

Tomorrow, check out Indie Demo Day, where you'll have the chance to demo four of the top independent ski and snowboard brands: Parlor, Never Summer, Amalgam Skis, and Winterstick. Swing by the White Cap Base Lodge between 8AM and 12PM to register for the event, and demo equipment from 8AM until 2PM. 

Temps are cold and holding, and we'll keep expanding.

Think Cold!

- Team Snow

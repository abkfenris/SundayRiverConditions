Afternoon Report
Last Updated: March 1, 2024 | 1:33 PM

Temps might be low, but the sun is out, and the skies are blue. The turns are fast out there, and the wind died down significantly from yesterday. What a way to head into the weekend.

Our snowmaking team will continue to resurface trails across the mountain, including American Express and Kansas. Our grooming team will be adding some more terrain back into the mix, including Cowardly Lion, Merrill Hill, and a few of the trails that had snowmaking last night. Tomorrow, we're looking at over 80 groomed trails for skiing and riding across all 8 peaks.

Tonight, ski under the lights for some twilight skiing, or head into the Foggy Goggle to listen to James Rice from 3-6PM. Tomorrow, we have more live music at the Foggy Goggle and the Barker Bar, twilight skiing at South Ridge, a Demo Day at White Cap Basin, and Sips & Sweets at the Jordan Spa. The après experience at the Alpeniglu at Sunday River has a few spots left for tomorrow night so grab a ticket while you still can. On Sunday, join Sunday River Christian Ministry for a mountaintop service on North Peak. 

Do you know the Skier Responsibility Code? It's never a bad time to review it. Take a moment, refresh yourself before heading out, and please remember to go slow in the base areas.

We'll see you out there!

- Team Snow

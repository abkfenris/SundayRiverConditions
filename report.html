SUNDAY, FEBRUARY 14

We have another beautiful day on tap with eight pretty peaks, mild temps, and drool-worthy conditions. To top things off, it looks like we have some snow heading our way tomorrow night. This Valentine’s Day take your significant other to the slopes and show them your first true love, shredding at Sunday River. If you’re single, well you don’t really get to mingle, please keep a safe distance while at the resort. Masks are the latest fashion trend, so don’t forget to keep yours up at all times unless actively skiing or sitting down to eat.

Last night the groomers worked their way across each peak laying down perfect stripes for today. Enjoy fun morning turns on trails like Lazy River, Risky Business, Escapade, Northern Lights, and Lollapalooza. Hop into the woods over on Jordan Bowl to search for secret stashes. If you’re just learning, start the day at South Ridge for the best beginner terrain. If you’re staying at the Grand Summit, there are also great green trails off of Little White Cap Quad for some mellow turns.

We have quite the line-up for Presidents’ Week with twilight skiing every night, fireworks, and Valentine’s Day specials. Plus, let’s not forget the best part of all, that we are 100% open with fantastic conditions. As you plan your trip please keep in mind lift tickets are limited and days are close to selling out, so plan ahead if you want to hit the slopes this week. Or book lodging with us and guarantee access to full-day lift tickets. Please note that kids ski school lessons are now available to book online with limited availability.

One-night stays are now available through Thursday, February 18, for the Jordan Hotel and Snow Cap Inn. 

We are requiring online lift ticket purchase and setting limits on the number of tickets sold for each day. This includes full-day, half-day, beginner, Threedom additional days, and Passholder Friend tickets. We only expect these limits to affect peak dates, but encourage everyone to buy early to ensure access on the dates you want. Season passes, Ikon passes, and lift ticket packs remain direct-to-lift with no restrictions or reservations.

Fast and contactless lift ticket pickup is available at our PUBs (Pick Up Boxes) at base lodges and hotels. Just scan the barcode at the bottom of your confirmation email and your RFID cards will print with your lift tickets or ticket packs loaded. For best results turn off dark mode on your phone if you use it and avoid email apps that use dark backgrounds.

Please remember to take a self-administered Health Screening and visit our COVID Winter Guide before each trip to stay up-to-date on safety protocols and state guidelines.

Summer Update

August is here and it's time for some summer fun!

Make us your basecamp for adventure with golf, world-class dining, scenic lift rides, axe throwing, and  hiking trails. Looking for more excitement? Check out Sunday River Outfitters for archery lessons, and paddle boarding and kayaking tours. There's something for everyone to experience summer in the heart of the Mahoosuc Mountains.

Looking ahead to fall, mark your calendar for River Fest – a jam-packed weekend series of events! Experience the Maine Brew Fest, Maine Artisan Fest, Mahoosuc Adventure Fest, and Fall Fest—all surrounded by the stunning colors of autumn.

Seeking a challenge? Sign up for next year's Tough Mountain Challenge and conquer rugged terrain with your friends and family. Tickets are on sale now! 

Don't forget to share your summer joy with us on Happy Place Found – we love seeing your adventures.

The mountains are calling, so gear up and bring your crew. We can't wait to see you out there!

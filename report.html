Mid-May Update

Exciting things are happening up at the resort. The team is full steam ahead on the Merrill Hill project, working towards installing a new lift, trails, and snowmaking for the Sunday River Real Estate-exclusive development. During this time, the mountain is closed to all pedestrian traffic Monday through Friday for the foreseeable future. Please consider other places to explore, as the area is filled will great hikes and walks. 

The Sunday River Golf Club is open for the season and the course is in fantastic shape. Tee times are available to book online and the earlier you book, the more you'll save. The Pro Shop is freshly stocked with all new gear and open daily 8AM to 5PM. The Clubhouse is open for limited food and beverages Friday through Sunday, 10AM to 4PM and is the only dining option on resort for the month of May.

There is plenty of fun to look forward to this summer. Local mountain biking and hiking trails are already open. The Bethel Farmers' Market happens every Saturday. Scenic lift rides on the Chondola open up July 3. And Tough Mountain Challenge is on for 2021. Participants that rolled over their registration from last year are already on the books. We'll open up registration to everyone else starting May 21.

Please note the Jordan Hotel is closed through May 27 and the Grand Summit Hotel is scheduled to close starting May 24 through June 24.
